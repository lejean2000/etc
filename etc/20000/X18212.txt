<h3 id="X18212">X(18212) = DAO PERSPECTOR OF X(5)</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; (SA^2-3*S^2)*(SB+SC)*(R^2*SB+2*SA*SC)*(R^2*SC+2*SA*SB) : :

<p>
In the plane of a triangle ABC, let P be a point.  Let<br>
(BCP) = cirumcircle of BCP, and define (CAP) and (ABP) cyclically;<br>
A' = the point of interection, other than P, or AP and (BCP), and define B' and C' cyclically;<br>
A'' = reflection of A' in the center of (BCP), and define B'' and C'' cyclically.<br>
Then A''B''C'' is perspective to ABC.  (Thanh Oai Dao, April 29, 2018)
</p>
<p>
X(18212) and X(18213) were contributed by César Lozada, April 30, 2018, with the following notes.  The perspector of ABC and A''B''C'' is here named the <b>Dao perspector of P</b>, denoted by D(P).  The appearance of (i,j) in the following list means that D(X(i)) = X(j):  (1, 1), (2, 13608), (3, 24), (4, 68), (5,18212), (6, 14262), (13, 18), (14,17), (15, 62), (16, 61), (20,18213), (36, 35), (59, 11247), (80, 79), (249, 10279), (265, 14111).  In general, if P = x : y : z (barycentrics), then
</p>
<p>
D(P) = (-x^2*SA+SB*x*y+SC*x*z+2*a^2*y*z)*(-SC*c^2*x*y+SB*b^2*x*z-2*a^2*c^2*y^2-SA*a^2*y*z)*(SC*c^2*x*y-SB*b^2*x*z-SA*a^2*y*z-2*a^2*b^2*z^2) : :
</p>
