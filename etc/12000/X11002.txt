Let PaPbPc be the reflection triangle of a point P.  Let Q be the isogonal conjugate of P wrt PaPbPc.  When P = X(2) or X(381), Q = X(11002).  (Quang Tuan Bui, Hyacinthos #20331, November 10, 2011)
