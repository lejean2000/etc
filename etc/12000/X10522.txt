<h3 id="X10522">X(10522)&nbsp;=&nbsp;HOMOTHETIC CENTER OF ABC AND CROSS-TRIANGLE OF INNER AND OUTER JOHNSON TRIANGLES</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; a - b - c - a(cos A)(1 - cos A - 2 sin(A/2) cos(B/2 - C/2)) + b(cos B)(1 - cos B - 2 sin(B/2) cos(C/2 - A/2)) + c(cos C)(1 - cos C - 2 sin(C/2) cos(A/2 - B/2)) : :
<p>
Of the two tangents to the B- and C-Johnson circles, let L<sub>A</sub> be the one on the opposite side of BC from A, and let M<sub>A</sub> be the other.
Define L<sub>B</sub>, M<sub>B</sub>, L<sub>C</sub>, M<sub>C</sub> cyclically.  Let A' = L<sub>B</sub>&#8745;L<sub>C</sub>, and define B', C' cyclically.  Let A" = M<sub>B</sub>&#8745;M<sub>C</sub>, and define B", C" cyclically.
Triangles A'B'C' and A"B"C" are here named the <b>inner</b> and <b>outer Johnson triangles</b>, resp.<br>
