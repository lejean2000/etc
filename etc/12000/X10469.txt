<h3 id="X10469">X(10469)&nbsp;=&nbsp;COMPLEMENT OF X(10468)</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; 2a^4(b + c) + a^3(3b^2 + 4bc + 3c^2) + 2a^2(b + c)^3 + a(b + c)^2(b^2 + bc + c^2) + bc(b + c)^3 : :
<p>
Let L<sub>A</sub> be the radical axis of the A-excircle and the excircles radical circle, and define L<sub>B</sub>, L<sub>C</sub> cyclically.
Let A' = L<sub>B</sub>&#8745;L<sub>C</sub>, B' = L<sub>C</sub>&#8745;L<sub>A</sub>, C' = L<sub>A</sub>&#8745;L<sub>B</sub>.  Triangle A'B'C' is perspective to ABC at X(2051), and to the excentral triangle at X(10469).
A'B'C' is the complement of the 5th Conway triangle, and so is homothetic to the 5th Conway triangle at X(2).
</p>
