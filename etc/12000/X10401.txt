<p>
Let A<sub>B</sub>, A<sub>C</sub> be the points where the A-excircle touches lines CA and AB resp., and define B<sub>C</sub>, B<sub>A</sub>, C<sub>A</sub>, C<sub>B</sub> cyclically.  A<sub>B</sub>, A<sub>C</sub>, B<sub>C</sub>, B<sub>A</sub>, C<sub>A</sub>, C<sub>B</sub> lie on the Yiu conic (defined at X(478)).
Let T<sub>A</sub> be the intersection of the tangents to the Yiu conic at B<sub>C</sub> and C<sub>A</sub>, and define T<sub>B</sub>, T<sub>C</sub> cyclically.
Let T<sub>A</sub>' be the intersection of the tangents to the Yiu conic at B<sub>A</sub> and C<sub>B</sub>, and define T<sub>B</sub>', T<sub>C</sub>' cyclically.
Let S<sub>A</sub> = T<sub>B</sub>T<sub>C</sub>&#8745;T<sub>B</sub>'T<sub>C</sub>', S<sub>B</sub> = T<sub>C</sub>T<sub>A</sub>&#8745;T<sub>C</sub>'T<sub>A</sub>', S<sub>C</sub> = T<sub>A</sub>T<sub>B</sub>&#8745;T<sub>A</sub>'T<sub>B</sub>'  (i.e. S<sub>A</sub>S<sub>B</sub>S<sub>C</sub> is the side triangle of T<sub>A</sub>T<sub>B</sub>T<sub>C</sub> and T<sub>A</sub>'T<sub>B</sub>'T<sub>C</sub>').
S<sub>A</sub>S<sub>B</sub>S<sub>C</sub> is perspective to ABC at X(65), and to the intouch triangle at X(10401).
</p>
