<h3 id="X10105">X(10105)&nbsp;=&nbsp; 28th HATZIPOLAKIS-MONTESDEOCA POINT</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; a (a^7 b^2-3 a^5 b^4+3 a^3 b^6-a b^8-2 a^7 b c-6 a^6 b^2 c+3 a^5 b^3 c+13 a^4 b^4 c-8 a^2 b^6 c-a b^7 c+b^8 c+a^7 c^2-6 a^6 b c^2-16 a^5 b^2 c^2+9 a^4 b^3 c^2+15 a^3 b^4 c^2-2 a^2 b^5 c^2-b^7 c^2+3 a^5 b c^3+9 a^4 b^2 c^3+12 a^3 b^3 c^3+10 a^2 b^4 c^3+a b^5 c^3-3 b^6 c^3-3 a^5 c^4+13 a^4 b c^4+15 a^3 b^2 c^4+10 a^2 b^3 c^4+2 a b^4 c^4+3 b^5 c^4-2 a^2 b^2 c^5+a b^3 c^5+3 b^4 c^5+3 a^3 c^6-8 a^2 b c^6-3 b^3 c^6-a b c^7-b^2 c^7-a c^8+b c^8) : :
<p>
Let I be the incenter of a triangle ABC, and<br>
A'B'C' = intouch triangle (the pedal triangle of I)<br>
A''B''C'' = cevian triangle of I<br>
Ab = orthogonal projection of A'' on IB, and define Bc and Ca cyclically<br>
Ac = orthogonal projection of A'' on IC, and define Ba and Cb cyclically<br>
A'b = orthogonal projection of A'' on IB', and define B'c and C'a cyclically<br>
A'c = orthogonal projection of A'' on IC', and define B'a and C'b cyclically<br>
(Nab) = nine-point cricle of A''AbA'b, and define (Nbc) and (Nca) cyclically<br>
(Nac) = nine-point cricle of A''AcA'c, and define (Nba) and (Ncb) cyclically<br>
Ra = radical axis of (Nab) and (Nac) = perpendicular bisector of segment NabNac.
</p>
<p>
The lines Ra, Rb, Rc concur in X(10105).  Also, the parallels to Ra, Rb, Rc through A', B', C, respectively, concur in X(942); and the parallels to Ra, Rb, Rc through A'', B'', C'', respectively, concur in X(500).   (Antreas Hatzipolakis and Angel Montesdeoca, August 8, 2016; see <a href="http://www.hyacinthos.epizy.com/message.php?msg=23972">Hyacinthos 23972</a>)
</p>
