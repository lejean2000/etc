<b>

<p>

HaHbHc = orthic triangle<br>
Ja = incenter of AHbHc, and define Jb and Jc cyclically<br>
Ab = JbJc&#8745;CA, and define Bc and Ca cyclically<br>
Ac = JcJa&#8745;BA, and define Ba and Cb cyclically<br>
</p>
<p>
The points Jb,Jc,Ca,Ba lie on a circle, (Oa); define (Ob) and (Oc) cyclically.
</p>
<p>
X(10206) = radical center of (Oa), (Ob), (Oc); X(10206) lies on the Euler line of OaObOc.
</p>
