<p>
Let N be the nine-point center of triangle ABC, and let<br>
Ha = orthocenter of NBC, and define Hb and Hc cyclically<br>
Oa = circumcenters of NBC, and define Ob and Oc cyclically<br>
Nha = nine-point center of NHbHc, and define Nhb and Nhc cyclically<br>
Noa = nine-point center of NObOc, and define Nob and Noc cyclically.<br>
Then X(10285) = nine-point center of NhaNhbNhc.  This point and X(10286) lie on the Euler line of ABC.  See Tran Quang Hung and Peter Moses, <a href="http://www.hyacinthos.epizy.com/message.php?msg=24664"> 24664</a>.
