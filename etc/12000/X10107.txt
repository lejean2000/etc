<h3 id="X10107">X(10107)&nbsp;=&nbsp; 30th HATZIPOLAKIS-MONTESDEOCA POINT</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; a(3a^2(b+c)-2a b c-3b^3+5b c(b+c)-3c^3) : :
<br>
<b>
X(10107) = 3(4R + r)*X(7) + (4R-3r)*X(8)
</b>
<p><b></b>
Let I be the incenter of a triangle ABC, and<br>
A'B'C' = intouch triangle (the pedal triangle of I)<br>
H' = X(4)-of-A'B'C'<br>
Ab = orthogonal projection of A on H'B', and define Bc and Ca cyclically<br>
Ac = orthogonal projection of A on H'C', and define Ba and Cb cyclically<br>
La = Euler line of AAbAc, and define Lb and Lc cyclically<br>
Ia = excenter of ABC, and define Ib and Ic cyclically<br>
Pa = orthogonal projection of Ia on BC, and define Pb and Pc cyclically<br>
A* = midpoint of AbAc, and define B* and C* cyclically<br>
Qa = line through A* parallel to La, and define Qb and Qc cyclically<br>
</p>
<p>
The lines Qa, Qb, Qc concur in X(10107).  Let
</p>
