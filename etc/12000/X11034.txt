<h3 id="X11034">X(11034)&nbsp;=&nbsp;PERSPECTOR OF THESE TRIANGLES: INCIRCLE-CIRCLES AND AQUILA</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; 3*a^3+12*(b+c)*a^2-13*(b-c)^2*a-2*(b^2-c^2)*(b-c)  :  :  <p>
The incircle-inverses of the sidelines of ABC are three congruent circles with radii=r/2 and concurrent at X(1). The triangle with vertices at the centers of these circles is here named here the <b>incircle-circles triangle</b> and its A-vertex has trilinear coordinates:<br>
A' = 2*a : (a^2+4*a*b+b^2-c^2)/b : (a^2+4*a*c+c^2-b^2)/c<br>
