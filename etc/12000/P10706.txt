<p class="textcenter">
This preamble and centers X(10706)-X(10720) were contributed by Clark Kimberling and Peter Moses, November 8, 2016.
</p>
<p>
Suppose that P is a point on the circumcrcle of a triangle ABC, and let
</p>
<p>
P' = reflection of P in the centroid, G, of ABC<br>
P<sup>c</sup> = complement of P<br>
P<sup>a</sup> = anticomplement of P.<br>
</p>
<p>
Then
</p>
<p></p>
P' = midpoint of G and P<sup>a</sup><br>
P' = reflection of G in P<sup>c</sup><br>
P' = {P<sup>c</sup>, P<sup>a</sup>}-harmonic conjugate of P.<br>
<p></p>
