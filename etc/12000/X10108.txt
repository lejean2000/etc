<h3 id="X10108">X(10108)&nbsp;=&nbsp; 31st HATZIPOLAKIS-MONTESDEOCA POINT</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; a (a^4 (b-c)^2-a^2 (b^4+7 b^3 c+16 b^2 c^2+7 b c^3+c^4)-a (b^5+b^4 c+8 b^3 c^2+8 b^2 c^3+b c^4+c^5)+b c (b^2-c^2)^2+a^3 (b^3-7 b^2 c-7 b c^2+c^3)) : :
<p>
Let I  be the incenter of a triangle ABC, and<br>
A'B'C' = cevian triangle of X(I)<br>
Ab = orthogonal projection of A' on BB', and define Bc and Ca cyclically<br>
Ac = orthogonal projection of A' on CC', and define Ba and Cb cyclically<br>
Abc = orthogonal projection of Ab on CC', and define Bca and Cab cyclically<br>
Acb = orthogonal projection of Ac on BB', and define Bac and Cba cyclically<br>
La = Euler line of IAbcAcb, and define Lb and Lc cyclically<br>
</p>
<p>
The lines La, Lb, Lc concur in X(10108).  (Antreas Hatzipolakis and Angel Montesdeoca, August 10, 2016; see <a href="http://www.hyacinthos.epizy.com/message.php?msg=23949">Hyacinthos 23949</a>)
</p>
<p>
