A'B'C' = pedal triangle of H; i.e., A'B'C' = orthic triangle<br>
Ab = orthogonal projection of A' on HB, and define Bc and Ca cyclically<br>
Ac = orthogonal projection of A' on HC, and define Ba and Cb cyclically<br>
Na = nine-point cneter of A'AbAc, and define Nb and Nc cyclically<br>
Oa = nine-point circle of A'AbAc, and define Ob and Oc cyclically<br>
Qa = reflection of Qa in BC, and define Qb and Qc cyclically<br>
X(11548) = radical center of Qa, Qb, Qc, a point on the Euler line of ABC.  See Antreas Hatzipolakis and Angel Montesdeoca, <a href="http://www.hyacinthos.epizy.com/message.php?msg=24935">Hyacinthos 24935</a>
