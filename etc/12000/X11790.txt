<h3 id="X11790">X(11790)&nbsp;=&nbsp;  CENTER OF 1ST PRZYBY&#321;OWSKI-BOLLIN-MOSES CONIC </h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; a (3 Sqrt[3] a (a^2-b^2-c^2)+2 (5 a-4 b-4 c) S) : :
<p>
See X(11791) and the preamble just before X(11752).  If you have The Geometer's Sketchpad, you can view <a href="TBM1stConic.gsp"><b>TBM 1st Conic</b></a>.
</p>
<p>
A barycentric equation for the 1st Przyby&#322;owski-Bollin-Moses conic is f(a,b,c,x,y,z) + f(b,c,a,y,z,x) + f(c,a,b,z,x,y) = 0, where
</p>
<p>
f(a,b,c,x,z,y) = (b^2 c^2 (a^2-b^2+b c-c^2) (a^4+a^2 b^2-2 b^4+a^2 c^2+4 b^2 c^2-2 c^4)+2 Sqrt[3] a^2 b^2 c^2 (a^2-b^2+b c-c^2) S) x^2+a^2 b c (3 a^6+2 a^5 b-4 a^4 b^2-4 a^3 b^3-a^2 b^4+2 a b^5+2 b^6+2 a^5 c-2 a^4 b c-a^3 b^2 c-2 a^2 b^3 c-a b^4 c+4 b^5 c-4 a^4 c^2-a^3 b c^2-8 a^2 b^2 c^2-a b^3 c^2-2 b^4 c^2-4 a^3 c^3-2 a^2 b c^3-a b^2 c^3-8 b^3 c^3-a^2 c^4-a b c^4-2 b^2 c^4+2 a c^5+4 b c^5+2 c^6+2 Sqrt[3] (a^4-3 a^2 b^2+2 b^4-2 a^2 b c-a b^2 c-3 a^2 c^2-a b c^2-4 b^2 c^2+2 c^4) S) y z  (Peter Moses, January 23, 2017)
</p>
<p>
An equation for the 2nd Przyby&#322;owski-Bollin conic is obtained by substituting -S for S.
</p>
<p>

{1,62}, {3,3196}, {9,10646}, {15,44}, {16,45}
</p>
