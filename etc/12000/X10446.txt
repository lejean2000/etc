<h3 id="X10446">X(10446)&nbsp;=&nbsp;HOMOTHETIC CENTER OF 2nd AND 3rd CONWAY TRIANGLES</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; a^4(b + c) + a^3(b^2 - bc + c^2) - a^2(b^3 + c^3) - a(b - c)^2(b^2 + bc + c^2) - bc(b - c)^2(b + c) : :
<p>
Let O<sub>A</sub> be the A-extraversion of the Conway circle  (the circle centered at the A-excenter and passing through A, with radius Sqrt(r<sub>A</sub><sup>2</sup> + s<sup>2</sup>), where r<sub>A</sub> is the A-exradius).

</p>
