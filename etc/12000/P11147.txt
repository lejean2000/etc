<p>
The <b>anti-Artzt triangle</b>, A'B'C', is here introduced as the triangle of which ABC is the Artzt triangle.  A'B'C' is also the anti-McCay triangle of the 1st Brocard triangle.  A'B'C' is perspective to ABC at X(598) and homothetic to the Artzt triangle at X(2).  A'B'C' is similar to the circumsymmedial triangle with similitude center X(110), and inversely similar to the 4th Brocard triangle with center of inverse similitude X(11187), to the 4th anti-Brocard triangle with center of inverse similitude X(1995), and to the McCay and anti-McCay triangles with center of inverse similitude X(2).
</p>
<p>
Barycentrics for the A-vertex of the anti-Artzt triangle are given by
</p>
<p>
A' = b<sup>2</sup> + c<sup>2</sup> - 5a<sup>2</sup>  :  4a<sup>2</sup> + 4b<sup>2</sup> - 2c<sup>2</sup>  :  4c<sup>2</sup> + 4a<sup>2</sup> - 2b<sup>2</sup><br>
A' = SA - 2*SC - 2*SB  :  SA + SB + 4*SC  :  SC + SA + 4*SB
</p>
