<h3 id="X10128">X(10128)&nbsp;=&nbsp; 4th HATZIPOLAKIS-MOSES-EULER POINT</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; 2 a^6+a^4 b^2-2 a^2 b^4-b^6+a^4 c^2+16 a^2 b^2 c^2+b^4 c^2-2 a^2 c^4+b^2 c^4-c^6 : :
<p>
Let A'B'C' be the pedal triangle of the nine-point center, N, and let<br>
Ab = orthogonal projection of A' on NB'<br>
Ac = orthogonal projection of A' on NC'<br>
M1 = midpoint of AbAC, and define M2 and M3 cyclically<br>
Ma = midpoint of A2A3, and define Mb and Mc cyclically<br>
X(10128) = centroid of M1M2M3<br>
X(10129) = centroid of MaMbMc<br>
Both points lie on the Euler line.  (Antreas Hatzipolakis and Peter Moses, August 18, 2016.  See <a href="http://www.hyacinthos.epizy.com/message.php?msg=24074">Hyacinthos 24074</a>).
</p>
