</p>

<p class="textcenter">
This preamble and centers X(10153)-X(10200) were contributed by CÃ©sar Eliud Lozada, August 28, 2016.
</p>
<p>
Let ABC be the reference triangle and U, V, W three points, at least two of them distinct, and let
</p>
<p>
G<sub>a</sub> = centroid of AVW, and define G<sub>b</sub> and G<sub>c</sub> cyclically<br>
G<sub>u</sub> = centroid of UBC, and define G<sub>v</sub> and G<sub>w</sub> cyclically.
</p>
<p>
Then the six centroids lie on a (possibly degenerate) conic, and the triangles G<sub>a</sub>G<sub>b</sub>G<sub>c</sub> and G<sub>u</sub>G<sub>v</sub>G<sub>w</sub> are congruent and homothetic, and their homothetic center is the center of the conic.<br>
</p>
<p>
The conic is here named the <b>UVW-centroidal conic</b> and also the <b>centroidal conic of UVW</b>.  The triangles G<sub>a</sub>G<sub>b</sub>G<sub>c</sub> and G<sub>u</sub>G<sub>v</sub>G<sub>w</sub> are the <b>1st and 2nd UVW-centroidal triangles</b> and also the <br>
1st and 2nd centroidal triangles of UVW.
</p>
<p>
The center O(UVW) of the centroidal-conic-of-UVW is a triangle center if U, V, W are either triangle centers or vertices of a central triangle.
</p>
<p>
If U, V, W have <b>normalized</b> barycentric coordinates
</p>
<p>
U = x<sub>u</sub> : y<sub>u</sub> : z<sub>u</sub> <br>
V = x<sub>v</sub> : y<sub>v</sub> : z<sub>v</sub> <br>
W = x<sub>w</sub> : y<sub>w</sub> : z<sub>w</sub>
</p>
<p>
then O(UVW) = x<sub>u</sub> + x<sub>v</sub> + x<sub>w</sub> + 1 : y<sub>u</sub> + y<sub>v</sub> + y<sub>w</sub> + 1 : z<sub>u</sub> + z<sub>v</sub> + z<sub>w</sub> + 1.
</p>
<p>
The triangles ABC and G<sub>a</sub>G<sub>b</sub>G<sub>c</sub> are perspective if and only if
</p>
<p>
(x<sub>u</sub> + x<sub>v</sub>)*(y<sub>v</sub> + y<sub>w</sub>)*(z<sub>w</sub> + z<sub>u</sub>) = (x<sub>u</sub> + x<sub>w</sub>)*(y<sub>v</sub> + y<sub>u</sub>)*(z<sub>w</sub> + z<sub>v</sub>).
</p>
<p>
The triangles ABC and G<sub>u</sub>G<sub>v</sub>G<sub>w</sub> are perspective if and only if
</p>
<p>
(x<sub>v</sub> + 1)*(y<sub>w</sub> + 1)*(z<sub>u</sub> + 1) = (x<sub>w</sub> + 1)*(y<sub>u</sub> + 1)*(z<sub>v</sub> + 1).
</p>
<p>
The appearance of (T,i,j,k) in the following list means that
</p>
<p>
X(i) = center of the T-centroidal conic<br>
X(j) = perspector of ABC and 1st T-centroidal triangle<br>
Xk) = perspector of ABC and 2nd T-centroidal triangle
</p>
<p>
The appearance of "np" means "not perspective".
</p>
