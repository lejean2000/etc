<p>
In the plane of a triangle ABC, let H = X(4) = orthocenter and N = X(5) = nine-point center.  Let<br>
A'B'C' = pedal triangle of H; i.e., A'B'C' - orthic triangle<br>
A''B''C'' = pedal triangle of N<br>
A* = reflection of N in BC, and define B* and C* cyclically<br>
Aa = orthogonal projection of A* on AA', and define Bb and Cc cyclically<br>
Ab = orthogonal projection of A* on BB', and define Bc and Ca cyclically<br>
Ac = orthogonal projection of A* on CC', and define Ba and Cb cyclically<br>
La = Euler line of AaAbAc, and define Lb and Lc cyclically<br>
L1 = reflection of La in BC, and define L2 and L3 cyclically.<br>
Ra = reflection of LA in AA', and define Rb and Rc cyclically<br>
Pa = line through A parallel to La, and define Lb and Lc cyclically<br>
Sa = line through A' parallel to La, and define Sb and Sc cyclically<br>
Ua = line through A'' parallel to La, and define Ub and Uc cyclically<br>
Va =  line through A* parallel to La, and define Vb and Vc cyclically<br>
Wa = line through A' parallel to L1, and define Wb and Wc cyclically<br>
