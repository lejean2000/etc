X(37636) is the crosspoint of X(6) and X(2918) wrt both the excentral and tangential triangles.  Equivalently, X(37636) is the intersection of the tangents at X(6) and X(2918) to the Stammler hyperbola.  (Randy Hutson, March 29, 2020)

Let R<sub>A</sub> be the radical axis of (B<sub>C</sub>), (C<sub>B</sub>), and define R<sub>B</sub> and R<sub>C</sub> cyclically.  The parallels to R<sub>A</sub>, R<sub>B</sub>, R<sub>C</sub> through A, B, C, resp., concur in X(1994).  The parallels to R<sub>A</sub>, R<sub>B</sub>, R<sub>C</sub> through A", B", C", resp., concur in X(37636).  (Randy Hutson, March 29, 2020)
