<p>
Contributed by Clark Kimberling and Peter Moses, February 29, 2020.

<p>
Many triangles, including Gemini triangles 1-111, are itemized in <a href="IndexOfTrianglesReferencedInETC.html">Index of Triangles Referenced in ETC</a>.  Gemini triangles 112-119 are introduced here by A-vertex, A', as follows:
</p>
<p>
Gemini triangle 112: A' = a^2 : b^2 - c^2 : c^2 - b^2 <br>
Gemini triangle 113: A' = a^2 : c^2 - b^2 : b^2 - c^2 (see note below)<br>
Gemini triangle 114: A' = b c : a(b - c) : a(c - b) <br>
Gemini triangle 115: A' = b c : a(c - b) : a(b - c) <br>
Gemini triangle 116: A' = a^2 : 2(b^2 - c^2) : 2(c^2 - b^2) <br>
Gemini triangle 117: A' = a^2 : 2(c^2 - b^2) : 2(b^2 - c^2) <br>
Gemini triangle 118: A' = 2 a : b - c : c - b <br>
Gemini triangle 119: A' = 2 a : c - b : b - c <br>
</p>
<p>
Note: Gemini triangle 113 is the orthic triangle of the anticomplementary triangle.
</p>
