<p>
Let H be the orthocenter and M the midpoint of AH. Let B<sub>a</sub> and C<sub>a</sub> be the orthogonal projections of B and C on CM and BM, respectively. Define C<sub>b</sub> and A<sub>c</sub> cyclically, and define A<sub>b</sub> and B<sub>c</sub> cyclically. Let A'B'C' be the triangle having sidelines B<sub>a</sub>C<sub>a</sub>, C<sub>b</sub>A<sub>b</sub>, A<sub>c</sub>B<sub>c</sub>. Then A'B'C' is perspective to ABC, and the perspector is X(36809).
