<h3 id="X37838">X(37838)&nbsp;=&nbsp; VIJAY REFLECTED SQUARES POINT </h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; a^2 / ((a^2 + S)(8S^2 + 5S a^2 - 2a^2 SA) - 2a^2 (b^2 + S)(c^2 + S)) : :
<br>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; a^2*(b^2*(11*a^4 - 18*a^2*b^2 + 7*b^4 - 14*a^2*c^2 - 18*b^2*c^2 + 11*c^4) - 4*(-2*a^4 + a^2*b^2 + 4*b^4 + 4*a^2*c^2 + b^2*c^2 - 2*c^4)*S)*(c^2*(11*a^4 - 14*a^2*b^2 + 11*b^4 - 18*a^2*c^2 - 18*b^2*c^2 + 7*c^4) - 4*(-2*a^4 + 4*a^2*b^2 - 2*b^4 + a^2*c^2 + b^2*c^2 + 4*c^4)*S) : :
<p>
In the plane of a triangle ABC, let A' = reflection of A in BC, and define B' and C' cyclically. Let Ab and Ac be the centers of squares constructed outwards on the sides of BA' and CA' respectively. Likewise define Bc, Ba, Ca, Cb.  Let
</p>
<p>
Pa = CaCb&cap;BaBc,    Pb = AbAc&cap;CbCa,    Pc = BcBa&cap;AcAb.
</p>
<p>
The triangle PaPbPc is perspective to ABC, and the perspector is X(37838); see <a href="X(37838).pdf">X(37838)</a>.  (Vijay Krishna, April 9, 2020).
</p>
