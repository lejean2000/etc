<p class="preamble-title">
Points associated with cubics pK(U*Y,U): X(31644)-X(31648)
</p>
<img src="rightri.svg" style="height:30px" alt="rightri">
</header>
<div class="preamble-sect">
<p>
This preamble and centers X(31644)-X(31648) were contributed by Clark Kimberling and Peter Moses, February 20, 2019.
</p>
<p>
Suppose that U = u : v : w (barycentrics) is a point in the plane of a triangle ABC.  The <b>self-inverse Gemini triangle based on U</b> is defined (above, in the preamble just before X(31610) as the triangle with vertices
</p>
<p>
A' = - u : v + w : v + w,&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; B' = - v : w + u : w + u,&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; C' = - w : u + v : u + v .
</p>
<p>
The triangle A'B'C' is perspective to the anticevian triangle of a point X = x : y : z if and only if X lies on the cubic
</p>
<p>
(y - z) (y z u^2 + x^2 v w) + (z - x) (z x v^2 + y^2 w u) + (x - y) (x y w^2 + z^2 u v) = 0,
</p>
<p>
this being, in Bernard Gibert's notation (see <a href="https://bernard-gibert.pagesperso-orange.fr/files/Resources/SITP.pdf "> <b>Special Isocubics in the Triangle Plane (Section 1.3)</b></a>) the cubic pK(U*Y, U), where
</p>
<p>
Y = 1/(v + w) : 1/(w + u) : 1/(u + v) = cevapoint of X(2) and U, and * = barycentric product.
</p>
<p>
Points on this cubic, which we denote by &Gamma;*(U), include the following:
</p>
<p>
1 : 1 : 1 = X(2) = centroid<br>
u : v : w = U<br>
1/(v + w ) : 1/(w + u) : 1/(u + v) = cevapoint of X(2) and U = isotomic conjugate of the complement of U<br>
u/(v + w ) : v/(w + u) : w/(u + v)
</p>
