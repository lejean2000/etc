<h3 id="X33696">X(33696)&nbsp;=&nbsp; X(1)X(3845)&cap;X(8)X(4532)</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; (5*a^2-c*a-5*b^2+5*c^2)*(5*a^2-b*a+5*b^2-5*c^2) : :

<p>
See Antreas Hatzipolakis and César Lozada, <a href="http://www.hyacinthos.epizy.com/message.php?msg=29263"> <b>Hyacinthos 29263</b></a>.
</p>
<p>
Let I<sub>A</sub>I<sub>B</sub>I<sub>C</sub> be the excentral triangle.  Let L<sub>A</sub> be the trilinear polar of I wrt BCI<sub>A</sub>, and define L<sub>B</sub> and L<sub>C</sub> cyclically.  Let A' = L<sub>B</sub>&cap;L<sub>C</sub>, B' = L<sub>C</sub>&cap;L<sub>A</sub>, C' = L<sub>A</sub>&cap;L<sub>B</sub>.  Let A" be the reflection of A' in BC, and define B" and C" cyclically.  The lines AA", BB", CC" concur in X(33696).  (Randy Hutson, August 19, 2019)
</p>
<p>
X(33696) lies on the Feuerbach hyperbola and these lines: {1, 3845}, {8, 4532}, {3583, 13606}
</p>
