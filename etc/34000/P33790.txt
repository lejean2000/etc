<p class="preamble-title">
Perspectors T3(X(i), X(j)) involving triangles inscribed in a circumconic:  X(33790)-X(33809)
</p>
<img src="rightri.svg" style="height:30px" alt="rightri">

<div class="preamble-sect">
<p>
Centers X(33790)-X(33809) were contributed by Clark Kimberling and Peter Moses, August 4, 2019
</p>
<p>
Suppose that P = p : q : r and U = u : v : w are points  Define M(U) = p/(v - w) : q/(w - u) : r/(u - v), so that M(U) lies on the circumconic p/x + q/y + r/z = 0
</p>
<p>
Let T<sub>1</sub> be the triangle with vertices<br>
A' = -p : q : r<br>
B' = p : - q : r<br>
C' = p : q : -r
</p>
<p>
Let T<sub>2</sub> be the triangle with vertices<br>
A" = 0 : z : -y<br>
B" = -z : 0 : x<br>
C" = y : -x : 0
</p>
<p>
The triangles  T<sub>1</sub> and M(T<sub>2</sub>) are perspective, and their perspector is given by
</p>
<p>
T3(P,X) = p*(y^2 + z^2 - x^2) : q*(z^2 + x^2 - y^2) : r*(x^2 + y^2 - z^2)
</p>
