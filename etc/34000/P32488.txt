<p>
Given a triangle ABC, build the square &sigma;'<sub>a</sub> = AA'<sub>b</sub>A'<sub>a</sub>A'<sub>c</sub>, with the same orientation as ABC, and such that B and C lie on the lines A'<sub>a</sub>A'<sub>b</sub> and A'<sub>a</sub>A'<sub>c</sub>, respectively. Define &sigma;'<sub>b</sub> and &sigma;'<sub>c</sub> cyclically.
</p>
<p>
Build now the square &sigma;"<sub>a</sub> = AA"<sub>b</sub>A"<sub>a</sub>A"<sub>c</sub>, with opposite orientation of ABC, and such that B and C lie on the lines A"<sub>a</sub>A"<sub>b</sub> and A"<sub>a</sub>A"<sub>c</sub>, respectively, and construct &sigma;"<sub>b</sub> and &sigma;"<sub>c</sub> cyclically.
</p>
<p>A'<sub>a</sub> and A"<sub>a</sub> have barycentric coordinates:</p>
<p>&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;A'<sub>a</sub> = 1/SA : 1/(SB-S) : 1/(SC-S).</p>
<p>&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;A"<sub>a</sub> = 1/SA : 1/(SB+S) : 1/(SC+S).</p>
<p>The lines AA'<sub>a</sub>, BB`<sub>b</sub> and CC'<sub>a</sub> concur at X(486) and similarly the lines AA"<sub>a</sub>, BB"<sub>b</sub> and CC"<sub>a</sub> concur at X(485). Moreover, the triangle bounded by the lines A'<sub>b</sub>A'<sub>c</sub>, B'<sub>a</sub>B'<sub>c</sub> and C'<sub>a</sub>C'<sub>b</sub> is the inner-Vecten triangle of ABC and the triangle enclosed by the lines A"<sub>b</sub>A"<sub>c</sub>, B"<sub>a</sub>B"<sub>c</sub> and C"<sub>a</sub>C"<sub>b</sub> is the outer-Vecten triangle of ABC. For the latter reasons, the triangle A'<sub>a</sub>B'<sub>b</sub>C'<sub>c</sub> are here named here the <b>2nd inner-Vecten triangle of ABC</b> and the triangle A"<sub>a</sub>B"<sub>b</sub>C"<sub>c</sub> are here named the <b>2nd outer-Vecten triangle of ABC</b>.</p>
<p>Let A', A" be the centers of &sigma;'<sub>a</sub> and &sigma;"<sub>a</sub>, respectively, and define (B', B"), (C', C") cyclically. The triangles A'B'C' and A"B"C" are here named the <b>3rd inner-Vecten triangle of ABC</b> and <b>3rd outer-Vecten triangle of ABC</b>, respectively. The have first vertices barycentrics coordinates:</p>
<p>&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;A' = (3*S^2+SB*SC-2*S*SW)/SA : SC-S : SB-S</p>
<p>&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;A" = (3*S^2+SB*SC+2*S*SW)/SA : SC+S : SB+S</p>
