Let A'B'C' and A"B"C" be the cevian and circumcevian triangles of the orthocenter. Let L<sub>a</sub> be the radical axis of circles with segments BC and A'A" as diameters, and define L<sub>b</sub> and L<sub>c</sub> cyclically. The triangle formed by lines L<sub>a</sub>, L<sub>b</sub>, L<sub>c</sub> is perspective to ABC, and the perspector is X(34285).  (Angel Montesdeoca, September 15, 2019)
