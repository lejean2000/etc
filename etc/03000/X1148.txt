through a point P, respectively perpendicular to sidelines BC, CA, AB.
Let A<sub>B</sub> be the point where L<sub>A</sub> meets AB, and let
A<sub>C</sub> be the point where L<sub>A</sub> meets AC. Define
B<sub>C</sub>, B<sub>A</sub>, C<sub>A</sub>, C<sub>B</sub> cyclically.
Then X(1148) is the point P, which satisfies</p>
<p>
|PA<sub>B</sub>| + |PA<sub>C</sub>| = |PB<sub>C</sub>| +
|PB<sub>A</sub>| = |PC<sub>A</sub>| + |PC<sub>B</sub>|.</p>
