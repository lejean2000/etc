<h3 id="X2091">X(2091)&nbsp;=&nbsp;2nd STEVANOVIC PERSPECTOR</h3>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) : f(c,a,b), where f(a,b,c) = [1/(b + c - a)](sin
B/2 + sin C/2) sec A/2<br>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; af(a,b,c) : bf(b,c,a) : cf(c,a,b)
<p>The bisector AX(1) of angle A meets the A-excircle in two points;
let A' be the point closer to I, and define B' and C' cyclically. Then
triangle A'B'C' is perspective to the intouch triangle, and the
perspector is X(2091). (Milorad Stevanovic, Hyacinthos #8088,
10/02/03)</p>
<p>X(2091) lies on this line: {7,4146}, {57,173}
