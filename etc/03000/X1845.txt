Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; (y + z) sec A : (z + x) sec B : (x + y) sec C, where x : y : z = X(80)
<br>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp;(y + z) tan A : (z + x) tan B : (x + y) tan C, where x : y : z = X(80)

The following ten points lie on a circle: X(i) for i = 11, 36, 65, 80, 108, 759, 1354, 1845, 2588, 2589. (Chris Van Tienhoven, Hyacinthos, January 4, 2011)
