TRIANGLE</h3>

<p>
X(1485) = X(1486)-of-tangential triangle if ABC is acute. (Darij
Grinberg, 5/24/03)</p>
<p></p>
Let A'B'C' be the orthic triangle.  Let La be the reflection of line B'C' in the perpedicular bisector of BC, and define Lb and Lc cyclically.  Let A" = Lb&cap;Lc, B" = Lc&cap;La, C" = La&cap;Lb.  The lines AA", BB", CC" concur in X(1485). (Randy Hutson, July 31 2018)
