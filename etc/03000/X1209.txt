<p>
In the plane of a triangle ABC, let<br>
A'B'C' = medial triangle;<br>
B<sub>A</sub> = reflection of B' in BC, and define C<sub>B</sub> and A<sub>C</sub> cyclically;<br>
C<sub>A</sub> = reflection of C' in BC, and define A<sub>B</sub> and B<sub>C</sub> cyclically;<br>
O<sub>A</sub> = circle {{B', C', B<sub>A</sub>, C<sub>A</sub>}}, and define O<sub>B</sub> and O<sub>C</sub> cyclically.<br>
Then X(1209) = radical center of O<sub>A</sub>, O<sub>B</sub>, O<sub>C</sub>.
(Dasari Naga Vijay Krishna, September 8, 2021)
