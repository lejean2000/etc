Let A'BC be the external equilateral triangle on side BC, and define
CB'A and AC'B cyclically. Let (AB'C') be the circle passing through the
points A, B', C', and define (BC'A') and (CA'B') cyclically. The three
circles concur in X(1337). Wernau is a town near Stuttgart, the site of
a mathematics olympiad seminar in Spring 2003. (Darij Grinberg;
Hyacinthos, April, 2003: #6874, 6881, 6882; coordinates by Jean-Pierre
Ehrmann)</p>
