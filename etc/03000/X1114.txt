Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; (R + d)cos A - 2R cos B cos C : (R + d)cos B - 2R cos C cos A : (R + d)cos C - 2R cos A cos B, where R = circumradius, d = distance between X(3) and X(4). (Joe Goggins, 2002)
