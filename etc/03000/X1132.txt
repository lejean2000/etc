<h3 id="X1132">X(1132)&nbsp;=&nbsp;ARCTAN(-2) KIEPERT POINT</h3>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; 1/(sin A - 2 cos A) : :
<br>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; csc(A - t) : : , where t = arctan(2)
<p>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; 1/(2 SA - S) : :
<p>
X(1132) is constructed in the manner for X(1131), using squares
erected inward, so that the three equal angles have common measure
arctan(-2), and X(1132) lies on the Kiepert hyperbola. (Darij Grinberg
9/22/02)
</p>
