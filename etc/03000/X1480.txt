<h3 id="X1480">X(1480)&nbsp;=&nbsp;1st SHADOW POINT</h3>

: f(B,C,A) : f(C,A,B), where f(A,B,C) = (2 - cos A)(2 + cos A - cos B -
cos C)<br>

C)f(C,A,B)<br>
