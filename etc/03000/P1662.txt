<br>
The next cluster of points were contributed by Peter J. C. Moses during
August, 2003. Symbols used to represent functions of a,b,c (or A,B,C)
include
<p>&sigma; = area of triangle ABC<br>
&omega; = arccot(cot A + cot B + cot C) = arccot[(a<sup>2</sup> +
b<sup>2</sup> +c<sup>2</sup>)/(4&sigma;)]; &omega; is the Brocard angle
of ABC<br>
e = sqrt(1 - 4 sin<sup>2</sup>&omega;) (as in Gallatly, p. 96, along
with other formulas involving &omega;)<br>
s = (a + b + c)/2 = semiperimeter of ABC<br>
r = &sigma;/s = inradius of ABC</p>
