Let A'B'C' be the excentral triangle, and let U be the bianticevian conic of X(1) and X(4).  Let T be the tangential triangle, wrt the anticevian triangle of X(19), of U.  Then A'B'C' and T are perspective, and their perspector is X(1577).
(Randy Hutson, December 26, 2015)
