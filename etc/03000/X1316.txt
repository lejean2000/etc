<p>
As a point on the Euler line, X(1316) has Shinagawa coefficients

<p>
X(1316) is the point of intersection, other than X(3), of the Euler line and Brocard circle.<br>
X(1316) is the point of intersection, other than X(6), of the Brocard circle and orthosymmedial circle. <br>
X(1316) is the point of intersection of the Euler line and the trilinear polar of X(98). (P.J.C. Moses, 6/22/04)<br>
X(1316) is the orthogonal projection of X(6) on the Euler line.
</p>

The lines A<sub>2</sub>A', B<sub>2</sub>B', C<sub>2</sub>C' concur in X(1316).  (Randy Hutson, January 29, 2015)
