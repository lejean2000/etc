<h3 id="X1080">X(1080)&nbsp;=&nbsp;INTERCEPT OF EULER LINE AND LINE (13)X(98)</h3>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;f(A,B,C) : f(B,C,A) : f(C,A,B),<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
where f(A,B,C) = csc(B - C) [sin 2B cos(C - &omega;) sin(C - &pi;/3) -
sin 2C cos(B - &omega;) sin(B - &pi;/3)]<br>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; g(a,b,c) :
g(b,c,a) : g(c,a,b),<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;

64(3<sup>1/2</sup>)&sigma;<sup>3</sup>]&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (M. Iliev,

<p>Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp;(sin A)f(A,B,C) : (sin B)f(B,C,A) : (sin

</p>
<p>
Coordinates for X(1080) are obtained from those of X(383) by
changing &pi;/3 to - &pi;/3; contributed by Edward Brisse.</p>
