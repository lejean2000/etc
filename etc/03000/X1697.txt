<h3 id="X1697">X(1697)&nbsp;=&nbsp;INSIMILICENTER(BEVAN CIRCLE, INCIRCLE)</h3>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; f(A,B,C) = 3 + cos A - cos B - cos C : :
<br>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; -1 + sin A/2 cos B/2 cos C/2 : :
<p>
The exsimilicenter of the Bevan circle and incircle is X(57).
</p>
<p>
Randy Hutson (January 29, 2015) gives 3 constructions:
<br>
(1)  Let A'B'C' be the mixtilinear incentral triangle.  Let A" be the cevapoint of B' and C', and define B" and C" cyclically. The lines AA", BB", CC" concur in X(1697).
<br>
(2)  Let A'B'C' be the mixtilinear incentral triangle.  Let A" be the trilinear product B'*C', and define B" and C" cyclically.  The lines A'A", B'B", C'C" concur in X(1697).
<br>
Let A'B'C' be the intouch triangle, A"B"C" the extouch triangle, and A*B*C* the excentral triangle.  Let O<sub>A</sub> be the circle through A'A"A*, and define O<sub>B</sub> and O<sub>C</sub>c cyclically.  X(1697) is the radical center of circles O<sub>A</sub>, O<sub>B</sub>, O<sub>C</sub>.
</p>
