Reflections, 2093 - 2105
</p>

<div class="preamble-sect">
Suppose X = x : y : z and U = u : v : w (trilinears). Then the reflection of X in U is the point
<p>(au - bv - cw)x + 2u(by + cz) : (bv - cw - au)y + 2v(cz + ax) : (cw - au - bv)z + 2w(ax + by).</p>

<p>Thus, if x = x(a,b,c), y = x(b,c,a), z = x(c,a,b) and u = u(a,b,c),
v = u(b,c,a), w = u(c,a,b), then the reflection of X in U is given by
f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) : f(c,a,b), where f(a,b,c) = (au - bv - cw)x + 2u(by + cz).</p>

<p>In this section, several coordinate representations were contributed
by Peter J. C . Moses.</p>
