<h3 id="X1488">X(1488)&nbsp;=&nbsp;2nd STEVANOVIC POINT</h3>

<p>
Let U be the A-excenter of triangle ABC; let A' be the incenter of
triangle UBC, and define B', C' cyclically. Let A" = IA'&cap;BC, and
define B", C" cyclically. The lines AA", BB", CC" concur in X(1488).
(Milorad R. Stevanovic, Hyacinthos #7185, 5/21/03. See also X(1130) and
X(1489).)</p>
