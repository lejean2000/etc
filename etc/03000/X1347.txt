<h3 id="X1347">X(1347)&nbsp;=&nbsp;EXSIMILICENTER(NINE-POINT CIRCLE, ORTHOCENTROIDAL CIRCLE)</h3>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;f(a,b,c)
: f(b,c,a) : f(c,a,b), where f(a,b,c) = R cos A - |OH|cos(B - C) + 4 R cos B cos C<br>
<b><br>
X(1347) = 3(-2 + |OH|/R)*X(2) + (3 - |OH|/R)*X(3)
</b>
<p>
As a point on the Euler line, X(1347) has Shinagawa coefficients (R - |OH|,3R - |OH|).
</p>
<p>
X(1347) = external center of similitude of nine-point circle and orthocentroidal circle (Peter J. C. Moses, 4/2003)</p>
