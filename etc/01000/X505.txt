<h3 id="X505">X(505)&nbsp;=&nbsp;3rd ISOSCELIZER POINT</h3>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;f(A,B,C) : f(B,C,A) : f(C,A,B), where f(A,B,C) = 1/(sin B/2 + sin C/2 - sin A/2)<br>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp;af(A,B,C) : bf(B,C,A) : cf(C,A,B)
<p>
The isoscelizer equations H(A,X)D(A,X) = H(B,X)D(B,X) = H(C,X)D(C,X) have solution X = X(505). (Peter Yff, 4/9/99)
</p>
<p>
X(505) is the perspector of ABC and the excentral triangle of the excentral triangle of ABC. (Randy Hutson, 9/23/2011)
</p>

</p>
<p>

X(505) = X(266)-cross conjugate of X(1)<br>

</p>
