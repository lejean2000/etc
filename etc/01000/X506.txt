<h3 id="X506">X(506)&nbsp;=&nbsp;4th ISOSCELIZER POINT</h3>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;f(A,B,C)
        : f(B,C,A) : f(C,A,B), where f(A,B,C) = (cos A/2)<sup>-2/3</sup><br>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp;af(A,B,C) : bf(B,C,A) : cf(C,A,B)
        <p>The isoscelizer equations</p>
<p>
X(A)[area of T(A,X)] = X(B)[area of T(B,X)] = X(C)[area of T(C,X)]<br>
</p>
<p>have solution X = X(506). (Peter Yff, 4/9/99)
</p>
