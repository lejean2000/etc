<b>
X(84) = (r + 2R)*X(1) - 6R*X(2) + (4R - 2r)*X(3) &nbsp;&nbsp; (Peter Moses, April 2, 2013)
</b>
<p>
Let A',B',C' be the excenters. The perpendiculars from B' to AB and from C' to AC meet in a point A&Prime;. Points B&Prime; and C&Prime; are determined cyclically. The hexyl triangle, A&Prime;B&Prime;C&Prime;, is perspective to ABC, and X(84) is the perspector.
</p>
<p>
Let A'B'C' be the extouch triangle.  Let A&Prime; be the orthocenter of AB'C', and define B&Prime; and C&Prime; cyclically.  The lines AA&Prime;, BB&Prime;, CC&Prime; concur in X(84).  (Randy Hutson, September 14, 2016)
</p>
<p>
Let A1B1C1 be the 1st Conway triangle.  Let A' be the crosspoint of B1 and C1, and define B' and C' cyclically.  The lines AA', BB', CC' concur in X(84). (Randy Hutson, December 2, 2017)
</p>
