<h3 id="X507">X(507)&nbsp;=&nbsp;5th ISOSCELIZER  POINT</h3>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;f(A,B,C) : f(B,C,A) : f(C,A,B), where f(A,B,C) = (cos A/2)<sup>-1/2</sup>
<br>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp;af(A,B,C) : bf(B,C,A) : cf(C,A,B)
<p>
The isoscelizer equations
</p>
[area of T(A,X)][X(A)]<sup>2</sup> = [area of T(B,X)][X(B)]<sup>2</sup> = [area of T(C,X)][X(C)]<sup>2</sup>
<p>have solution X = X(507). (Peter Yff, 4/9/99)
</p>
<p>
If you have The Geometer's Sketchpad, you can view <a href="X(507).gsp" class="bold">X(507)</a>.
</p>
<p>X(507) = trilinear square root of X(174)
