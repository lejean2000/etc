<h3 id="X504">X(504)&nbsp;=&nbsp;2nd ISOSCELIZER POINT</h3>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;f(A,B,C) : f(B,C,A) : f(C,A,B), where f(A,B,C) = b sin B/2 + c sin C/2 - a sin A/2<br>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp;af(A,B,C) : bf(B,C,A) : cf(C,A,B)
<p>
The isoscelizer equations [H(A,X)]/a = [H(B,X)]/b = [H(C,X)]/c have solution X = X(504). (Peter Yff, 4/9/99)
</p>

</p>
