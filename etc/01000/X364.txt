<h3 id="X364">X(364)&nbsp;=&nbsp;WABASH CENTER (EQUAL AREAS ISOSCELIZER POINT)</h3>
<a id="WabashPoint"></a>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; b<sup>1/2</sup> + c<sup>1/2</sup> -
        a<sup>1/2</sup> : :
<p>
If X = X(364), the isoscelizer triangles T(X,a), T(X,b), T(X,c) have equal areas.
</p>
<p>
If you have The Geometer's Sketchpad, you can view <a href="X(364).gsp" class="bold">X(364)</a>.
</p>
<p>
X(364) lies on these lines: {1, 365}, {9, 366}, {57, 40378}, {165, 4166}, {239, 40383}, {1743, 2068}, {20673, 20695}
</p>
<p>
X(364) = X(366)-Ceva conjugate of X(1)
</p>
