<h3 id="X481">X(481)&nbsp;=&nbsp;1st EPPSTEIN POINT</h3>
<a id="FirstEppsteinPoint"></a>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; 1 - 2 sec A/2 cos B/2 cos C/2 : :
<br>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; 1 - 4(area)/[a(b + c - a)] : :  &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (E. Brisse, 3/20/01)
<br>
<b>
X(481) = s*X(1) - (r + 4R)*X(7)<br>
X(481) = [A]/Ra + [B]/Rb + [C]/Rc - X(1)/r, where Ra, Rb, Rc = radii of Soddy circles<br>
X(481) = [A]/Ra + [B]/Rb + [C]/Rc + X(175)/Rs', where Ra, Rb, Rc = radii of Soddy circles, and Rs' = radius of outer Soddy circle
</b>
<p>
Let S be the inner Soddy circle and S<sub>A</sub>, S<sub>B</sub>,
        S<sub>C</sub> the Soddy circles tangent to S. Let Ia =
        S&cap;S<sub>A</sub>, Ea = S<sub>B</sub>&cap;S<sub>C</sub>, and
        determine Ib, Ic, Eb, Ec cyclically. Then X(481) is the point of
        concurrence of lines I<sub>A</sub>-to-E<sub>A</sub>,
        I<sub>B</sub>-to-E<sub>B</sub>, I<sub>C</sub>-to-E<sub>C</sub>.
</p>
<p>
<b>David Eppstein,</b> "Tangent spheres and triangle centers,"<i>American Mathematical Monthly,</i> 108 (2001) 63-66.
</p>
<p>
Let S' be the outer Soddy center, X(175).  X(481) is the point of concurrence of the Soddy lines of BS'C, CS'A, and AS'B.  (Randy Hutson, September 14, 2016)
</p>
<p>
Let La be the line tangent to the outer Soddy circle at the touchpoint with the A-Soddy circle.  Define Lb and Lc cyclically.  Let A' = Lb&cap;Lc and define B' and C'cyclically.  Triangle A'B'C', here introduced as the outer Soddy tangential triangle, is perspective to ABC, and the perspector is X(481).  (Randy Hutson, September 14, 2016)
</p>
