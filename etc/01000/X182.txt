<b>
X(182) = X(3) + X(6)

</b>
X(182) is the midpoint of the Brocard diameter (the segment X(3)-to-X(6)); also the center of the 1st Lemoine circle, and the center of the Brocard circle.  If you have The Geometer's Sketchpad, you can view <a href="X(1316).gsp" class="bold">X(1316)</a>, which includes X(182).

<p>
X(182) = radical center of Lucas(2 tan &omega;) circles, where 2 tan &omega; is the value of t for which the Brocard circle is the radical circle of the Lucas(t) circles.  (Randy Hutson, January 29, 2015)
</p>
