Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; (cos A)(v + w - u) : : , where u = u(A,B,C) = cos A cos(B - A) cos(C - A)
<br>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp;  a[a^8 + b^8 + c^8 - 4a^6(b^2 + c^2) + a^4(6b^4 + 6c^4 + 5b^2c^2) - a^2(4b^6 + 4c^6 - b^4c^2 - b^2c^4) - 2b^2c^2(b^4 + c^4 - b^2c^2)] : :

<p>
Let A' be the isogonal conjugate of the A-vertex of the outer Napoleon triangle, and define B' and C' cyclically.  Let A&Prime; be the isogonal conjugate of the A-vertex of the inner Napoleon triangle, and define B&Prime; and C&Prime; cyclically.  The lines A'A&Prime;, B'B&Prime;, C'C&Prime; concur in X(195).  (Randy Hutson, November 18, 2015)
</p>
<p>
The Napoleon axis and Napoleon-Feuerbach cubic K005 meet in three points:  X(17), X(18), and X(195).
(Randy Hutson, November 18, 2015)
</p>
