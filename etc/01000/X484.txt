<h3 id="X484">X(484)&nbsp;=&nbsp;1st EVANS PERSPECTOR</h3>
<a id="1st-EvansPerspector"></a>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;1 + 2(cos A - cos B - cos C) : 1 + 2(cos B - cos C - cos A) : 1 + 2(cos C - cos A - cos B)
<br>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; a^3 + a^2 (b + c) - a (b^2 + b c + c^2) - (b - c)^2 (b + c) : :
<br>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp;a[1 + 2(cos A - cos B - cos C)] : b[1 + 2(cos B - cos C - cos A)] : c[1 + 2(cos C - cos A - cos B)]

X(484) is the perspector of the excentral triangle and the triangle A'B'C', where A' is the reflection of vertex A in sideline BC and B',         C' are determined cyclically. (Lawrence Evans, 10/22/98)
</p>
