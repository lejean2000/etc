<h3 id="X589">X(589)&nbsp;=&nbsp;2nd KENMOTU-VAN LAMOEN POINT</h3>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) : f(c,a,b), where f(a,b,c) = a/(a<sup>2</sup> - 4*area(ABC))<br>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp;g(a,b,c) : g(b,c,a) : g(c,a,b), where g(a,b,c) = af(a,b,c)
<p>
X(589) is the perspector of triangles ABC and A'B'C', where A' is the circumcenter of X(372) and the points where the squares in the Kenmotu configuration with center X(372) meet sideline BC, and B' and
C' are defined cyclically. See the reference at X(588).
</p>
<p>
X(588) is the homothetic center of triangle ABC and the Lucas(-2:1) homothetic triangle; see X(371) and X(588). (Randy Hutson, 9/23/2011)
</p>
<p>

</p>

</p>
