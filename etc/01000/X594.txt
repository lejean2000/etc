<h3 id="X594">X(594)&nbsp;=&nbsp;ISOGONAL CONJUGATE OF X(593)</h3>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (b +
        c)<sup>2</sup>/a : (c + a)<sup>2</sup>/b : (a + b)<sup>2</sup>/c<br>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp;(b + c)<sup>2</sup> : (c + a)<sup>2</sup> : (a + b)<sup>2</sup>
<p>
Let A'B'C' be the Feuerbach triangle.  Let La be the tangent to the nine-point circle at A', and define Lb and Lc cyclically.  La, Lb, Lc are also tangent to the Steiner inellipse.  Let A&Prime;, B&Prime;, C&Prime; be the respective touchpoints.  The lines AA&Prime;, BB&Prime;, CC&Prime; concur in X(594).  (Randy Hutson, October 15, 2018)
</p>
<p>
For a construction of X(594), see Dasari Naga Vijay Krishna, "On A Simple Construction of Triangle Centers X(8), X(197), X(K) &amp; X(594)",  <a href="https://admin.umt.edu.pk/Media/Site/SSC/SubSites/SIR/FileManager/Sir2Three/Article-3%20(1).pdf" class="bold">Scientific Inquiry and Review, Vol. 2, Issue 3, July 2018.</a>
</p>
