A'B'C' = excentral triangle;<br>
Ba = reflection of B in A', and define Cb and Ac cyclically;<br>
Ca = reflection in C in A', and define Ab and Bc cyclically;<br>
Va = AcBc&cap;CbAb, and define Vb and Vc cyclically.<br>
The triangle VaVbVc is perspective to ABC, and the perspector is X(57).<br>
(Dasari Naga Vijay Krishna, June 23, 2021)
