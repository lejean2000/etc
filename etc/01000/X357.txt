<h3 id="X357">X(357)&nbsp;=&nbsp;1st MORLEY-TAYLOR-MARR CENTER</h3>
<a id="1stMorleyTaylorMarrCenter"></a>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;sec A/3 : sec B/3 :
        sec C/3<br>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp;sin A sec A/3 : sin B sec B/3 : sin C sec C/3
        <p>X(357) is the perspector of Morley triangle and ABC, and also the
        Hofstadter 1/3 point. See</p>
<p><b>F. Glanville Taylor and W. L. Marr,</b> "The six trisectors of
        each of the angles of a triangle," <i>Proceedings of the Edinburgh
        Mathematical Society</i> 33 (1913-14) 119-131, especially item 9, page
        127.</p>
<p>If you have The Geometer's Sketchpad, you can view
<a href="X(357).gsp" class="bold">X(357)</a>.
        <p>X(357) lies on these lines: 356,358&nbsp;&nbsp; 1134,3275&nbsp;&nbsp;  3280,5454
</p>
<p>
X(357) = isogonal conjugate of X(358)<br>
X(357) = {X(1135),X(3272)}-harmonic conjugate of X(3603)<br>
X(357) = trilinear product of vertices of 1st Morley triangle<br>
