<h3 id="X578">X(578)&nbsp;=&nbsp;ORTHOHARMONIC OF X(216)</h3>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; sin A + cot D/2 cos A, where cot(D/2) = (a<sup>2</sup>b<sup>2</sup>c<sup>2</sup> cos A cos B cos C)/[8*(area)<sup>3</sup>]
<br>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; cos A + sin A (tan A + tan B + tan C) : :
<br>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; cos A (cot A cot B cot C) + sin A : :
<br>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; a^2[a^8 - 3a^6(b^2 + c^2) + a^4(3b^4 + 4b^2c^2 + 3c^4) - a^2(b^2 - c^2)^2(b^2 + c^2) - 2b^2c^2(b^2 - c^2)^2] : :

Joe Goggins notes (10/1/2008), in connection with the note at X(389), that trilinears for X(578) are sin(A-T) : sin(B-T) : sin(C-T), where tan(T) = - cot A cot B cot C.
</p>
