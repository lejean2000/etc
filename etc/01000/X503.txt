<h3 id="X503">X(503)&nbsp;=&nbsp;1st ISOSCELIZER POINT</h3>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;f(A,B,C) : f(B,C,A) : f(C,A,B), where f(A,B,C) = sec B/2 + sec C/2 - sec A/2<br>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp;af(A,B,C) : bf(B,C,A) : cf(C,A,B)
<p>
The isoscelizer equations aH(A,X) = bH(B,X) = cH(C,X) have solution X = X(503). (Peter Yff, 4/9/99)
</p>
<p>
If you have The Geometer's Sketchpad, you can view <a href="X(503).gsp" class="bold">X(503)</a>.
<p>

</p>
