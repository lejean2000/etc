<p class="bold">X(33) = (r + 2R - s)(r + 2R + s)*X(1) -
        6rR*X(2) + 4rR*X(3)&nbsp;&nbsp; (Peter Moses, April 2, 2013)
</p>
<p>
Let L<sub>A</sub> be the reflection of line BC in the internal angle
        bisector of angle A, and define L<sub>B</sub> and L<sub>C</sub>
        cyclically. Let DEF be the triangle formed by L<sub>A</sub>,
        L<sub>B</sub>, L<sub>C</sub>. Then DEF (the intangents triangle) is
        homothetic to the orthic triangle, and the homothetic center is X(33).
        (Randy Hutson, 9/23/2011)
</p>
