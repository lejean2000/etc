<h3 id="X13291">X(13291)&nbsp;=&nbsp;PARALLELOGIC CENTER OF THESE TRIANGLES: 2nd PARRY TO 1st HYACINTH</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; (2*a^10-4*(b^2+c^2)*a^8+2*(b^4+4*b^2*c^2+c^4)*a^6-(b^2+c^2)^3*a^4+2*(b^4-b^2*c^2+c^4)^2*a^2-(b^6+c^6)*(b^2-c^2)^2)*(b^2-c^2)  :  :
<br>
<b>X(13291) = 2*X(125)-3*X(8371) = 3*X(1649)-4*X(5972) = X(3448)-3*X(5466) = X(3448)-4*X(12064)</b>
<p>
The reciprocal parallelogic center of these triangles is X(6102).
</p>
<p>
Let A* be the intersection of the lies through X(110) perpendicular to BC, and define B* and C* cyclically. Then
X(13291) is the centroid of the degenerate triangle A*B*C*.  (Angel Montesdeoca, November 1, 2021). See <a href="X(13291).png"> <b>X(13291)</b></a>.
</p>
<p>
See Antreas Hatzipolakis and Angel Montesdeoca, <a href="https://groups.io/g/euclid/message/2948"> <b>euclid 2948</b></a>.
</p>
