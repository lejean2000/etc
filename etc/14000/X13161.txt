<h3 id="X13161">X(13161)&nbsp;=&nbsp;  X(1)X(4)&cap;X(10)X(75)</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; (b+c)*a^3+(b^2+c^2)*a^2+(b+c)* (b^2+c^2)*a+(b^2-c^2)^2  : :
<b><br>
X(13161) = SW*X(1) - 2*r^2*X(4) = 2*(r^2 - s^2)*X(10) + (2*s^2 - SW)*X(75)
</b>
<p>
See Tran Quang Hung and César Lozada, <a href="http://www.hyacinthos.epizy.com/message.php?msg=25735">Hyacinthos 25735</a>.
</p>
<p>
Let A'B'C' be the Gergonne line extraversion triangle, as defined at X(10180).  Let La be the reflection of line BC in line B'C', and define Lb and Lc cyclically.  Let A" = Lb&cap;Lc, and define B" and C" cyclically.  The lines A'A", B'B", C'C" concur in X(13161).  (Randy Hutson, July 21, 2017)
</p>
<p>
For another construction see: Antreas Hatzipolakis and Peter Moses, <a href="https://groups.io/g/euclid/message/2531"><b>Euclid 2531 </b></a>.
</p>
