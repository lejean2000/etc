<h3 id="X13626">X(13626)&nbsp;=&nbsp; MIDPOINT OF X(381) AND X(1113) </h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; 3*SB*SC - S^2 + K*(2*S)/(3*R) : : ,  where K = 2*S*|OH|
<b><br>
X(13626) = (-3*R*S-2*K)*X(3) + (3*R*S-K)*X( 4)
</b>
<p>
As a point of the Euler line, X(13626) has Shinagawa coefficients (-3RS + 2K, 9RS).
</p>
<p>
See Antreas Hatzipolakis and César Lozada, <a href="http://www.hyacinthos.epizy.com/message.php?msg=26240"> <b>Hyacinthos 26240</b></a>.
</p>
<p>
X(13626) lies on this line:  {2,3}<br>
</p>
