<h3 id="X13627">X(13627)&nbsp;=&nbsp; MIDPOINT OF X(381) AND X(1114) </h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; 3*SB*SC - S^2 - K*(2*S)/(3*R) : : ,  where K = 2*S*|OH|
<b><br>
X(13627) = (-3*R*S-2*K)*X(3) - (3*R*S-K)*X( 4)
</b>
<p>
As a point of the Euler line, X(13626) has Shinagawa coefficients (-3RS - 2K, 9RS).
</p>
