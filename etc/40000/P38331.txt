<p>
This preamble is based on notes from Vu Thanh Tung, May 9, 2020.<br>
<p>
In the plane of a triangle ABC, let P = p : q : r (barycentrics), and let <br>
A<sub>1</sub>B<sub>1</sub>C<sub>1</sub> = circumcevian triangle of P<br>
H<sub>bc</sub> = orthocenter of PB<sub>1</sub>C, and define H<sub>ca</sub> and H<sub>ab</sub> cyclically<br>
H<sub>cb</sub> = orthocenter of PC<sub>1</sub>B, and define H<sub>ac</sub> and H<sub>ba</sub> cyclically<br>
A' = H<sub>ca</sub>H<sub>ac</sub>&cap;H<sub>ab</sub>H<sub>ba</sub>, and define B' and C' cyclically.<br>
Then A'B'C' is perspective to ABC, and the perspector is given by
</p>
<p>
V(P) = (2 b^2 c^2 p^2 + a^2 c^2 p q + b^2 c^2 p q - c^4 p q + a^2 b^2 p r - b^4 p r + b^2 c^2 p r + a^4 q r - a^2 b^2 q r - a^2 c^2 q r)
* (-a^2 b^2 c^2 p q + b^4 c^2 p q - a^2 c^4 p q - 2 b^2 c^4 p q + c^6 p q - a^4 c^2 q^2 + a^2 b^2 c^2 q^2 - a^2 c^4 q^2 + a^4 b^2 p r - 2 a^2 b^4 p r + b^6 p r - 2 b^4 c^2 p r + b^2 c^4 p r + a^6 q r - 2 a^4 b^2 q r + a^2 b^4 q r - a^4 c^2 q r - a^2 b^2 c^2 q r)
* (a^4 c^2 p q + b^4 c^2 p q - 2 a^2 c^4 p q - 2 b^2 c^4 p q + c^6 p q - a^2 b^4 p r + b^6 p r - a^2 b^2 c^2 p r - 2 b^4 c^2 p r + b^2 c^4 p r + a^6 q r - a^4 b^2 q r - 2 a^4 c^2 q r - a^2 b^2 c^2 q r + a^2 c^4 q r - a^4 b^2 r^2 - a^2 b^4 r^2 + a^2 b^2 c^2 r^2) : :
</p>

See <a href="VuCircumcevianOrthocenterPerspector.pdf"><b>Vu Circumcevian-Orthocenter Perspector</b></a>.
</p>
