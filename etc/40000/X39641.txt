<h3 id="X39641">X(39641) = 1ST IMAGINARY FOCUS OF BROCARD INELLIPSE</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp;  Sin[A]*(Sin[A] + i*Cos[A]) : :
<br>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp;  a^2*(i*(b^4 + c^4 - a^2*(b^2 + c^2)) + 2*(b^2 + c^2)*S) : :
<b><br>
X(39641) = (1 + i)*X[39] - X[3102] = (1 - i)*X[39] - X[3103] = X[3102] - i*X[3103]
</b>
<p>
Contributed by Peter Moses, September 11, 2020.
</p>
<p>
The <b>real foci</b> of the Brocard inellipse are the Brocard points.  They are a bicentric pair, with barycentrics 1/b^2 : 1/c^2 : 1/a^2 and 1/c^2 : 1/b^2 : 1/a^2, denoted by PU(1) in <a href="BicentricPairs.html">Bicentric Pairs of Points</a>.
</p>
<p>
In contrast to the real foci, the <b>imaginary foci</b> of the Brocard inellipse are major triangle centers. They both lie on the Brocard axis, X(3)X(6), and on the Kiepert circumhyperbola, and on the following curves: K003, K019, K049, K102, K115, K166, K248, K268, K326, K359, K369, K373, K444, K512, K516, K587, K588, K643, K688, K708, K731, K763, K801, K828, K833, K1096, K1098, K1103, K1113, K1139, Q019, Q039, Q065, Q073, Q088, Q094, Q138.
</p>
