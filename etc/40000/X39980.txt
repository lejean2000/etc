<h3 id="X39980">X(39980) = ISOGONAL CONJUGATE OF X(3731)</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; a*(3*a + 3*b - c)*(3*a - b + 3*c) : :
<p>
Let DEF be the intouch triangle and I<sub>a</sub>I<sub>b</sub>I<sub>c</sub> the excentral-triangle. Let D' be the trisector nearest I<sub>a</sub> of segment DI<sub>a</sub>. Let L<sub>a</sub> be the radical axis of (DEF) and (BCD'), and define L<sub>b</sub> and L<sub>c</sub> cyclically.  The triangle A'B'C' formed by the lines L<sub>a</sub>, L<sub>b</sub>, L<sub>c</sub> is perspective to ABC, and the perspector is X(39980). See also X(34578).  (Angel Montesdeoca, December 31, 2020)
