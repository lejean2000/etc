Let Ab be the point in (BC) that satisfies |BAb| = |AC|, and let Ac be the point in (BC) that satisfies |BAc| = |AB|. Let La be the line of the diagonal points, other than A, of the complete quadrangle BCAbAc. Define Lb and Lc cyclically. Let A' = Lb&cap;Lc, and define B' and C' cyclically.
