<br>

<p>
In contrast to the real foci, the <b>imaginary foci</b> of the Brocard inellipse are major triangle centers. They both lie on the Brocard axis, X(3)X(6), and on the Kiepert circumhyperbola, and on the following curves: K003, K019, K049, K102, K115, K166, K248, K268, K326, K359, K369, K373, K444, K512, K516, K587, K588, K643, K688, K708, K731, K763, K801, K828, K833, K1096, K1098, K1103, K1113, K1139, Q019, Q039, Q065, Q073, Q088, Q094, Q138.
</p>
