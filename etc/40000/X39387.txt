<p>
As a point on the Euler line, X(39387) has Shinagawa coefficients (E+F+2*S, -S)
</p>
<p>
Let A*B*C* be the inner- (or outer-) Vecten triangle of ABC. Let a* be the circle through A centered at A*, and define b* and c* cyclically. Let {A', A"} = b*&cap;c* and define {B', B"} and {C', C"} cyclically, with A&prime;, B&prime;, C&prime; farthest to the radical center of a*, b*, c*. Then AA', BB', CC' concur in X(4) and AA", BB", CC" concur in a point, Q, on the Euler line of ABC (Dao Thanh Oai, August 17, 2020).
</p>
<p>
Continuing, if A*B*C* is the outer-Vecten triangle of ABC then Q=X(39837), and if A*B*C* is the innerr-Vecten triangle of ABC then Q=X(39838).  (C&eacute;sar Lozada, August 17, 2020).
</p>
<p>
Let DEF be the inner Vecten triangle and let V be the inner Vecten circle.  Let U be the circle having diameter BC, and let A' be the point of intersection, other than D, of U and V.  Define B' and C' cyclically.  The triangle A'B'C' is perspective to ABC, and the perspector is X(39387).  For the "outer version", see X(39838).  See also <a href="TriangleCentersAroundVectenCircles.pdf">Triangle centers around Vecten circles</a>. (Stanis&lstrok;aw Majchrzak, June 24, 2021)
