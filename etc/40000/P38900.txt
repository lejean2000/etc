<p>
This preamble is contributed by Vu Thanh Tung, June 22, 2020.<br>
<p>
Let P = p : q : r (barycentrics) be a point in the plane of a triangle ABC, not on any of the lines AO, BO, CO, where O = X(3) = circumcenter. Let
</p>
<p>
A<sub>1</sub>B<sub>1</sub>C<sub>1</sub> = pedal triangle of P <br>
A<sub>2</sub> = the point, other than A, where the circles (ABC) and (AB<sub>1</sub>C<sub>1</sub>) meet, and define B<sub>2</sub> and C<sub>2</sub> cyclically <br>
A<sub>3</sub> = BB<sub>2</sub>&cap;CC<sub>2</sub>, define B<sub>3</sub> and C<sub>3</sub> cyclically <br>
A<sub>4</sub>B<sub>4</sub>C<sub>4</sub> = circumcevian triangle of P.
</p>
<p>
Then A<sub>4</sub>B<sub>4</sub>C<sub>4</sub> is perspective to A<sub>2</sub>B<sub>2</sub>C<sub>2</sub>, and the perspector is the point V(P), here named the <b>Vu 1st PCC perspector</b>, given by
</p>
<p>
V(P) = a^2 (2 b^2 c^2 p^2 + a^2 c^2 p q + b^2 c^2 p q - c^4 p q + a^2 b^2 p r - b^4 p r + b^2 c^2 p r + a^4 q r - a^2 b^2 q r - a^2 c^2 q r) : :
</p>
<p>
Also, A<sub>4</sub>B<sub>4</sub>C<sub>4</sub> is perspective to A<sub>3</sub>B<sub>3</sub>C<sub>3</sub>, and the perspector is the point T(P), here named the <b>Vu 2nd PCC perspector</b>, given by
</p>
<p>
T(P) = -2 a^6 c^2 p^3 q + 2 a^4 b^2 c^2 p^3 q + 2 a^2 b^4 c^2 p^3 q - 2 b^6 c^2 p^3 q + 2 a^4 c^4 p^3 q - 4 a^2 b^2 c^4 p^3 q + 2 b^4 c^4 p^3 q + 2 a^2 c^6 p^3 q + 2 b^2 c^6 p^3 q - 2 c^8 p^3 q - 4 a^6 c^2 p^2 q^2 +
 8 a^4 b^2 c^2 p^2 q^2 - 4 a^2 b^4 c^2 p^2 q^2 + 4 a^2 c^6 p^2 q^2 - 2 a^6 b^2 p^3 r + 2 a^4 b^4 p^3 r + 2 a^2 b^6 p^3 r - 2 b^8 p^3 r + 2 a^4 b^2 c^2 p^3 r - 4 a^2 b^4 c^2 p^3 r + 2 b^6 c^2 p^3 r + 2 a^2 b^2 c^4 p^3 r + 2 b^4 c^4 p^3 r - 2 b^2 c^6 p^3 r - 3 a^8 p^2 q r +
 2 a^6 b^2 p^2 q r + 4 a^4 b^4 p^2 q r - 2 a^2 b^6 p^2 q r - b^8 p^2 q r + 2 a^6 c^2 p^2 q r - 8 a^4 b^2 c^2 p^2 q r + 2 a^2 b^4 c^2 p^2 q r + 4 b^6 c^2 p^2 q r + 4 a^4 c^4 p^2 q r + 2 a^2 b^2 c^4 p^2 q r - 6 b^4 c^4 p^2 q r - 2 a^2 c^6 p^2 q r + 4 b^2 c^6 p^2 q r - c^8 p^2 q r -
 4 a^8 p q^2 r + 8 a^6 b^2 p q^2 r - 4 a^4 b^4 p q^2 r - 4 a^6 c^2 p q^2 r + 4 a^2 b^4 c^2 p q^2 r + 4 a^4 c^4 p q^2 r - 8 a^2 b^2 c^4 p q^2 r + 4 a^2 c^6 p q^2 r - 4 a^6 b^2 p^2 r^2 + 4 a^2 b^6 p^2 r^2 + 8 a^4 b^2 c^2 p^2 r^2 - 4 a^2 b^2 c^4 p^2 r^2 - 4 a^8 p q r^2 -
 4 a^6 b^2 p q r^2 + 4 a^4 b^4 p q r^2 + 4 a^2 b^6 p q r^2 + 8 a^6 c^2 p q r^2 - 8 a^2 b^4 c^2 p q r^2 - 4 a^4 c^4 p q r^2 + 4 a^2 b^2 c^4 p q r^2 - 4 a^8 q^2 r^2 + 4 a^4 b^4 q^2 r^2 -  8a a^4 b^2 c^2 q^2 r^2 + 4 a^4 c^4 q^2 r^2 : :
</p>
<p>
See <a href="Vu%20Pedal%20Circles%20Circumcevian%20Perspector.pdf"><b>Vu Pedal Circles Circumcevian Perspector</b></a>.
</p>
