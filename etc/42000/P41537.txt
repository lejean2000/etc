This preamble and centers X(41537)-X(41679) were contributed by C&eacute;sar Eliud Lozada, March 07, 2021.
</p>
<p><i>
Let P', P" be any two distinct, finite points not both on the circumcircle of a triangle ABC and such that they are collinear with its circumcenter O=X(3). Then the pedal triangles A'B'C' and A"B"C" of P' and P" are orthologic.
</i></p>
<p>
Let Q' and Q" be the orthologic centers (A'B'C' to A"B"C") and (A"B"C" to A'B'C'), respectively. If P' is fixed and P" moves along the line OP', then Q' describes a rectangular hyperbola &Hopf;(P') circumscribed to A'B'C', whilst Q" moves on a line &Lopf;(P'). &Hopf;(P) is named here the <b>bipedal circumcentral conic of P'</b> (shortened to the <b>BPC-conic of P'</b>) and &Lopf;(P') is named here the <b>bipedal circumcentral line of P'</b>, abbreviated as the <b>BPC-line of P'</b>.
</p>
<p>If P' = x:y:z (barycentrics), then
</p>

<ul>
<li>the center of &Hopf;(P') is:<br>
&Hopf;*(P') =
(a^2*b^4*c^4*x^3+((3*SA-SW)*S^2-2*(SB+SC)^2*SA)*b^2*c^2*x*y*z+((S^2-SB^2)*b^2*z+(S^2-SC^2)*c^2*y)*SA*a^2*y*z-b^2*c^2*((2*S^2-(2*SB+SC)*SB)*b^2*z+(2*S^2-(SB+2*SC)*SC)*c^2*y)*x^2+((S^2*SA+SB^3)*b^4*z^2+(S^2*SA+SC^3)*c^4*y^2)*x)*(SB+SC) : :
</li>
<li>the tripole of &Lopf;(P') is:<br>
&Lopf;*(P') = (SA*a^2*y-SB*b^2*x)*(SA*a^2*z-SC*c^2*x)*(b^2*z+c^2*y) : :
<br>
<br>
If P' lies on K174, &Lopf;, then *(P') lies on the circumcircle of ABC.
</li>
</ul>
