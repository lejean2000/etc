<p>
As a point on the Euler line, X(3628) has Shinagawa coefficients (5, 1).
</p>
<p>
X(3628) is the centroid of the set {A', B', C', X(5)}, where A'B'C' is the medial triangle; more generally, H<sup>-1</sup>(X; M, 2) is the centroid of the set {A', B', C', X}. (Angel Montesdeoca, 12/20/2011)
</p>
<p>
For another construction see Antreas Hatzipolakis and Peter Moses, <a href="https://groups.io/g/euclid/message/2576"><b>Euclid 2576 </b></a>.
