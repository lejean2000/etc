<h3 id="X3406">X(3406)&nbsp;=&nbsp;SEC(A + 2&omega;) POINT</h3>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; sec(A + 2&omega;) : :
<p>
Let A' be the apex of the isosceles triangle BA'C constructed outward on BC such that &ang;A'BC = &ang;A'CB = &omega;.  Define B' and C' cyclically.  Let Oa be the circumcenter of BA'C, and define Ob and Oc cyclically.  The lines AOa, BOb, COc concur in X(3406).  (Randy Hutson, July 20, 2016)
</p>
<p>
Let A' be the apex of the isosceles triangle BA'C constructed inward on BC such that &ang;A'BC = &ang;A'CB = 2&omega;.  Define B' and C' cyclically.  Let Ha be the orthocenter of BA'C, and define Hb and Hc cyclically.  The lines AHa, BHb, CHc concur in X(3406).  (Randy Hutson, July 20, 2016)
</p>
<p>

note at X(3364).</p>
