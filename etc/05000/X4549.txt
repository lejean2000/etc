POINT</h3>

where f(a,b,c) = (b<sup>2</sup> + c<sup>2</sup> -
a<sup>2</sup>)(3a<sup>8</sup> + b<sup>8</sup> + c<sup>8</sup> -

X(4549). &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (Antreas Hatzipolakis, January 14
2012; see <a href=
