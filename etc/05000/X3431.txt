Let A'B'C' be the orthocentroidal triangle.  X(3431) is the radical center of the circumcircles of A'BC, B'CA, C'AB. (Randy Hutson, June 27, 2018)

<p>
In the plane of a triangle ABC, let<br>
Oa = circle with diameter BC, and define Ob and Oc cyclically;<br>
A' = reflection of A in Oa, and define B' and C' cyclically;<br>
Pa = polar of A' with respect to Oa, and define Pb and Pc cyclically;<br>
A" = Pc&cap;Pb, and define B" and C" cyclically.<br>
The triangle A"B"C" is perspective to ABC, and the perspector is X(3431).
(Dasari Naga Vijay Krishna, June 8, 2021)
