<br>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; (csc A)(1 + cos 2B + cos 2C) : :
<p>
Let La, Lb, Lc be the lines through A, B, C, resp. parallel to the orthic axis.  Let Ma, Mb, Mc be the reflections of BC, CA, AB in La, Lb, Lc, resp.  Let A' = Mb&cap;Mc, and define B' and C' cyclically.  Triangle A'B'C' is inversely similar to, and 3 times the size of, ABC.  Let A"B"C" be the reflection of A'B'C' in the orthic axis.  The triangle A"B"C" is homothetic to ABC, and its centroid is X(3580); see Hyacinthos #16741/16782, Sep 2008.  (Randy Hutson, March 25, 2016)
</p>
