<h3 id="X3609">X(3609)&nbsp;=&nbsp;2nd MORLEY-GIBERT PERSPECTOR</h3>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;f(A,B,C)
: f(B,C,A) : f(C,A,B), where f(A,B,C) = cos A + 2 cos B cos C + 3
sin(A/3+&pi;/6) + 6 sin(B/3) sin(C/3)<br>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; (sin A)f(A,B,C) : (sin B)f(B,C,A) : (sin
C)f(C,A,B)
<p>X(3609) is the perspector of the triangle of the cusps of the
Steiner deltoid and the equilateral triangle J defined just before
X(3272). (Bernard Gibert, November 3, 2010)</p>
<p>X(3609) lies on these lines: 5,3272&nbsp;&nbsp; 20,3334&nbsp;&nbsp;
631,3335</p>
