<h3 id="X3082">X(3082)&nbsp;=&nbsp;RADICAL CENTER OF EXTERNAL MALFATTI CIRCLES</h3>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
f(A,B,C) : f(B,C,A) : f(C,A,B), where f(A,B,C) = 1/(1 - cos A/2)<br>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; (sin A)f(A,B,C) : (sin B)f(B,C,A) : (sin
C)f(C,A,B)
<p>
Let (O<sub>A</sub>) be the I-excircle of triangle IBC, where I = X(1), and define (O<sub>B</sub>) and (O<sub>C</sub>) cyclically.  Let A' be the touchpoint of (O<sub>A</sub>) and side BC, and define B' and C' cyclically.  Let A" be the insimilicenter of (O<sub>B</sub>) and (O<sub>C</sub>), and define B" and C" cyclically.  The lines A'A", B'B", C'C" concur in X(3082).  (Randy Hutson, July 11, 2019)
</p>
