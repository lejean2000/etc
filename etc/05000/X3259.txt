Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp; 1/((a - b)(cos C + cos A - 1)) + 1/((c - a)(cos A + cos B - 1)) : :

X(3259) is the center of the rectangular hyperbola that passes through the points A, B, C, and X(56). (Randy Hutson, 9/23/2011)
