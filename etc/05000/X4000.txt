<p>
As described in the preamble to X(3758), the notation T(-a, c)
represents the central triangle whose A-vertex is the point -a : c : b.
This triangle and seven others are discussed just before X(4357).
</p>
<p>
X(4000) is the center of the inellipse that is the barycentric square of the Gergonne line.  The Brianchon point (perspector) of the inellipse is X(279).  (Randy Hutson, October 15, 2018)
</p>
