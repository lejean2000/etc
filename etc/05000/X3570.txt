<h3 id="X3570">X(3570)&nbsp;=&nbsp;INTERSECTION OF LINES P(6)U(8) AND P(8)U(6)</h3>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
bc(a<sup>2</sup> - bc)/(b - c) : ca(b<sup>2</sup> - ca)/(c - a) :
ab(c<sup>2</sup> - ab)/(a - b)<br>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; (a<sup>2</sup> - bc)/(b - c) : (b<sup>2</sup>
- ca)/(c - a) : (c<sup>2</sup> - ab)/(a - b)
<p>The points given by trilinears P(6) = b : c : a and U(6) = c : a : b
are indexed at Bicentric Pairs (accessible using the Tables button at
the top of ETC), and likewise for their isogonal conjugates P(8) = 1/b
: 1/c : 1/a and U(8) = 1/c : 1/a : 1/b. The bicentric pair of lines
P(6)U(8) and P(8)U(6) concur in X(3570). Contributed by Peter Moses,
July 7, 2009..</p>
<p>If you have The Geometer's Sketchpad, you can view <a href=
"X(3570).gsp" style="font-weight: bold">X(3570)</a>.</p>

666,1026&nbsp;&nbsp; 1016,1023&nbsp;&nbsp; 1914,3253
</p>
