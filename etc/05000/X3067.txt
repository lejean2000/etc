<h3 id="X3067">X(3067)&nbsp;=&nbsp;HOFSTADTER ELLIPSE INTERSECTION</h3>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; a/(AB - AC) : b/(BC - BA) : c/(CA -CB)
<br>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; a<sup>2</sup>/(AB - AC) : b<sup>2</sup>/(BC - BA) : c<sup>2</sup>/(CA -CB)
<p>
The Hofstadter ellipse E(0) is described at X(359). The point other than A, B, and C in which E(0) meets the circumcircle is X(3067). As with X(359) and X(360), this is a transcendental center, with "exposed
angles" A, B, C in its coordinates.
</p>
