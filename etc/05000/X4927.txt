<h3 id="X4927">X(4927)&nbsp;=&nbsp;X(1639)com[T(a, c - a)]</h3>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) : f(c,a,b), where
f(a,b,c) = (b - c)(b<sup>2</sup> + c<sup>2</sup> + ab + ac - 4bc)

<p>Let A' be the pole with respect to the incircle of the A-median, and
define B' and C' cyclically. Then A', B', C' are collinear on the line
X(3667)X(3676), which is the polar of X(2) with respect to the
incircle, and X(4927) is the centroid of {A', B', C'}. (Randy Hutson,
September 29, 2014)</p>

<p>X(4927) lies on these lines:
{11, 1111}, {325, 523}, {514, 1639}, {812, 1638}, {900, 903}, {918,
4120}, {2487, 4380}, {3667, 3676}, {3700, 3776}, {3716, 4778}</p>

<p>X(4927) = isotomic conjugate of X(6079)</p>
