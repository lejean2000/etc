<h3 id="X3370">X(3370)&nbsp;=&nbsp;SEC(A - 2&pi;/5) POINT</h3>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; f(A,B,C)
: f(B,C,A) : f(C,A,B), where f(A,B,C) = sec(A - 2&pi;/5)<br>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; (sin A)f(A,B,C) : (sin B)f(B,C,A) : (sin
C)f(C,A,B)
<p>
X(3370) and related points occupy Bernard Gibert's Table 38. See the
note at X(3364).
</p>
