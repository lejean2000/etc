<h3 id="X4222">X(4222)&nbsp;=&nbsp;INTERSECTION OF LINES X(2)X(3) AND X(34)X(106)</h3>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) :
f(c,a,b), where f(a,b,c) = a(a<sup>2</sup> + ab + ac -
bc)/(b<sup>2</sup> + c<sup>2</sup> - a<sup>2</sup>)
<p>
As a point on the Euler line, X(4222) has Shinagawa coefficients ($a$F, -$a$(E + F) + abc).
</p>
<p>
The trilinear polar of X(4222) meets the line at infinity at X(4132).  (Randy Hutson, June 7, 2019)
</p>
