<h3 id="X3572">X(3572)&nbsp;=&nbsp;INTERSECTION OF LINES P(6)U(6) AND P(8)U(8)</h3>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) : f(c,a,b), where f(a,b,c) = a(b -
c)/(a<sup>2</sup> - bc)<br>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; af(a,b,c) : bf(b,c,a) : cf(c,a,b)

<p>The points given by trilinears P(6) = b : c : a and U(6) = c : a : b
are indexed at Bicentric Pairs (accessible using the Tables button at
the top of ETC), and likewise for their isogonal conjugates P(8) = 1/b
: 1/c : 1/a and U(8) = 1/c : 1/a : 1/b. The pair of central lines
P(6)U(6) and P(8)U(8) concur in X(3572). Contributed by Peter Moses,
July 7, 2009.</p>
