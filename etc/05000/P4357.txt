<p>
This section consists of points of the form X(k)com[T(f(a,b,c),g(b,c,a)], for choices of the pairs (f(a,b,c), g(b,c,a)) as indicated in the following list:
</p>

<p>
T(a, c), T(-a, c), T(a, b - c), T(a, c - b), T(a<sup>2</sup>, bc), T(-a<sup>2</sup>, bc), T(bc, bc + c<sup>2</sup>), T(-bc, bc + c<sup>2</sup>), T(bc, b<sup>2</sup> + bc), T(-bc, b<sup>2</sup> + bc) )
</p>

<p>
