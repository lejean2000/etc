<p>Trilinears for X(3221) are obtained by applying the symbolic
substitution (a,b,c) &#8594; (bc,ca,ab) to trilinear coordinates of
X(647). As a symbolic substitution, this mapping takes lines onto
lines. In particular, points on the Euler line, X(2)X(3), with
coefficients given by X(647), are mapped to the line X(6)X(194), with
coefficients given by X(3221). Symbolic substitutions are introduced in
the following article:</p>

<p><b>C. Kimberling,</b> "Symbolic substitutions in the transfigured
plane of a triangle," <i>Aequationes Mathematicae</i> 73 (2007)
156-171.</p>
