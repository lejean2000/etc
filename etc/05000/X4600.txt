<h3 id="X4600">X(4600)&nbsp;=&nbsp;TRILINEAR POLE OF THE LINE X(99)X(101)</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; 1/[(b + c)(b - c)<sup>2</sup>] : :
<p>

</p>
<p>

{99, 901}, {190, 892}, {643, 799}, {1016, 1252}, {1331, 1978}, {2396,
2398}
</p>
