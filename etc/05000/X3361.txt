<h3 id="X3361">X(3361)&nbsp;=&nbsp;INTERSECTION OF LINES X(1)X(3) AND X(7)X(1125)</h3>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; (3a + b + c)/(-a + b + c)
<p>
Let O<sub>A</sub> be the circle tangent to side BC at its midpoint
and to the circumcircle on the side ob BC opposite A. Define
O<sub>B</sub> and O<sub>C</sub> cyclically. Let A' be the
insimilicanter of O<sub>B</sub> and O<sub>C</sub>, and define B' and C'
cyclically. The lines AA', BB', CC' concur in X(3361). See the
reference at X(1001).
</p>
<p>
Let Ja, Jb, Jc be the excenters and I the incenter.  Let A' be the centroid of JbJcI, and define B' and C' cyclically.  A'B'C' is also the cross-triangle of the excentral and 2nd circumperp triangles.  A'B'C' is homothetic to the inverse-in-incircle triangle at X(3361).
(Randy Hutson, July 31 2018)
</p>
