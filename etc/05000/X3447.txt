<h3 id="X3447">X(3447)&nbsp;=&nbsp;X(523)-VERTEX CONJUGATE OF X(523)</h3>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; a/[a<sup>2</sup>vwyz - ux(bw + cv)(bz + cy)] : : , where u : v : w = x : y : z = X(523)
<br>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; a/[a<sup>6</sup> - a<sup>4</sup>(b<sup>2</sup> + c<sup>2</sup>) + a<sup>2</sup>(b<sup>4</sup> - b<sup>2</sup>c<sup>2</sup> + c<sup>4</sup>) - (b<sup>2</sup> + c<sup>2</sup>)(b<sup>2</sup> - c<sup>2</sup>)<sup>2</sup>] &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (E. Danneels)
<p>
Let E be the Euler line and T<sub>A</sub>T<sub>B</sub>T<sub>C</sub> the tangential triangle of ABC.  Let D<sub>A</sub> = E&cap;T<sub>B</sub>T<sub>C</sub>, and define D<sub>B</sub> and D<sub>C</sub> cyclically.  Let A' = D<sub>B</sub>T<sub>B</sub>&cap;D<sub>C</sub>T<sub>C</sub>, and define B' and C' cyclically.  Then A'B'C' is perspective to ABC, and the perspector is X(3447).
