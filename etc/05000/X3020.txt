<h3 id="X3020">X(3020)&nbsp;=&nbsp;4th STEVANOVIC POINT</h3>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) : f(c,a,b),
where f(a,b,c) = (b + c - a)[(b - c)(b<sup>2</sup> + c<sup>2</sup> -
bc)]<sup>2</sup>

Milorad Stevanovic (Hyacinthos, Dec. 7-8, 2004)</p>
