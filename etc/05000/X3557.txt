<p>X(3557) was submitted and called the Pappus point by Roland Bacher
(March 11, 2009); coordinates and the related point X(3558) were found
by Peter Moses (March 12, 2009).</p>

<p> X(3557) is the crosspoint (and crosssum) of the real foci of the Steiner inellipse.  (Bernard Gibert, January 4, 2015)
<p>
X(3557) is the Brocard axis intercept, other than X(1340), of the circle {{X(1340),PU(1)}}.  Also, X(3557) is the insimilicenter of the 2nd Lemoine circle and the circle {{X(371),X(372),PU(1),PU(39)}}.  (Randy Hutson, January 5, 2015)
</p>
<p>X(3557) lies on the cubics K028, K289, K704 and these lines: 3,6&nbsp;&nbsp; 4,3414&nbsp;&nbsp; 194,3413&nbsp;&nbsp; 262,6178
</p>
