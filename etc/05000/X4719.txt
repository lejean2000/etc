<h3 id="X4719">X(4719)&nbsp;=&nbsp;X(1125)com[INVERSE(nT(a, a + b))]</h3>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) : f(c,a,b), where
f(a,b,c) = a(3a + b + c)(ab + ac + b<sup>2</sup> + c<sup>2</sup>)

<p>Let O<sub>A</sub> be the circle tangent to side BC at its midpoint
and to the circumcircle on the side of BC opposite A. Define
O<sub>B</sub> and O<sub>C</sub> cyclically. Let L<sub>A</sub> be the
external tangent to circles O<sub>B</sub> and O<sub>C</sub> that is the
farther of the two from O<sub>A</sub>. Define L<sub>B</sub> and
L<sub>C</sub> cyclically. Let A' = L<sub>B</sub>&cap;L<sub>C</sub>, and
define B' and C' cyclically. The lines A'A'', B'B'', C'C'' concur in
X(4719). See the reference at X(1001).</p>
