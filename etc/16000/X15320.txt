<h3 id="X15320">X(15320)&nbsp;=&nbsp; ISOGONAL CONJUGATE OF X(4184)  </h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; (a^2+(b-c)*(a+b))*(a^2-(b-c)*( a+c))*(b+c) : :
<b><br>
X(15320) = 7*X(3090) - 5*X(3462)
</b>
<p>
Let A'B'C' be the intouch triangle.  Let La be the reflection of BC in line B'C', and define Lb, Lc cyclically.  Let A" = Lb/\Lc, B" = Lc/\La, C" = La/\Lb.  The lines AA", BB", CC" concur in X(15320). (Randy Hutson, December 2, 2017)
</p>
<p>
See Antreas Hatzipolakis and César Lozada, <a href="http://www.hyacinthos.epizy.com/message.php?msg=26802"> <b>Hyacinthos 26802</b></a>.
</p>
