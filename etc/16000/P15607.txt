<li><span class="bold">Problem 1:</span>
<p>
<i>Let T<sub>1</sub> be the medial triangle and T<sub>2</sub> be the pedal triangle of any point P inside &Delta;ABC. Prove that the side-triangle of T<sub>1</sub> and T<sub>2</sub> is perspective to T<sub>2</sub> and that the center of perspective lies on the nine-points-circle.</i>
</p>
<p>
If P = u : v : w (trilinears) and T<sub>2</sub> is the pedal triangle of P, then the perspector Q(P) is:
<br>
<br>&emsp;&emsp; Q(P) = b*c*((b^2-c^2)*u+a*(b*v-c*w))*(c*(-c^2+a^2+b^2)*v-b*(-b^2+c^2+a^2)*w) : : (trilinears)
</p>

The perspector Q(P) is the same point on the nine-points-circle for every point on the line X(3)P and it will be denoted here as the <span class="bold">(7-1)-TCCT image of the line X(3)P</span>.

<li><span class="bold">Problem 4:</span>
<p>
<i>Prove that if T<sub>1</sub> and T<sub>2</sub> are distinct circumcevian triangles, then the vertex-triangle of T<sub>1</sub> and T<sub>2</sub> is perspective to &Delta;ABC.</i>
</p>
<p>
If U = u : v : w and X = x : y : z (both trilinears) and T<sub>1</sub>, T<sub>2</sub> are the circumcevian triangles of U and X, respectively, then the perspector Q(U,X) is:
<br>
<br>&emsp;&emsp; Q(U,X) = a/(a^2*v*w*y*z-u*x*(b*z+c*y)*(c*v+b*w)) : : (trilinears)
</p>
<p>
The perspector Q(U,X) will be denoted here as the <span class="bold">(7-4)-TCCT perspector of U and X</span>; it is also the vertex conjugate of U and X.
</p>
<p>
The appearance of (i, j, k) in the following partial list means that Q(X(i), X(j))=X(k):<br>
(1, 2, 3415), (1, 3, 84), (1, 4, 3417), (1, 6, 2163), (1, 7, 3418), (1, 8, 15617), (1, 9, 3420), (1, 10, 15618), (2, 3, 3424), (2, 4, 3425), (2, 6, 1383), (3, 4, 4), (3, 5, 15619), (3, 6, 3426), (3, 7, 3427), (3, 8, 10309), (3, 10, 3429), (4, 5, 15620), (4, 6, 3431)

<li><span class="bold">Problem 18: </span>
<p>
<i>Prove that the unary cofactor triangle of the intangents triangle is perspective to the anticevian triangle of each point on the circumcircle.</i>
</p>
<p>

<br>
<br>&emsp;&emsp; Q(P) = 2*a*(2*a-b-c)*v*w+a*(-a+b+c)*u^2+((4*b-c)*a-2*b^2-b*c+c^2)*w*u+((4*c-b)*a+b^2-2*c^2-b*c)*u*v : : (trilinears)

This perspector will be denoted here as the <span class="bold">(7-18)-TCCT perspector of P</span>.
