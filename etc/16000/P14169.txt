Le Viet An equilateral triangles: X(14169)-X(14188)
</p>
<img src="rightri.svg" style="height:30px" alt="rightri">

<div class="preamble-sect">

<p>
This preamble and centers X(14169)-X(14188) were contributed by CÃ©sar Eliud Lozada, August 30, 2017.
</p>
<p>
Let ABC be a triangle and BCA', CAB', ABC' equilateral triangles erected out/in - wardly of ABC. Let B<sub>c</sub>, C<sub>b</sub> be the circumcenters of CC'B and BB'C, respectively, and build C<sub>a</sub>, A<sub>c</sub>, A<sub>b</sub> and B<sub>a</sub> cyclically. Denote the circumcenters of B<sub>c</sub>C<sub>b</sub>A, C<sub>a</sub>A<sub>c</sub>B, A<sub>b</sub>B<sub>a</sub>C as O<sub>a</sub>, O<sub>b</sub>, O<sub>c</sub>, respectively. Then, in each case, the triangle O<sub>a</sub>O<sub>b</sub>O<sub>c</sub> is equilateral. (See: <a href="http://www.hyacinthos.epizy.com/message.php?msg=26551" target="blank">Hyacinthos 26551</a>).
</p>
<p>
For BCA', CAB', ABC' built outwards ABC, the triangle  O<sub>a</sub>O<sub>b</sub>O<sub>c</sub> will be referred here as the <b>outer-Le Viet An triangle</b>. <br>
This triangle has sidelength O<sub>b</sub>O<sub>c</sub> = 4*S^2*R*|(SW+sqrt(3)*S)/((sqrt (3)*SA+S)*(sqrt(3)*SB+S)*(sqrt (3)*SC+S))|.
<br>
O<sub>a</sub> has coordinates: O<sub>a</sub> = (SW+sqrt(3)*S)*a : (SB-SC)*b : (SC-SB)*c  (trilinears)
</p>
<p>
For BCA', CAB', ABC' built inwards ABC, the triangle  O<sub>a</sub>O<sub>b</sub>O<sub>c</sub> will be referred here as the <b>inner-Le Viet An triangle</b>. <br>
This triangle has sidelength O<sub>b</sub>O<sub>c</sub> = 4*S^2*R*|(SW-sqrt(3)*S)/((sqrt (3)*SA-S)*(sqrt(3)*SB-S)*(sqrt (3)*SC-S))|
<br>
O<sub>a</sub> has coordinates: O<sub>a</sub> = (SW-sqrt(3)*S)*a : (SB-SC)*b : (SC-SB)*c  (trilinears)
</p>
<p>
Both triangles are perspective to the anti-orthocentroidal triangle.
</p>
<p>
The outer-Le Viet An triangle is orthologic to these triangles: 1st Ehrmann, inner-Le Viet An and inner-Napoleon. It is also parallelogic to these triangles: inner-Le Viet An and outer-Napoleon.
</p>
<p>
The inner-Le Viet An triangle is orthologic to these triangles: 1st Ehrmann, outer-Le Viet An, 1st Morley, 2nd Morley, 3rd Morley, outer-Napoleon, Roussel, Stammler. It is also parallelogic to these triangles: 1st Ehrmann, outer-Le Viet An, 1st Morley, 2nd Morley, 3rd Morley, outer-Napoleon, Roussel, Stammler.
</p>
<p>
When constructing the outer- and the inner- Le Viet An triangles, the Euler lines of B<sub>c</sub>C<sub>b</sub>A, C<sub>a</sub>A<sub>c</sub>B, A<sub>b</sub>B<sub>a</sub>C are concurrent at X(14185) and X(14187), respectively.
</p>
