<h3 id="X14166">X(14166)&nbsp;=&nbsp;PERSPECTOR OF THESE TRIANGLES: 1st MORLEY ADJUNCT AND ROUSSEL</h3>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; 4*x*y*z*(b*c*(4*x*y*z+1)+(b^2+c^2)*x+a*(b*y+c*z))-4*b*c*y^2*z^2+2*a^2*x*(y^2+z^2)-a*((-8*x^2*y*z+2*y*z-x)*(y*c+b*z)+a*y*z)  :  :  <br>
where x=cos(A/3), y=cos(B/3), z=cos(C/3)<p>
Contributed by César Lozada, Aug. 26, 2017.
</p>
<p>
Let A'B'C' be the 1st Morley triangle. Let Ma be the line through A parallel to B'C', and define Mb and Mc cyclically. Let A" = Mb&cap;Mc, B" = Mc&cap;Ma, C" = Ma&cap;Mb.  Triangle A"B"C" is perspective to the 1st adjunct Morley triangle and homothetic to the Roussel triangle at X(14166).(Randy Hutson, November 2, 2017)
</p>
<p>
X(14166) lies on these lines: {1135,10259}, {3604,8067}, {8011,11228}
</p>
