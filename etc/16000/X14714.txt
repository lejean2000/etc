<h3 id="X14714">X(14714)&nbsp;=&nbsp;CENTER OF THE {ABC, INTANGENTS}-CIRCUMCONIC</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; a^2*(b-c)^2*(a^4-(b+c)*a^3-(b^2-b*c+c^2)*a^2+(b+c)*(b^2+c^2)*a-b*c*(b-c)^2)*(-a+b+c)^2  :  :
<p>
The {ABC,intangents}-circumconic is the isogonal conjugate of the Soddy line.  Let P and U be the circumcircle intercepts of the Soddy line.  Then X(14714) is the crosssum of P and U. (Randy Hutson, November 2, 2017)
</p>
<p>
Let A'B'C' be the orthic triangle.  Let La be the Soddy line of triangle AB'C', and define Lb and Lc cyclically.  Let A" = Lb&cap;Lc, and define B" and C" cyclically.  Triangle A"B"C" is inversely similar to ABC, with similicenter X(14714). (Randy Hutson, July 31 2018)
</p>
