<h3 id="X15330">X(15330)&nbsp;=&nbsp; MIDPOINT OF X(2) AND X(1658) </h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp;  (37*R^2-10*SW)*S^2-3*(5*R^2-2* SW)*SB*SC : :
<b><br>
X(15330) = X(4) - 4*X(12010)
</b>
<p>
As a point on the Euler line, X(15330) has Shinagawa coefficients (-3*E-40*F, 9*E+24*F).
</p>
<p>
See Antreas Hatzipolakis and César Lozada, <a href="http://www.hyacinthos.epizy.com/message.php?msg=26813"> <b>Hyacinthos 26813</b></a>.
</p>
<p>

</p>
<p>
