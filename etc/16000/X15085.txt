<h3 id="X15085">X(15085)&nbsp;=&nbsp;ANTI-ALTIMEDIAL JOHNSON CIRCLES RADICAL CENTER</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; a^2(a^2 - b^2 - c^2)(a^12 - a^8(7b^4 + b^2c^2 + 7c^4) + a^6(8b^6 + 5b^4c^2 + 5b^2c^4 + 8c^6) + a^4(b^4 - 5b^2c^2 + c^4)(3b^4 - 2b^2c^2 + 3c^4) - a^2(b - c)^2(b + c)^2(b^2 + c^2)(8b^4 - 15b^2c^2 + 8c^4) + (b^2 - c^2)^4(3b^4 + 2b^2c^2 + 3c^4)) : :
