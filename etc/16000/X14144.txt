<h3 id="X14144">X(14144)&nbsp;=&nbsp; DUAL OF X(5613)</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; Sqrt(3) (S^2 SA + SB SC SW) + S (3 SA^2 + 6 SB SC - 5 S^2) : : &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (Peter Moses, August 27, 2017)
<b><br>
X(14144) = 4 X(619) - X(11122), X(622) - 4 X(11132) </b> &nbsp;&nbsp; (Peter Moses)
<p>
Let BA'C be the equilateral triangle with side BC and A' on the side of BC that does not include A.  Let La be the line through A' parallel to B'C', and define Lb and Lc cyclically.  Let A'' = Lb&cap;Lc, and define B''and C'' cyclically.  The Euler lines of BA"C, CB''A, AC''B concur in X(14144).  The acute angle between each pair of these Euler lines is &pi;/6.  See Dao Thanh Oai, <a href="http://www.adgeom.epizy.com/message.php?msg=4032">

<b>ADGEOM 4033</b></a>.  See also X(5617) and X(14145).
