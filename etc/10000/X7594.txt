<h3 id="X7594">X(7594)&nbsp;=&nbsp; PERSPECTOR OF ABC AND 1st PAMFILOS-ZHOU TRIANGLE</h3>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; S<sub>A</sub>/(a<sup>2</sup>S + bcS<sub>&omega;</sub>) : :
<p></p>
The Pamfilos-Zhou A-rectangle R<sub>A</sub> = AA<sub>B</sub>A<sub>A</sub>A<sub>C</sub> is the rectangle of maximal area such that A is a vertex of R<sub>A</sub>, B lies on the line A<sub>A</sub>A<sub>C</sub>, and C lies on the line A<sub>A</sub>A<sub>B</sub>.  Trilinears for the vertices, found by César Lozada, are as follows:

<p>
A = 1 : 0 : 0
<br>
A<sub>A</sub> = -(S<sup>2</sup> + S<sub>B</sub>S<sub>C</sub>)/a : cS + bS<sub>B</sub> : bS + cS<sub>C</sub>
<br>
A<sub>B</sub> = -(cS<sub>C</sub> - bS)/a : S + bc : -S<sub>A</sub>
<br>
A<sub>C</sub> = -(bS<sub>B</sub> - cS)/a : -S<sub>A</sub> :  S + bc
</p>
<p>
The Pamfilos-Zhou B- and C-rectangles, R<sub>B</sub> and R<sub>C</sub>, are defined cyclically.
</p>
<p>
Let A' = B<sub>C</sub>B<sub>A</sub>&cap;C<sub>A</sub>C<sub>B</sub>, and define B' and C' cyclically.  The 1st Pamfilos-Zhou triangle, A'B'C', is perspective to ABC, and the perspector is X(7594).
</p>
<p>
Let A'' = C<sub>A</sub>A<sub>C</sub>&cap;A<sub>B</sub>B<sub>A</sub>, and define B'' and C'' cyclically.  The 2nd Pamfilos-Zhou triangle, A''B''C'', is perspective to ABC, and the perspector is X(7595).
</p>
<p>
See Paris Pamfilos, Li Zhou, César Lozada, Randy Hutson, <a href="http://www.adgeom.epizy.com/message.php?msg=2497
">ADGEOM 2497</a> (May 2015)
</p>
<p>
Trilinears for the A-vertex of the 1st Pamfilos-Zhou triangle:
<br>
A' = a^6+b*c*(a^4-4*S*(b+c)*a+(b^2-c^2)^2)-(b^2+c^2)*(b+c)^2*a^2 :  <br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; -(a^2-b^2+c^2)*(a^3*b+b*(b^2+c^2)*a+2*c^2*S) : <br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; -(a^2+b^2-c^2)*(a^3*c+c*(b^2+c^2)*a+2*b^2*S)
</p>
<p>
Trilinears for the A-vertex of the 2nd Pamfilos-Zhou triangle:
<br>
A" = -2*(b+c)*S+(a-b-c)*((b+c)*a+(b-c)^2):  <br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (2*(a-c)*a*S-(a-b-c)*(a^2*c+(b-c)*(b^2+a*b+c^2)))/b :  <br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (2*(a-b)*a*S-(a-c-b)*(a^2*b+(c-b)*(c^2+a*c+b^2)))/c
</p>
<p>
X(7594) lies on these lines:  {1, 3103}, {43, 7347}, {1659, 7146}
</p>
