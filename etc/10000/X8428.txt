<h3 id="X8428">X(8428)&nbsp;=&nbsp;X(23)-CEVA CONJUGATE OF X(25)</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; a^2 (a^2+b^2-c^2) (a^2-b^2+c^2) (a^12-2 a^10 b^2-a^8 b^4+4 a^6 b^6-a^4 b^8-2 a^2 b^10+b^12-2 a^10 c^2+3 a^8 b^2 c^2-a^6 b^4 c^2-3 a^4 b^6 c^2+3 a^2 b^8 c^2-a^8 c^4-a^6 b^2 c^4+4 a^4 b^4 c^4-a^2 b^6 c^4-b^8 c^4+4 a^6 c^6-3 a^4 b^2 c^6-a^2 b^4 c^6-a^4 c^8+3 a^2 b^2 c^8-b^4 c^8-2 a^2 c^10+c^12) : :
<p>
X(8428) and X(2079) are the points of intersection of the tangential circle and the <b>Moses-Parry circle</b>, denoted by MP and defined here as the circle tangent to the Parry circle and which has center X(2492).  MP passes through X(i) for these i:  6, 111, 112, 115, 187, 1560, 2079, 3569, 5000, 5001, 5523, 5913, 6032, 8105, 8106, 8426, 8427.  MP is mentioned briefly in TCCT, p. 228.  (Based on notes from Peter Moses, November 2, 2015)
</p>
<p>
MP is orthogonal to each circle in the coaxal system of the circumcircle and nine-point circle.<br>
MP is orthogonal to circumcircle at X(111) and X(112).<br>
MP is orthogonal to nine-point circle at X(115) and X(1560)<br>
MP is orthogonal to orthocentroidal circle at X(8426) and X(8427).<br>
MP is orthogonal to the tangential circle at X(2079) and X(8428)<br>
MP is orthogonal to the Moses circle at X(115)<br>
MP meets the Hutson-Parry circle at X(111) and X(6032)<br>
MP meets the Brocard circle at X(6) and X(8429)<br>
MP has radius = a^3 b^3 c^3 J^2 / (2 (a^2-b^2) (b^2-c^2) (c^2-a^2) (a^2+b^2+c^2)), where J = |OH|/R <br>
MP has A-power = b^2 c^2 (a^2-b^2-c^2) (2 a^2-b^2-c^2) / (2 (a^2-b^2) (a^2-c^2) (a^2+b^2+c^2))<br>
</p>
