<p class="textcenter">
This preamble and centers X(8856)-X(8950) were contributed by CÃ©sar Eliud Lozada, December 11, 2015.
</p>

<p>
Let ABC be a triangle and let P<sub>1</sub> = p<sub>1</sub> : q<sub>1</sub> : r<sub>1</sub>, P<sub>2</sub> = p<sub>2</sub> : q<sub>2</sub> : r<sub>2</sub>, P<sub>3</sub> = p<sub>3</sub> : q<sub>3</sub> : r<sub>3</sub> (barycentrics) be three non-collinear points,  none on a sideline of ABC. The locus of a point M such that the anticevian triangle of M and the triangle T = P<sub>1</sub>P<sub>2</sub>P<sub>3</sub> are perspective, with perspector denoted by Z(M), is given by
</p>
<p class="textcenter">
F(x,y,z) = (r<sub>3</sub>&delta;<sub>32</sub>y<sup>2</sup>-&delta;<sub>23</sub>q<sub>2</sub>z<sup>2</sup>)x+(&delta;<sub>31</sub>r<sub>3</sub>x<sup>2</sup>-&delta;<sub>13</sub>p<sub>1</sub>z<sup>2</sup>)y+(&delta;<sub>21</sub>q<sub>2</sub>x<sup>2</sup>-&delta;<sub>12</sub>p<sub>1</sub>y<sup>2</sup>)z+(p<sub>2</sub>q<sub>3</sub>r<sub>1</sub>-p<sub>3</sub>q<sub>1</sub>r<sub>2</sub>)xyz = 0 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (1)
</p>
<p>
where &delta;<sub>ij</sub> is the (i, j)-minor of the vertex matrix of the triangle T.</p>
<p>If T and ABC are perspective we have p<sub>2</sub>q<sub>3</sub>r<sub>1</sub> - p<sub>3</sub>q<sub>1</sub>r<sub>2</sub> = 0 and, if the equation (1) is non-degenerate, it represents a pivotal cubic with pole &Omega;(T) and pivot P(T) given by:</p>

<p class="textcenter">
&Omega;(T) = p<sub>1</sub>&delta;<sub>12</sub>&delta;<sub>13</sub> &nbsp;&nbsp;:&nbsp;&nbsp; q<sub>2</sub>&delta;<sub>21</sub>&delta;<sub>13</sub> &nbsp;&nbsp;:&nbsp;&nbsp; r<sub>3</sub>&delta;<sub>31</sub>&delta;<sub>12</sub>
<br>
<br>
P(T) = &delta;<sub>13</sub>&delta;<sub>32</sub> &nbsp;&nbsp;:&nbsp;&nbsp; -&delta;<sub>13</sub>&delta;<sub>31</sub> &nbsp;&nbsp;:&nbsp;&nbsp; &delta;<sub>31</sub>&delta;<sub>12</sub>
</p>
<p>This section deals with the poles and pivots of these cubics and the perspectors Z(M) for most of the centers M lying on them. In the following table, each column contains:</p>
<ul>
<li>1st column: triangle T</li>
<li>2nd column: barycentrics equation of the locus for perspectivity</li>
<li>3rd column: <a href="https://bernard-gibert.pagesperso-orange.fr/ctc.html">Gibert's catalogue</a> index</li>
<li>4th column: pole of the cubic</li>
<li>5th column: pivot of the cubic</li>
<li>6th column: pairs of centers {M,Z(M)}</li>
<li>7th column: locus of Z(M)</li>
</ul>
