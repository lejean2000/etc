<h3 id="X8158">X(8158)&nbsp;=&nbsp; CENTER OF THE APOLLONIAN CIRCLE OF THE EXTERNAL MIXTILINEAR CIRCLES</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; a^2 (a^5-a^4 b-2 a^3 b^2+2 a^2 b^3+a b^4-b^5-a^4 c+2 a^3 b c-8 a^2 b^2 c-2 a b^3 c+9 b^4 c-2 a^3 c^2-8 a^2 b c^2+18 a b^2 c^2-8 b^3 c^2+2 a^2 c^3-2 a b c^3-8 b^2 c^3+a c^4+9 b c^4-c^5) : :
<b><br>
X(8158) = 3X(5603) - 2X(5763) = 4R*X(1) - (r + 4R)*X(3)
</b>
<p>
Let (Aem) denote the Apollonian circle of the external mixtilinear circles.  The touch point of (Aem) and the A-mixtilinear external circle has barycentrics a (a^2-2 a b-3 b^2-2 a c+6 b c-3 c^2) : 2 b^2 (a+b-c) : 2 c^2 (a-b+c)}.  (Peter Moses, October 4, 2015)  See X(6767).
<p></p>

<p>
The vertices of the <b>9th mixtilinear triangle</b> are the touchpoints of the outer Apollonian circle of the mixtilinear incircles, with A-vertex, A', given by these trilinears:
</p>
<p>
A' = a^2 - 2*a*b - 3*b^2 - 2*a*c + 6*b*c - 3*c^2 : 2*b*(a + b - c) : 2*c*(a - b + c)  (Dan Reznik and Peter Moses, September 10, 2021).
</p>
