Perspectors of pairs of triangles:  X(8372)-X(8424)
</p>
<img src="rightri.svg" style="height:30px" alt="rightri">

<div class="preamble-sect">

<p>
Central triangles A'B'C' and A''B''C'' are <b>perspective</b> if the lines A'A'', B'B'', C'C'' concur.  The point of concurrence is their <b>perspector</b> (or center of perspective).  If A'B'C' and A''B''C'' are not only perspective, but homothetic, their perspector is their <b>homothetic center</b>.  Centers X(8372)-X(8424) are perspectors, including homothetic centers.
