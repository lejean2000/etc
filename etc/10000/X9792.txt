<h3 id="X9792">X(9792)&nbsp;=&nbsp;ENDO-HOMOTHETIC CENTER OF THESE TRIANGLES:  2ND CONWAY AND 1ST SHARYGIN</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; (SB+SC)*(S^2+SA*SB)*(S^2+SA*SC)*(S^2+SW*(4*R^2-SW)+2*SB*SC) : :
<p>

</p>
