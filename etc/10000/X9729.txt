Let A'B'C' be the midheight triangle of ABC and let B<sub>A</sub> and C<sub>A</sub> be the orthogonal projections of B' and C' on BC, respectively. Build A<sub>B</sub>, C<sub>B</sub>, A<sub>C</sub> and B<sub>C</sub> similarly.
