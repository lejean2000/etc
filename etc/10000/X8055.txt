<h3 id="X8055">X(8055)&nbsp;=&nbsp;   ISOTOMIC CONJUGATE OF X(8051)</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp;  (b + c - a)(a^2 + b^2 + c^2 + 2ab + 2ac - 6bc) : :

<p>

</p>
