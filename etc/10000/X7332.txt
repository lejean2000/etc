<h3 id="X7332">X(7332)&nbsp;=&nbsp;PERSPECTOR OF ABC AND THE EXTRA-TRIANGLE OF X(5949)</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; (b - c)<sup>2</sup> / [a(a<sup>2</sup> - b<sup>2</sup> + 3bc - c<sup>2</sup>) + (b + c)(a - b + c)(a + b - c)] : :

<p>
Let A'B'C' be the Feuerbach triangle.  Let L<sub>A</sub> be the line through A' parallel to BC, and define L<sub>B</sub> and L<sub>C</sub> cyclically.  Let A" = L<sub>B</sub>&cap;\L<sub>C</sub>, and define B" and C" cyclically.  Triangle A"B"C" is homothetic to ABC, and the center of homothety is X(7332).  (Randy Hutson, April 11, 2015)
</p>
