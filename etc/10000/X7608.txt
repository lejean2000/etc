Let M<sub>A</sub>M<sub>B</sub>M<sub>C</sub> be the McCay triangle, defined at X(7606).  Let A' be the reflection of M<sub>A</sub> in line BC, and define B' and C' cyclically.  Then the lines AA', BB', CC" concur in X(7608).  Also, X(7608) is the trilinear product A'*B'*C'.
