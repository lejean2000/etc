<h3 id="X9463">X(9463)&nbsp;=&nbsp; ISOGONAL CONJUGATE OF  X(9462)</h3>
Barycentrics a^2(2a^2b^2 + 2a^2c^2 - b^2c^2) : :
<p></p>
Let A'B'C' be the circumsymmedial triangle.  Let Ma be the line through A parallel to B'C', and define Mb, Mc cyclically.
Let A" = Mb/\Mc, B" = Mc/\Ma, C" = Ma/\Mb.  The triangle A"B"C" is homothetic to A'B'C' at X(9463).  (Randy Hutson, January 26, 2016)
