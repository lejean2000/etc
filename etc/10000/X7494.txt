<p>
As a point on the Euler line, X(7494) has Shinagawa coefficients (-E - 2 F, S<sub>&omega;</sub>).
</p>
<p>
Let DEF = medial triangle and D'E'F' = circummedial triangle.  Let &Gamma; = circumcircle, and let Ab be the point, other than E', in which the line E'F intersects &Gamma;. Define Bc and Ca cyclically.  Let Ac be the point, other than F', in which the line F'E intersects &Gamma;. Let Oa be the center of the conic tangent to the five lines BC, AE', AF', Bab, CAc, and define Ob and Oc cyclically.  The finite fixed point of the affine transformation that carries ABC onto OaObOC is X(7494). (Angel Montesdeoca, May 3, 2020)
</p>
