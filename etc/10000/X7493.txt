<h3 id="X7493">X(7493)&nbsp;=&nbsp; {X(2),X(23)}-HARMONIC CONJUGATE OF X(4)</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; 3a^6 + b^6 + c^6 - (a^4 + b^2c^2)(b^2 + c^2) - 3a^2(b^4 + c^4) + 2a^2b^2c^2 : :

<p>
As a point on the Euler line, X(7493) has Shinagawa coefficients (-E - 4 F, 2 S<sub>&omega;</sub>).
</p>
<p>
Let L<sub>A</sub> be the polar of X(4) wrt the circle centered at A and passing through X(2), and define L<sub>B</sub> and L<sub>C</sub> cyclically.  Let A' =  L<sub>B</sub>&cap;L<sub>C</sub>, and define B' and C' cyclically.  Then A'B'C' is homothetic to the anticomplementary triangle, and the center of homothety is X(7493).  The orthocenter of A'B'C' is X(376).   (Note: the orthocenter is the perspector of every circle centered at a vertex of ABC.)  (Randy Hutson, April 15, 2015)
</p>
