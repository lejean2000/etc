<h3 id="X7595">X(7595)&nbsp;=&nbsp; PERSPECTOR OF ABC AND 2nd PAMFILOS-ZHOU TRIANGLE</h3>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; 1/[a<sup>2</sup>S + aS(S<sub>A</sub> - S)] : :
<p></p>
The Pamfilos-Zhou A-rectangle R<sub>A</sub> = AA<sub>B</sub>A<sub>A</sub>A<sub>C</sub> is the rectangle of maximal area such that A is a vertex of R<sub>A</sub>, B lies on the line A<sub>A</sub>A<sub>C</sub>, and C lies on the line A<sub>A</sub>A<sub>B</sub>.  Let A'' = C<sub>A</sub>A<sub>C</sub>&cap;A<sub>B</sub>B<sub>A</sub>, and define B'' and C'' cyclically.  The 2nd Pamfilos-Zhou triangle, A''B''C'', is perspective to ABC, and the perspector is X(7595).  See X(7594).

<p>
X(7595) lies on the Feuerbach hyperbola and these lines:  {1, 7596}, {8, 637}
