<h3 id="X8105">X(8105)&nbsp;=&nbsp;  X(2)-CEVA CONJUGATE OF X(1313) </h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; (b^2-c^2) (2 a^4-a^2 b^2-b^4-a^2 c^2+2 b^2 c^2-c^4 + a^2 (a^2-b^2-c^2) J) : : , where J = |OH|/R (as at X(1113)
<p>
The trilinear polar of X(8105) meets the line at infinity at X(512).
</p>
<p>
Let MaMbMc = medial triangle and HaHbHc = orthic triangle. Let A' = reflection of A in X(3), and let A'' be the point, other than A', where the line A'Ma meets the circumcircle.  Define B'' and C'' cyclically.  The points D=BC&cap;HbHc, E=CA&cap;HcHa, F=AB&cap;HaHb, D'=B"C"&cap;MbMc, E'=C"A"&cap;McMa, F'=A"B"&cap;MaMb lie on orthic axis (common perspectrix of ABC and HaHbHc, MaMbMc and A"B"C" ).  The fixed points of the projectivity that maps D, E, F onto D', E', F', respectively, are X(8105) and X(8106).  (Angel Montesdeoca, May 17, 2019)
</p>
