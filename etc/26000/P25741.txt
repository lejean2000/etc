<div class="preamble-sect">
<p>
Extending the preamble just before X(24537), let m(X) denote the (A,B,C,X(2); A',B',C',X(2)) collineation image of X = x : y : z, where A'B'C' = Gemini triangle 31, as in centers X(25741)-X(25767).  Then
</p>
<p>
m(X) = b c (b ^2 - a c)(c^2 - a b) x + b^2 (a^2 - b c)(c^2 - a b) y + c^2 (a^2 - b c) (b^2 - a c) z : :
</p>
<p>
A point X lies on the Euler line if and only if m(X) also lies on the Euler line. (Clark Kimberling, October 26, 2018)
