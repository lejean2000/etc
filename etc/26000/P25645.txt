<p>
Extending the preamble just before X(24537), let m(X) denote the (A,B,C,X(2); A',B',C',X(2)) collineation image of X = x : y : z, where A'B'C' = Gemini triangle 27, as in centers X(25645)-X(25689).  Then
</p>
<p>
m(X) = (a + b) (a + c)(a - b - c) x + b (b + c) (a + b) y + c (b + c) (a + c) z : :
</p>
<p>
The point m(X) is on the Euler line if and only if the point X is on the Euler line. (Clark Kimberling, October 24, 2018)
