<p>
Extending the preamble just before X(24537), let m(X) denote the (A,B,C,X(2); A',B',C',X(2)) collineation image of X = x : y : z, where A'B'C' = Gemini triangle 22, as in centers X(25474)-X(25484).  Then
</p>
<p>
m(X) = (a + b + c)(a + 2b + c)(a + b + 2 c) x - b (2 a + b + c)(a + b + 2 c) y - c (2 a + b + c)(a + 2 b + c) z  : :
</p>
<p>
A point X lies on the Euler line if and only m(X) lies on the Euler line. (Clark Kimberling, October 19, 2018)
