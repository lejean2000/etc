Points with 1st barcentric b<sup>3</sup> + k a b c + c<sup>3</sup>:  X(25957)-X(25961)

<div class="preamble-sect">
<p>
For each real number k, let P(k) = a (a^2 - b c) ((a^2 - b c) (a b^3 + a c^3 - 2 b^2 c^2) - b c (a c - b^2) (a b - c^2) k) : : .  Then the point (A,B,C,X(2); A',B',C',X(2)) collineation image of P(k), where A'B'C' = Gemini triangle 31, is the point with barycentrics b^3 + k a b c + c^3 : : .  Both P(k) and Q(k) are on the line X(2)X(31).  (Peter Moses, October 28, 2018)
<p>
