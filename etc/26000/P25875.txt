<div class="preamble-sect">
<p>
Extending the preamble just before X(24537), let m(X) denote the (A,B,C,X(2); A',B',C',X(2)) collineation image of X = x : y : z, where A'B'C' = Gemini triangle 35, as in centers X(25875)-X(25929).  Then
</p>
<p>
m(X) = (a - b - c)(a^2 - b^2 - c^2) x + 2 a c (a - b + c) y + 2 a b (a - c + b) z : :
</p>
<p>
A point X lies on the Euler line if and only if m(X) also lies on the Euler line. (Clark Kimberling, October 28, 2018)
