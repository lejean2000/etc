<p>
Extending the preamble just before X(24537), let m(X) denote the (A,B,C,X(2); A',B',C',X(2)) collineation image of X = x : y : z, where A'B'C' = Gemini triangle 20, as in centers X(25386)-X(25400).  Then
</p>
<p>
m(X) = -2 (b + c) (2 a + 2 b - c)(2a - b + 2 c) x + b (a - 2 b - 2 c)(2 a + 2 b - c) y + c (a - 2 b - 2 c) (2 a + 2c - b) z  : :
</p>
<p>
and m(X) is on the Euler line if and only if X is on the Euler line. (Clark Kimberling, October 15, 2018)
