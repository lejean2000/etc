<h3 id="X16459">X(16459)&nbsp;=&nbsp; PERSPECTOR OF ABC AND 5th FERMAT-DAO EQUILATERAL TRIANGLE</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; (SB+SC)*(5*S^2+sqrt(3)*(SB+SW)*S-3*SA*SC)*(5*S^2+sqrt(3)*(SC+SW)*S-3*SA*SB) : :  &nbsp;&nbsp; (César Lozada, March 14, 2018)
<p>
Let P be a point in the plane of a triangle ABC. Let Ba and Ca be points on BC such that the triangle PBaCa is equilateral.  Define the pairs {Cb, Ab} and {Ab, Bc}cyclically. Let A&prime; be the point other than P that lies on the circles (PAcBc) and (PCbAb), and define B&prime; and C&prime; cyclically.  If P = X(13) or P = X(14) then the triangle A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is equilateral.  (Dao Thanh Oai, March 7, 2018).  The converse is true; i.e., if A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is equilateral, then P = X(13) or P = X(14).  (Dao Thanh Oai, March 14, 2018)
</p>
<p>
If P = X(13), then A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is the <b>5th Fermat-Dao equilateral triangle.</b> If P = X(14), then A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is the <b>6th Fermat-Dao equilateral triangle.</b>  See also X(16247) and X(16267).
</p>
<p>
Barycentrics for the A-vertex of the 5th Fermat-Dao equilateral triangle:
</p>
<p>
A&prime; = ((7 SA + 4 SW)*S^2 + 5 sqrt(3) S (S^2 + SA^2) + 3 SA^3)/(S + sqrt(3) SA)^2 :<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;         (sqrt(3)(SA + SB) + 2 S)(SC + SA)/(S + sqrt(3) SB) : <br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;         (sqrt(3)(SC + SA) + 2 S)(SA + SB)/(S + sqrt(3) SC).
</p>
<p>
Barycentrics for the A-vertex of the 6th Fermat-Dao equilateral triangle:
</p>
<p>
A&prime; = ((7 SA + 4 SW)*S^2 - 5 sqrt(3) S (S^2 + SA^2) + 3 SA^3)/(- S + sqrt(3) SA)^2 :<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;         (sqrt(3)(SA + SB) - 2 S)(SC + SA)/(- S + sqrt(3) SB) : <br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;         (sqrt(3)(SC + SA) - 2 S)(SA + SB)/(- S + sqrt(3) SC).
</p>
<p>
(These barycentrics and centers X(16461)-X(16466) contributed by César Lozada, March 14, 2018)
</p>
