<h3 id="X16247">X(16247)&nbsp;=&nbsp; PERSPECTOR OF ABC AND 1ST FERMAT-DAO EQUILATERAL TRIANGLE</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; ((3*R^2*(18*R^2+3*SA-4*SW)-2*SA^2+2*SB*SC+2*SW^2)*S^2+sqrt(3)*(-2*(6*R^2-SW)*S^2+R^2*(54*R^2*SA-21*SA^2+12*SB*SC+SW^2)+2*SA^2*SW)*S-(9*R^2-2*SW)*SB*SC*SW)*(SB+SC) : :
&nbsp;&nbsp; (César Lozada, February 25, 2018)
<p>
In the plane of a triangle ABC, let F = X(13) or F = X(14), and let
</p>
<p>
O<sub>AB</sub> = circumcircle of AFB;<br>
O<sub>AC</sub> = circumcircle of AFC;<br>
L<sub>A</sub> = line through F parallel to BC;<br>
A<sub>C</sub> = the point, other than F, in L<sub>A</sub>&cap;O<sub>AB</sub>;<br>
A<sub>B</sub> = the point, other than F, in L<sub>A</sub>&cap;O<sub>AC</sub>;<br>
Define B<sub>A</sub> and C<sub>B</sub> cyclically;<br>
Define B<sub>C</sub> and C<sub>A</sub> cyclically;<br>
Let A&prime; = C<sub>B</sub>A<sub>B</sub>&cap;A<sub>C</sub>B<sub>C</sub>, and define B&prime; and C&prime; cyclically.
</p>
<p>
<b>Theorem (Thanh Oai Dao):  The triangle A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is equilateral and perspective to ABC.</b>
</p>
<p>
If F = X(13), the the triangle A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is the <b>1st Fermat-Dao triangle</b>, and the perspector is X(16247).  If F = X(14), the the triangle A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is the <b>2nd Fermat-Dao triangle</b>, and the perspector is X(16248).  Ffor other Fermat-Dao equilateral triangles, see X(16267), X(16459), X(395), and X(16536).
</p>
