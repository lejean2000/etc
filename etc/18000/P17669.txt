<div class="preamble-sect">

<p>
Consider a triangle center X having first barycentric of the form
</p>
<p>
k(1) a^4 + k(2) a^3 (b + c) + k(3) a^2 (b^2 + c^2) + k(4) a^2 b c + k(5) a (b^3 + c^3) + k(6) a(b^2 c + b c^2) + k(7) (b^4 + c^4) + k(8) (b^3 c + b c^3) + k(9) b^2 c^2,
</p>
<p>
where k(i) is in {-3,-2,-1,0,1,2,3} for k = 1, . . . , 9.
Such a point X is here named the <b>MOSES (k(1), k(2), k(3), k(4), k(5), k(6), k(7), k(8), k(9)) POINT.</b>  Barycentrics for X(17669)-X(17698), all on the Euler line, contributed by Peter Moses, April 14, 2018.
</p>
<p>
Extending the coding system introduced in the preamble just before X(17227), we refer to the 9-tuple (k(1),k(2),...,k(9)) as the <b>Moses code</b> for X.
<p>
