<img src="rightri.svg" style="height:30px" alt="rightri">

<p>
This preamble and centers X(17410)-X(17436) were contributed by CÃ©sar Eliud Lozada, April 10, 2018.
</p>
<p><i>
Let ABC and A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; be two not homothetic triangles. Let A&Prime;B&Prime;C&Prime; be any other triangle perspective to ABC and homothetic to A&prime;B&prime;C&prime;. Then the perspectors of ABC and A&Prime;B&Prime;C&Prime; lie on a fixed circumconic of ABC. (Dao Thanh Oai, March 17, 2018)</i>. (A proof of this theorem is given in <a href="Homo-Perspeconics.pdf">Homo-Perspeconics</a>.)
</p>
<p>
This circumconic is here named the <b>homo-perspeconic of A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; to ABC</b>. It is obvious that if two triangles are homothetic then their homo-perspeconics to ABC are the same.
</p>
<p>
The following lists show triangles grouped by their homo-perspeconics to ABC:
</p>
<ul>
<li>The homo-perspeconic of  the following triangles to ABC is the circumcircle:
<div style="margin-left:50px; margin-right:50px;">
1st Parry, 2nd Parry</div>
<br>
</li>
<li>The homo-perspeconic of  the following triangles to ABC is the Feuerbach hyperbola:
<div style="margin-left:50px; margin-right:50px;">
Ascella, Atik, 1st circumperp, 2nd circumperp, inner-Conway, Conway, 2nd Conway, 3rd Conway, 3rd Euler, 4th Euler, excenters-midpoints, excenters-reflections, excentral, extouch, 2nd extouch, Fuhrmann, 2nd Fuhrmann, inner-Garcia, Garcia-reflection, hexyl, Honsberger, Hutson extouch, inner-Hutson, Hutson intouch, outer-Hutson, incircle-circles, intouch, inverse-in-incircle, 6th mixtilinear, 2nd Pamfilos-Zhou, 1st Schiffler, 2nd Schiffler, 1st Sharygin, tangential-midarc, 2nd tangential-midarc, Yff central</div>
<br>
</li>
<li>The homo-perspeconic of  the following triangles to ABC is the Kiepert hyperbola:
<div style="margin-left:50px; margin-right:50px;">
anti-Artzt, 1st anti-Brocard, 6th anti-Brocard, anti-McCay, Artzt, 1st Brocard, 6th Brocard, inner-Fermat, outer-Fermat, 3rd Fermat-Dao, 4th Fermat-Dao, 7th Fermat-Dao, 8th Fermat-Dao, 11th Fermat-Dao, 12th Fermat-Dao, 15th Fermat-Dao, 16th Fermat-Dao, 1st isodynamic-Dao, 2nd isodynamic-Dao, 1st Lemoine-Dao, 2nd Lemoine-Dao, McCay, inner-Napoleon, outer-Napoleon, 1st Neuberg, 2nd Neuberg, 1st tri-squares-central, 2nd tri-squares-central, 1st tri-squares, 2nd tri-squares, 3rd tri-squares, 4th tri-squares, inner-Vecten, outer-Vecten</div>
<br>
</li>

<div style="margin-left:50px; margin-right:50px;">

</li>
<li>The homo-perspeconic of  the following triangles to ABC is the Morley conic: (see <a href="ETCPart9.html#X16271">X16271</a>)
<div style="margin-left:50px; margin-right:50px;">

</li>
</ul>
