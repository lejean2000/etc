<p>
k (a+b-c) (a-b+c) (a+b+c) + j a S = j s X[1] + 2 k (r + 4 R) X[7] = (4 k - j) (r + 4 R - 2 s) X[175] + (4 k + j) (r + 4 R + 2 s) X[176], and

<p>
({X(1),X(7)}-harmonic conjugate of k (a+b-c) (a-b+c) (a+b+c) + j a S : : ) = k (a+b-c) (a-b+c) (a+b+c) - j a S : : &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (Peter Moses, April 20, 2018)

<p>
For real numbers j and k, not both zero, let X = a^2 (j (-a^2 + b^2 + c^2) + 4 k S) : : . Then
</p>
<p>
k (a+b-c) (a-b+c) (a+b+c) + j a S = X-of-intouch-triangle. (Peter Moses, April 21, 2018)
</p>
<p>
Simiilarly, let U = a^2 ((j + 3 k) (-a^2 + b^2 + c^2) - (j + 4 k) S) and V = a^2 ((j - 3 k) (-a^2 + b^2 + c^2) - (j - 4 k) S).  Then
</p>
<p>
k (a+b-c) (a-b+c) (a+b+c) + j a S = U-of-outer-Soddy-triangle = V-of-inner-Soddy-triangle. (Peter Moses, April 21, 2018)
</p>
