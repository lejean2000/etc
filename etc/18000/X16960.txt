<p>
The triangles A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; and A&Prime;B&Prime;C&Prime; are here named the <b>15th Fermat-Dao equilateral triangle</b> and the <b>16th Fermat-Dao equilateral triangle</b>, respectively. Their respective centers are X(13) and X(14), with sidelengths a&prime;=4*S/sqrt(SW+sqrt(3)*S) and a&Prime;=4*S/sqrt(SW-sqrt(3)*S). The 1st vertices of each have barycentric coordinates:

<br>&emsp;&emsp;A&prime; = (sqrt(3)*(3*S^2+SB*SC)+5*(SB+SC)*S)/(SA-sqrt(3)*S) : sqrt(3)*SC+S : sqrt(3)*SB+S
<br>
<br>&emsp;&emsp;A&Prime; = (sqrt(3)*(3*S^2+SB*SC)-5*(SB+SC)*S)/(SA+sqrt(3)*S) : sqrt(3)*SC-S : sqrt(3)*SB-S
</p>
