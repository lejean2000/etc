<h3 id="X6221">X(6221)&nbsp;=&nbsp; PERSPECTOR OF LUCAS CENTRAL TRIANGLE AND LUCAS TANGENTS TRIANGLE</h3>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 3 cos A + 2 sin A : 3 cos B + 2 sin B : 3 cos C + 2 sin C
<br>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; a(3S<sub>A</sub> + 2S) : b(3S<sub>B</sub> + 2S) : c(3S<sub>C</sub> + 2S)
<br>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) : f(c,a,b), where f(a,b,c) =  a*(3*b^2+3*c^2-3*a^2+4*S)
<br>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; g(a,b,c) : g(b,c,a) : g(c,a,b), where g(a,b,c) = a^2 (3 a^2-3 b^2-3 c^2-4 S)
<br>
<b>
X(6221) = L<sub>A</sub>/R<sub>A</sub> + L<sub>B</sub>/R<sub>B</sub> + L<sub>C</sub>/R<sub>C</sub>, where L<sub>A</sub>, L<sub>B</sub>, L<sub>C</sub> are the centers of the Lucas circles, and R<sub>A</sub>, R<sub>B</sub>, R<sub>C</sub> their radii.  (Randy Hutson, January 29, 2015)
</b>

<p>
Let A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; be the Lucas central triangle.  Let A&Prime; be the trilinear pole, wrt A&prime;B&prime;C&prime;, of BC; define B&Prime;, C&Prime; cyclically.  The lines A&prime;A&Prime;, B&prime;B&Prime;, C&prime;C&prime; concur in X(6221).  Let A&Prime;B&Prime;C: be the Lucas tangents triangle.  Let A* be the trilinear pole, wrt A&Prime;B&Prime;C&Prime;, of line BC; define B*, C* cyclically.  The lines A&Prime;A*, B&Prime;B*, C&Prime;C* concur in X(6221).  The following triangles are perspective from X(6221):  circumsymmedial triangle, Lucas inner triangle, and Lucas(2) central triangle.  (Randy Hutson, January 29, 2015)
</p>
