<h3 id="X6153">X(6153)&nbsp;=&nbsp;MIDPOINT OF X(52) AND X(2888)</h3>
Trlinears &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; f(A,B,C) : f(B,C,A) : f(C,A,B), where f(A,B,C) = [cos 3A + 4 sin<sup>2</sup>A cos(B - C)][1 + 2 cos A cos(B - C)]
<br>
<b>
X(6153) = 3X(51) - X(195)</b>

<p>
X(6153) is the orthocenter of the pedal triangle of X(5); see X(6154).  (Antreas Hatzipolakis and C&eacute;sar Lozada, December 17, 2014; see
<a href="http://www.hyacinthos.epizy.com/message.php?msg=22907" class="bold">22907</a>
</p>
<p>
See also Antreas Hatzipolakis and Angel Montesdeoca,
<a href="http://www.hyacinthos.epizy.com/message.php?msg=25814"> <b>Hyacinthos 25814</b></a>.
</p>
<p>
X(6153) lies on these lines:  {51,195}, {52,2888}, {54,5462}, {539,973}, {546,1154}, {1209,1216}
</p>
<p>
X(6153) = midpoint of X(i) and X(j) for these {i,j}:  {52,2888}, {1209,6152}<br>
X(6153) = reflection of X(i) in X(j) for these (i,j);  (54,5462), (1216,1209)<br>
</p>
