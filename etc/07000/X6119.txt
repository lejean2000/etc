<p>
See X(6118) for a construction of the 2nd Dao-Vecten circle and some of its properties.
<p>
The 2nd Dao-Vecten circle is the nine-point circle of the inner Vecten triangle.  Let A&Prime;B&Prime;C&Prime; be the inner Vecten triangle.  Also, let (O*<sub>A</sub>) be the circle centered at A&Prime; and tangent to BC at M<sub>A</sub>, and define (O*<sub>B</sub>) and (O*<sub>C</sub>)  cyclically.  Then X(6119) is the radical center of (O*<sub>A</sub>), (O*<sub>B</sub>), (O*<sub>C</sub>).  (Randy Hutson, January 29, 2015)
</p>
