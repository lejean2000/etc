Let U, V, W be the congruent squares described at X(371). Let
L<sub>A</sub> be the extended diagonal of U that does not contain
X(371), and define L<sub>B</sub> and L<sub>C</sub> cyclically. Let A&prime; =
L<sub>B</sub>&cap;L<sub>C</sub>, and define B&prime; and C&prime; cyclically. The
triangle A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is homothetic to the orthic triangle, and the center of
homothety is X(5412). Also, A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is homothetic to the tangential
triangle at X(6), to the intangents triangle at X(2066), and to the
extangents triangle at X(5415); A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is named the 1st Kenmotu diagonals triangle at X(31).
(Randy Hutson, February 9, 2013)
