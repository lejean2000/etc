<h3 id="X5574">X(5574)&nbsp;=&nbsp; PERSPECTOR OF MEDIAL AND ANTLIA TRIANGLES</h3>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; a(b + c - a)<sup>3</sup>(a<sup>2</sup> + 3b<sup>2</sup> + 3c<sup>2</sup> - 6bc)

<p>Let A&prime; be the center of the inverse-in-A-excircle of the incircle,
and define B&prime; and C&prime; cyclically. The triangle with vertices A,&prime; B, C&prime;
is introduced here as the Antlia triangle. A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is a central triangle
of type 2. The following properties were communicated by Peter Moses,
November 10, 2013:</p>

<p>Barycentric coordinates for vertices:<br>
A&prime; = a[a<sup>2</sup> + 3(b - c)<sup>2</sup>] : - b[3a<sup>2</sup> + (b
- c)<sup>2</sup>] : - c[3a<sup>2</sup> + (b - c)<sup>2</sup>]<br>
B&prime; = - a[3b<sup>2</sup> + (c - a)<sup>2</sup>] : b[b<sup>2</sup> + 3(c
- a)<sup>2</sup>] : - c[3b<sup>2</sup> + (c - a)<sup>2</sup>]<br>
C&prime; = - a[3c<sup>2</sup> + (a - b)<sup>2</sup>] : - b[3c<sup>2</sup> +
(a - b)<sup>2</sup>] : c[c<sup>2</sup> + 3(a - b)<sup>2</sup>]</p>

<p>The triangle A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is perspective to the following triangles, with
perspector X(1): ABC, excentral, incentral, mid-arc, mixtilinear, 2nd circumperp.</p>
