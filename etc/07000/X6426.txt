<h3 id="X6426">X(6426)&nbsp;=&nbsp;PERSPECTOR OF SYMMEDIAL TRIANGLE AND LUCAS(-1) INNER TRIANGLE</h3>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp;  4 cos A - 3 sin A : :
<br>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp;  a(4S<sub>A</sub> - 3S) : :

<p>
Let &Gamma; be the circumcircle and O(A) the A-Lucas(-1) circle.  Let K<sub>A</sub> = 1 if O(A) is internally tangent to &Gamma;, and let K<sub>A</sub> = -1 if O(A) is externally tangent to &Gamma;.  Define K<sub>B</sub> and K<sub>C</sub> cyclically.  Let L<sub>A</sub> and R<sub>A</sub> be the center and radius of O(A), and define L<sub>B</sub> and L<sub>C</sub>, and also R<sub>B</sub> and R<sub>C</sub>, cyclically.  Let L* be the center of the Lucas(-1) radical circle and R* its radius.  Then X(6426) = (K<sub>A</sub>/R<sub>A</sub>)*L<sub>A</sub> + (K<sub>B</sub>/R<sub>B</sub>)*L<sub>B</sub> + (K<sub>C</sub>/R<sub>C</sub>)*L<sub>C</sub> - L*/R* - X(3)/R; see also X(6449).  (Randy Hutson, February 3, 2015)
</p>
<p>
X(6426) lies on these lines: {3,6}, {485,632}, {486,3627}, {1132,3069}
</p>
<p>
X(6426) = perspector of ABC and Lucas(-1) inner tangential triangle<br>
X(6426) = perspector of Lucas(-1) tangents triangle and 2nd Lucas(-1) secondary tangents triangle<br>
X(6426) = radical center of Lucas(-3/2) circles<br>
X(6426) = {X(i),X(j)}-harmonic conjugate of X(k) for these (i,j,k):  (3,6,6425), (372,1152,6), (1151,3312,6)
</p>
