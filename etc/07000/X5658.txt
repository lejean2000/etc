<p>

A<sub>A</sub>A<sub>B</sub>A<sub>C</sub> be the orthic triangle of the

A<sub>A</sub>B<sub>A</sub>C<sub>A</sub>, let B&prime; be the centroid of
A<sub>B</sub>B<sub>B</sub>C<sub>B</sub>, and let C&prime; be the centroid of

homothetic to ABC with center of homothety X(7308), and X(5658) = X(84)-of-A&prime;B&prime;C&prime;. (Randy Hutson, July
7, 2014)</p>
