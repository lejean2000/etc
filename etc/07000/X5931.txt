<h3 id="X5931">X(5931)&nbsp;=&nbsp; ISOTOMIC CONJUGATE OF X(5930)</h3>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) : f(c,a,b), where f(a,b,c) = (b + c - a)(c + a)(a + b)/(3a^4 - b^4 - c^4 - 2a^2b^2 - 2a^2c^2 + 2b^2c^2)
<p>
X(5931) is the trilinear pole of the perspectrix of these three triangles: 3rd extouch, anticevian of X(7), and extraversion of X(7). (Randy Hutson, July 11, 2014) See X(5927).
</p>
<p>
X(5931) lies on these lines: {20,64}, {75,1895}
</p>
<p>
X(5931) = isotomic conjugate of X(5930)<br>
</p>
