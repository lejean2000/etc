X(5642)), intersect in four points: X(2), X(110), X(5638), X(5639). Of
the latter two points, X(5639) is the closer to X(2); also, X(5639) =
the intersection closer to X(2) of the Parry circle and Lemoine axis.

the Steiner inellipse; also X(5639) = intersection of the trilinear
polars of X(6), F<sub>1</sub>, and F<sub>2</sub>.
(Randy Hutson, June 16, 2014)</p>
