where f(a,b,c) = (b<sup>2</sup> - c<sup>2</sup>)(2a<sup>4</sup> -
b<sup>4</sup> - c<sup>4</sup> - a<sup>2</sup>b<sup>2</sup> -

<p>Let U be the circle (X(5617), R) and V the circle (X(5613),R), so
that U passes through X(14) and V passes through X(13), and let &Gamma;
be the circumcircle of ABC. Then X(5664) is the radical center of the
circles U, V, and &Gamma;. (Dao Thanh Oai, ADGEOM #1256, April 23,
2014)</p>
