<h3 id="X5623">X(5623)&nbsp;=&nbsp; REFLECTION OF X(13) IN
X(5618)</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; f(A,B,C) : f(B,C,A) : f(C,A,B),
where f(A,B,C) = H<sub>A</sub> (H<sub>A</sub>V<sub>A</sub> -
H<sub>B</sub>V<sub>B</sub> - H<sub>C</sub>V<sub>C</sub>), where
H<sub>A</sub> = 3<sup>1/2</sup>a<sup>2</sup>S + S<sup>2</sup> +
3S<sub>B</sub>S<sub>C</sub> and V<sub>A</sub> = (S<sup>2</sup> -
3S<sub>A</sub>S<sub>B</sub>)(S<sup>2</sup> -
3S<sub>A</sub>S<sub>C</sub>) &nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp; (Peter Moses,
June 20, 2014)

<p>Referring to the construction of X(5618), the lines
A<sub>B</sub>A<sub>C</sub>, B<sub>C</sub>B<sub>A</sub>,
C<sub>A</sub>C<sub>B</sub> concur in X(5623). &nbsp;&nbsp; (Peter
Moses, December 4, 2013)</p>

<p>X(5623) lies on the Neuberg cubic and these lines: {13,74},
{14,3440}, {16,1138}, {3065,3383}</p>

<p>X(5623) = reflection of X(13) in X(5618)<br>
X(5623) = X(30)-Ceva conjugate of X(13)</p>
