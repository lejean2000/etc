<h3 id="X5533">X(5533)&nbsp;=&nbsp; INVERSE-IN-INCIRCLE OF
X(5)</h3>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) : f(c,a,b), where
f(a,b,c) = a<sup>4</sup>(b + c - a)(b<sup>2</sup> + c<sup>2</sup> -
4bc) - 2a<sup>3</sup>(b<sup>4</sup> + c<sup>4</sup> - 3b<sup>3</sup>c -
3bc<sup>3</sup> + 5b<sup>2</sup>c<sup>2</sup>) + 2a<sup>2</sup>(b +
c)(b - c)<sup>2</sup>(b<sup>2</sup> + c<sup>2</sup> - bc) + a(b +
c)<sup>2</sup>(b - c)<sup>4</sup> - (b - c)(b<sup>2</sup> -
c<sup>2</sup>)<sup>3</sup> &nbsp;&nbsp; (C&eacute;sar Lozada, August 15, 2013)

<p>X(5533) is the Gibert-Burek-Moses concurrent circles image of
X(5534). &nbsp;&nbsp; (Randy Hutson, July 18, 2013)</p>

<p>X(5533) = inverse-in-incircle of X(5), and X(5533) =
{X(11),X(1317)}-harmonic conjugate of X(5).</p>
