<h3 id="X5650">X(5650)&nbsp;=&nbsp; REFLECTION OF X(373) IN X(2)</h3>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; a^2(b^4 + c^4 - a^2b^2 - a^2c^2 + 6b^2c^2) : :
<p>
Let U be the tangent to the Thomson-Gibert-Moses hyperbola at X(2),
and let V be the tangent at X(3); then X(5650) = U&cap;V. Also, X(5650)
is the centroid of the X(2)-Brocard triangle; see X(5642). (Randy
Hutson, June 16, 2014)</p>

<p>X(5650) = pole of the Euler line with respect to the
Thomson-Gibert-Moses hyperbola. (Peter Moses, June 17, 2014)</p>

<p>X(5650) lies on these lines: {2,51}, {3,1495}, {6,5646}, {110,5092},
{549,5642}
</p>
