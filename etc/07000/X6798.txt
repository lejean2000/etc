<h3 id="X6798">X(6798)&nbsp;=&nbsp;1st ATRIA CYCLOLOGIC CENTER</h3>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp;  (cos(2*B)+cos(2*C))/(2*cos(A)*(cos(2*(B-C))+1)+cos(B-C)+cos(3*A)) : :  &nbsp;&nbsp;(C&eacute;sar Lozada)
<p>
Let H<sub>A</sub> be the reflection of the orthocenter in BC, and define H<sub>B</sub> and H<sub>C</sub> cyclically.  Let H<sub>AB</sub> be the reflection of H<sub>A</sub> in HB, and define H<sub>BC</sub> and H<sub>CA</sub> cyclically.   Let H<sub>AC</sub> be the reflection of H<sub>A</sub> in HC, and define H<sub>BA</sub> and H<sub>CB</sub> cyclically.  Let N<sub>A</sub> be the nine-point center of triangle H<sub>A</sub>H<sub>AB</sub>H<sub>AC</sub>, and define N<sub>B</sub> and N<sub>C</sub> cyclically.  Then ABC and N<sub>A</sub>N<sub>B</sub>N<sub>C</sub> are cyclologic triangles.  The (ABC, N<sub>A</sub>N<sub>B</sub>N<sub>C</sub>)-cyclologic center is X(6798), and the (N<sub>A</sub>N<sub>B</sub>N<sub>C</sub>, ABC)-cyclologic center is X(6799).

</p>
