X(399), in connection with the work of Cosmin Pohoata on the Parry
reflection point. The circle passes through the points X(13), X(16),
X(110), X(399), X(1338), X(2381), X(5610), X(5611), X(5612), X(5613),
and the 2nd Pohoata-Dao-Moses circle pass through X(14), X(15), X(110),
X(399), X(1337), X(2380), X(5614), X(5615), X(5616),
X(5617).&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (Dao Thanh Oai and Peter Moses, Nov.,
2013)</p>
