<h3 id="X5544">X(5544)&nbsp;=&nbsp; HIRIART-URRUTY MINIMIZER</h3>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) : f(c,a,b),
where f(a,b,c) = a(a<sup>4</sup> - 4a<sup>2</sup>b<sup>2</sup> -
4a<sup>2</sup>c<sup>2</sup> + 3b<sup>4</sup> + 3c<sup>4</sup> -
26b<sup>2</sup>c<sup>2</sup>)<br>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; g(A,B,C) : g(B,C,A) : g(C,A,B), where g(A,B,C)
= a<sup>2</sup>(7S<sup>2</sup> + 5S<sup>2</sup><sub>A</sub> +
S<sub>B</sub>S<sub>C</sub>) &nbsp;&nbsp; (Peter Moses, August 30, 2013)

<p>Let X be a point in the plane of a triangle ABC, and let A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; be
the pedal triangle of X. The sum |AX|<sup>2</sup> + |BX|<sup>2</sup> +
|CX|<sup>2</sup> + |A&prime;X|<sup>2</sup> + |B&prime;X|<sup>2</sup> +
|C&prime;X|<sup>2</sup> is minimized by X = X(5544). &nbsp;&nbsp;
(Jean-Baptiste Hiriart-Urruty; Toulouse, France; August 30, 2013)</p>

<p>The minimal value is (4S<sup>4</sup> - 12PT +
15S<sup>2</sup>T<sup>2</sup>)/(20S<sup>2</sup>T - 18P), where P =
S<sub>A</sub>S<sub>B</sub>S<sub>C</sub> and T = S<sub>A</sub> +
S<sub>B</sub> + S<sub>C</sub>.&nbsp;&nbsp; (Peter Moses, August 30,
2013)</p>

<p>X(5544) is the only point whose polar conic in the Thomson
cubic
<a href="https://bernard-gibert.pagesperso-orange.fr/Exemples/k002.html" style=
"font-weight: bold">(K002)</a> is a circle. (Bernard Gibert, June 22,
2014)</p>
