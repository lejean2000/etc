<h3 id="X5691">X(5691)&nbsp;=&nbsp; DE LONGCHAMPS POINT OF OUTER GARCIA TRIANGLE</h3>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; r - 4 R cos B cos C : :
<br>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; 3 a^4- a^3 (b + c) - a^2 (b - c)^2  + a (b - c)^2 (b + c) - 2 (b^2 - c^2)^2 : : &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (Angel Montesdeoca, January 21, 2015)
<b><br>
X(5691) = X(1) - 2 X(4)
</b>
<p>
The outer Garcia triangle is defined at X(5587).
</p>
<p>
Let I = X(1) and O = X(3).  Let A&Prime; be the reflection of I in line AO and let I<sub>A</sub> be the reflection of A&Prime; in line AI.  Define I<sub>B</sub> and I<sub>C</sub> cyclically.  Then ABC and I<sub>A</sub>I<sub>B</sub> I<sub>C</sub> are orthologic triangles, and X(5691) is the ABC-orthology center of I<sub>A</sub>I<sub>B</sub> I<sub>C</sub>.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (Angel Montesdeoca, January 21, 2015)
</p>
<p>
Let Ja, Jb, Jc be the excenters and I the incenter.  Let A&prime; be the centroid of JbJcI, and define B&prime; and C&prime; cyclically.  A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is also the cross-triangle of the excentral and 2nd circumperp triangles.  A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is homothetic to the 4th Euler triangle at X(5691).
(Randy Hutson, July 31 2018)
</p>
