<h3 id="X5737">X(5737)&nbsp;=&nbsp; INTERSECTION OF LINES X(2)X(6) AND X(3)X(10)</h3>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) : f(c,a,b),
where f(a,b,c) = a^3 - a^2*b - 2*a*b^2 - a^2*c - 2*a*b*c - 2*b^2*c -
2*a*c^2 - 2*b*c^2
<p>
Let A4B4C4 be the 4th Conway triangle.  Let A&prime; be the barycentric product B4*C4, and define B&prime; and C&prime; cyclically.  The lines AA&prime;, BB&prime;, CC&prime; concur in X(5737).  (Randy Hutson, December 10, 2016)
</p>
<p>
Let A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; be the tangential triangle, wrt the excentral triangle, of the excentral-hexyl ellipse.  A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is homothetic to the polar triangle of the Spieker circle at X(5737).  (Randy Hutson, August 19, 2019)
</p>
