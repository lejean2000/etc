POINT</h3>

where f(a,b,c) = a<sup>5</sup>b<sup>2</sup> +
a<sup>5</sup>c<sup>2</sup> - 2a<sup>5</sup>b<sup>2</sup>c<sup>2</sup> +

<b>X(5482) = 3*X(549) - X(970)<br>
X(5482) = (R - 2r)*X(140) - R*X(143)  </b>

<p>Let ABC be a triangle and let A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; be the cevian triangle of the
incenter, X(1). Let R be the radical center of the circles (A&prime;, |A&prime;B|,
{B&prime;,|B&prime;C|), (C&prime;, |C&prime;A|), and let S be the radical center of the circles
(A&prime;,|A&prime;C|), (B&prime;,|B&prime;A|), (C&prime;,|C&prime;B|). X(5482) is the midpoint of the
segment RS. &nbsp;&nbsp; (Antreas Hatzipolakis, May 4, 2013)</p>

<p>X(5482) is the {X(3),X(1764)}-harmonic conjugate of
X(3579)&nbsp;&nbsp; (Peter Moses, May 13, 2013)</p>
