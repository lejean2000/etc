<h3 id="X5403">X(5403)&nbsp;=&nbsp;SEC(A - &omega;/2) POINT</h3>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;f(A,B,C)
: f(B,C,A) : f(C,A,B), where f(A,B,C) = sec(A - &omega;/2)<br>
<p>
Let A&prime; be the apex of the isosceles triangle BA&prime;C constructed outward on BC such that &ang;A&prime;BC = &ang;A&prime;CB = &omega;/2.  Define B&prime; and C&prime; cyclically.  Let Ha be the orthocenter of BA&prime;C, and define Hb and Hc cyclically.  The lines AHa, BHb, CHc concur in X(5403).  (Randy Hutson, July 20, 2016)
</p>
