<h3 id="X5408">X(5408)&nbsp;=&nbsp;3rd LUCAS POLAR PERSPECTOR</h3>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; (cos A)(1 + cot A) : (cos B)(1 + cot B) : (cos C)(1 + cot C)
<br>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; cos A - sin A + csc A : :
<p>
Let A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; be the Lucas tangents triangle. Let L<sub>A</sub> be the
trilinear polar of A&prime;, and define L<sub>B</sub> and L<sub>C</sub>
cyclically. Let A&Prime; = L<sub>B</sub>&cap;L<sub>C</sub>, and define B&Prime;
and C&Prime; cyclically. The lines AA&Prime;, BB&Prime;, CC&Prime; concur in X(5408). The
same is true if A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is the Lucas(2) central triangle. X(5408) =
{X(3), X(394)}-harmonic conjugate of X(5409). (Randy Hutson, February
9, 2013)</p>
