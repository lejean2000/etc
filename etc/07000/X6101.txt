<h3 id="X6101">X(6101)&nbsp;=&nbsp; 18th HATZIPOLAKIS-MONTESDEOCA POINT</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) : f(c,a,b),
where f(a,b,c) = a^2 (a^6 (b^2 + c^2) - a^4 (3b^4 + 4b^2 c^2 + 3c^4) +
a^2(3b^6 + 2b^4 c^2 + 2b^2 c^4 + 3c^6) - b^8 + b^6 c^2 + b^2 c^6 - c^8)

<p>Let A<sub>B</sub> be the reflection of A in line OB, where O =
circumcenter of ABC, and define B<sub>C</sub> and C<sub>A</sub>
cyclically. Let A<sub>C</sub> be the reflection of A in line OC, and
define B<sub>A</sub> and C<sub>B</sub> cyclically. Let A&prime; be the
nine-point center of triangle AA<sub>B</sub>A<sub>C</sub>, and define
B&prime; and C&prime; cyclically. The circumcircles of the four triangles A&prime;B&prime;C&prime;,
A&prime;BC, AB&prime;C, ABC&prime; concur in X(6101). (Also, the circumcircles of the
four triangles ABC, AB&prime;C&prime;, A&prime;BC&prime;, A&prime;B&prime;C concur in X(930)).
See
<a href="http://www.hyacinthos.epizy.com/message.php?msg=22624"
class="bold">Hyacinthos</a> 22624, October 10, 2014.</p>
