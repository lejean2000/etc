<h3 id="X6041">X(6041)&nbsp;=&nbsp; TRIPOLAR CENTROID OF
X(1976)</h3>

where f(a,b,c) = a^2(b^2 - c^2)(2a^6 - b^6 - c^6 - 2a^4b^2 - 2a^4c^2 +
a^2b^4 + a^2c^4 + b^4c^2 + b^2c^4)&nbsp;&nbsp; (Randy Hutson, September
5, 2014)
