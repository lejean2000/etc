<h3 id="X6144">X(6144)&nbsp;=&nbsp; REFLECTION OF X(6) IN X(193)</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) : f(c,a,b), where f(a,b,c) = 2b<sup>2</sup> + 2c<sup>2</sup> - 5a<sup>2</sup>
<br>
<b>
X(6144) = 6X(2) - 7X(6)
</b>
<p>
Let A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; be the orthic triangle and A&Prime;B&Prime;C&Prime; the medial triangle of ABC.  Let A*B*C* be the mid-altitude triangle (midpoints of altitudes).  Let U be the reflection of line A&Prime;A* in A&prime;, and define V and W cyclically.  The lines U,V,W concur in X(6144).  (Antreas Hatzipolakis and Peter Moses, November 18, 2014; see
<a href="http://www.hyacinthos.epizy.com/message.php?msg=22765" class="bold">22765</a>)
</p>
