where f(a,b,c) = (-b+c) (2 a^4-a^3 b-3 a^2 b^2+a b^3+b^4-a^3 c-4 a^2 b

<p>For I = 5957, 5958, 5959, 5948, 5949, 5953, X(i) is the isogonal
conjugate of a point with respect to the Feuerbach triangle, called a
Feuerbach isogonal conjugate.</p>

<p>Follwing is the first barycentric of the Feuerbach isogonal
conjugate of an arbitrary point p : q : r. This lengthy function of
a,b,c,p,q,r (degree 11 in a,b,c and degree 2 in p,q,r) can be
cut-and-pasted into Mathematica. (Peter Moses, August 10, 2014)</p>
