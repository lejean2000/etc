<h3 id="X5530">X(5530)&nbsp;=&nbsp; HUTSON RADICAL CIRCLE POINT</h3>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) : f(c,a,b),
where f(a,b,c) = a<sup>3</sup>(b + c) + a<sup>2</sup>(3b<sup>2</sup> +
3c<sup>2</sup> + 4bc) + a(b + c)(b<sup>2</sup> + c<sup>2</sup>) -
(b<sup>2</sup> - c<sup>2</sup>)<sup>2</sup> &nbsp;&nbsp; (C&eacute;sar Lozada,
August 15, 2013)
