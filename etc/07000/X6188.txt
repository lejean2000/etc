<h3 id="X6188">X(6188)&nbsp;=&nbsp; DAO (a,b,c,R) PERSPECTOR</h3>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp;  1 /[a^8 - 4 a^6 (b^2 + c^2) + (b^2 - c^2)^2 (b^4 + 6 b^2 c^2 + c^4) + a^4 (6 b^4 - 3 b^2 c^2 + 6 c^4) + a^2 (-4 b^6 + 3 b^4 c^2 + 3 b^2 c^4 - 4 c^6)]
<p>
Let r<sub>A</sub> be a positive valued function of a,b,c, and define r<sub>B</sub> and r<sub>C</sub> cyclically.  Let (A) denote the circle with center A and radius r<sub>A</sub>, and define (B) and (C) cyclically.  Let P be the radical center of (A), (B), (C).  Let r<sub>P</sub> be a positive valued function of a,b,c, let L<sub>A</sub> be the radical axis of (A), (B), (P), and define L<sub>B</sub> and L<sub>C</sub> cyclically. Let A&prime; = L<sub>B</sub>&cap;L<sub>C</sub>, and define B&prime; and C&prime; cyclically.  Then the lines AA&prime;, BB&prime;, CC&Prime; concur in a point, D.  Moreover, the six points A, B, C, X(4), P, D lie on a hyperbola.  (Dao Thanh Oai,
<a href="http://www.adgeom.epizy.com/message.php?msg=893" class="bold">ADGEOM 893</a>, November 26, 2013)
</p>
<p>
The hyperbola is here called the Dao (r<sub>A</sub>,r<sub>B</sub>,r<sub>C</sub>,r<sub>P</sub>) hyperbola.  The triangle A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is the Dao (r<sub>A</sub>,r<sub>B</sub>,r<sub>C</sub>,r<sub>P</sub>) triangle, and the perspector D of ABC and A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is the Dao (r<sub>A</sub>,r<sub>B</sub>,r<sub>C</sub>,r<sub>P</sub>) perspector.
</p>
