<h3 id="X5594">X(5594)&nbsp;=&nbsp; PERSPECTOR OF ARA AND OUTER GREBE TRIANGLES</h3>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) : f(c,a,b),
where f(a,b,c) = a<sup>2</sup>[a<sup>6</sup> - b<sup>6</sup> -
c<sup>6</sup> + a<sup>4</sup>b<sup>2</sup> + a<sup>4</sup>c<sup>2</sup>
- a<sup>2</sup>b<sup>4</sup>- a<sup>2</sup>c<sup>4</sup> -
2a<sup>2</sup>b<sup>2</sup>c<sup>2</sup> + b<sup>4</sup>c<sup>2</sup> +
b<sup>2</sup>c<sup>4</sup> + (a<sup>2</sup> - b<sup>2</sup> +
c<sup>2</sup>)(a<sup>2</sup> + b<sup>2</sup> - c<sup>2</sup>)S]

<p>Let A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; be the tangential triangle of triangle ABC. Let A&Prime; be the
center of the A&prime;-excircle of A&prime;B&prime;C&prime;, unless this is also the
circumcircle of ABC, in which case let A&Prime; be the incenter of A&prime;B&prime;C&prime;.
Define B&Prime;, C&Prime; cyclically. The triangle A&Prime;B&Prime;C&Prime; is introduced here as
the Ara triangle, which appears in the sketch at X(5593). The vertices
of the Ara triangle are given by Peter Moses (Nov. 19, 2013):</p>

<p>A&Prime; = - a<sup>2</sup>(a<sup>2</sup> + b<sup>2</sup> + c<sup>2</sup>)
: b<sup>2</sup>(a<sup>2</sup> + b<sup>2</sup> - c<sup>2</sup>) :
c<sup>2</sup>(a<sup>2</sup> - b<sup>2</sup> + c<sup>2</sup>)<br>
B&Prime; = a<sup>2</sup>(b<sup>2</sup> - c<sup>2</sup> + a<sup>2</sup>) : -
b<sup>2</sup>(b<sup>2</sup> + c<sup>2</sup> + a<sup>2</sup>) :
c<sup>2</sup>(b<sup>2</sup> + c<sup>2</sup> - a<sup>2</sup>)<br>
C&Prime; = a<sup>2</sup>(c<sup>2</sup> + a<sup>2</sup> - b<sup>2</sup>) :
b<sup>2</sup>(c<sup>2</sup> - a<sup>2</sup> + b<sup>2</sup>) : -
c<sup>2</sup>(c<sup>2</sup> + a<sup>2</sup> + b<sup>2</sup>)</p>

<p>The Ara triangle is perspective to triangles as listed here with
perspectors: ABC, X(25); anticomplementary, X(22); Euler, X(1598);
tangential 1st circumperp, X(197). &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (Peter
Moses, Nov. 19, 2013)</p>

<p>The Ara triangle is homothetic to triangle ABC. If ABC is acute then
the Ara triangle is the excentral triangle of the tangential triangle.
(Randy Hutson, August 17, 2014)</p>
