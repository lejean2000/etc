<p>
Let A&prime;, B&prime;, C&prime; be the free vertices of the 2nd Kenmotu squares.  Let A&Prime;, B&Prime;, C&Prime; be the reflections of A&prime;, B&prime;, C&prime; in X(372).  Triangle A&Prime;B&Prime;C&Prime; is homothetic to ABC at X(6396).  The following 4 triangles are pairwise perspective:  (1) circumsymmedial, (2) Lucas(-1) tangents, (3) Lucas(-4) central, and (4) 1st Lucas secondary tangents triangles.  Their common perspector is X(6396).  (Randy Hutson, January 29, 2015)
</p>
