<p>
Let L<sub>A</sub> be the polar of X(4) wrt the circle centered at A and passing through X(6).  Define L<sub>B</sub>, L<sub>C</sub> cyclically.  (X(4) is the perspector of any circle centered at a vertex of ABC.)  Let A&prime; = L<sub>B</sub>&cap;L<sub>C</sub>, and define B&prime;, C&prime; cyclically.  The orthocenter of triangle A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is X(6776).
</p>
