<h3 id="X5455">X(5455)&nbsp;=&nbsp; 2nd MORLEY-KIRIKAMI
POINT</h3>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; f(A,B,C) : f(B,C,A) : f(C,A,B),
where f(A,B,C) = (4 sin A)g(A,B,C) + (sin B)g(B,C,A) + (sin C)g(C,A,B),
where g(A,B,C) = cos(A/3) + 2 cos(B/3) cos(C/3) &nbsp;&nbsp; (Peter
Moses, April 26, 2013)

<p>Let DEF be the 1st Morley triangle of triangle ABC. Let
L<sub>A</sub> be the line of the centroid of AEF and the centroid of
BCD, and define L<sub>B</sub> and L<sub>C</sub> cyclically. The lines
L<sub>A</sub>, L<sub>B</sub>, L<sub>C</sub> concur in
X(5455).&nbsp;&nbsp; (Seiichi Kirikami, April 26, 2013)</p>

<p>If you have The Geometer's Sketchpad, you can view
<a href="X(5455).gsp" class="bold">X(5455)</a>.</p>
