<p>The inner Garcia triangle A&Prime;B&Prime;C&Prime; is defined at X(5587); see also
X(5692).</p>

<p>Let A* be the reflection of X(5) in the perpendicular bisector of
segment BC, and define B* and C* cyclically. Triangle A*B*C* is
inversely similar to ABC, with similitude center X(3); also, A*B*C* is
perspective to ABC, with perspector X(3519), and X(5694) is the
incenter of A*B*C*.</p>
