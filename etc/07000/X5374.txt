<h3 id="X5374">X(5374)&nbsp;=&nbsp;TRILINEAR SQUARE ROOT OF X(63)</h3>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp; (cot A)<sup>1/2</sup> : (cot
B)<sup>1/2</sup> : (cot C)<sup>1/2</sup>
<p>
For any point P on segment BC of an acute triangle ABC, let Q be the
point on AB nearest to P and let R be the point on AC nearest to P. Let
A&prime; be the choice of P for which area(A&prime;QB) = area(A&prime;RC). Define B&prime; and
C&prime; cyclically. Then the lines AA&prime;, BB&prime;, CC&prime; concur in X(5374).
</p>
<p>
X(5374) is introduced in <b>Mowaffaq Hajja and Panagiotis T. Krasopoulos,</b> "Two more
triangle centers," <i>Elemente der Mathematik</i> 66 (2011)
164-174.
</p>
