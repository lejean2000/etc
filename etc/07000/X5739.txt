<h3 id="X5739">X(5739)&nbsp;=&nbsp; INTERSECTION OF LINES X(2)X(6) AND
X(4)X(8)</h3>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) : f(c,a,b),
where f(a,b,c) = -a^3 - a^2*b + a*b^2 + b^3 - a^2*c + 2*a*b*c + b^2*c +
a*c^2 + b*c^2 + c^3

<p>Let A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; be the extangents triangle, and let A<sub>B</sub> be the
touch point of the A-excircle and the line A&prime;B&prime;, and define
B<sub>C</sub> and C<sub>A</sub> cyclically. Let A<sub>C</sub> be the
touch point of the A-excircle and the line A&prime;C&prime;, and define
B<sub>A</sub> and C<sub>B</sub> cyclically. Let A&Prime; =
B<sub>C</sub>B<sub>A</sub>&cap;C<sub>A</sub>C<sub>B</sub>, and define
B&Prime; and C&Prime; cyclically. The A&Prime;B&Prime;C&Prime; is homothetic to ABC and to the
outer and inner Grebe triangles at X(6), to the medial triangle at
X(1211), and to the anticomplementary triangle at X(5739). (Randy
Hutson, July 7, 2014)
</p>
