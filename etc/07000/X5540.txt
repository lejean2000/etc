<h3 id="X5540">X(5540)&nbsp;=&nbsp; GIBERT-BUREK-MOSES CONCURRENT CIRCLES IMAGE OF X(101)</h3>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; a<sup>3</sup> - a<sup>2</sup>(b + c) + a(b<sup>2</sup> + c<sup>2</sup> - bc) - (b + c)(b - c)<sup>2</sup> : :
&nbsp;&nbsp; (Randy Hutson, July 18, 2013)
<p>
Gibert-Burek-Moses concurrent circles images are introduced at X(5524).
</p>
<p>
X(5540) is the point of concurrence of the reflections of the line X(1)X(6) in the sides of the excentral triangle. &nbsp;&nbsp; (Randy Hutson, August 14, 2013)
</p>
<p>
The 1st and 2nd Montesdeoca bisector triangles are inversely similar to the excentral triangle.  Let s<sub>1</sub> and s<sub>2</sub> be the similarity mappings.  Then there is a unique point X such that s<sub>1</sub>(X) = s<sub>2</sub>(X), and X = X(5540).
See
<a href="http://amontes.webs.ull.es/otrashtm/HGT2017.htm#HG220417"><b>HGT2017</b></a>
and
<a href="http://www.adgeom.epizy.com/message.php?msg=3769"><b>AdvGeom3769</b></a>
</p>
