<p>
The outer Garcia triangle is defined at X(5587). The definition is
re-stated here. Let T<sub>A</sub>T<sub>B</sub>T<sub>C</sub> be the
extouch triangle of a triangle ABC, and let L<sub>A</sub> be the line
perpendicular to line BC at T<sub>A</sub>. Of the two points on
L<sub>A</sub> at distance r from T<sub>A</sub>, let A&prime; be the one
farther from A and let A&Prime; be the closer. Define B&prime;, C&prime; and B&Prime;, C&Prime;
cyclically. Then A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is the outer Garcia triangle, and A&Prime;B&Prime;C&Prime; the
inner Garcia triangle. The outer triangle is introduced by Emmanuel
Garcia in ADGEOM #1205 (April 2, 2014), and the inner by Garcia in
ADGEOM #1212 (April 3, 2014).
