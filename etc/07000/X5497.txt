<h3 id="X5497">X(5497)&nbsp;=&nbsp; 5th HATZIPOLAKIS-MONTESDEOCA POINT</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp;  a[a^6 - a^5(b + c) - a^4(b + c)^2 + a^3(2b^3 + b^2c + bc^2 + 2c^3) - a^2(b^4 - b^3c - 3b^2c^2 - bc^3 + c^4) - a(b^5 + b^4c + bc^4 + c^5) + (b^2 - c^2)^2(b^2 + c^2)] : :
<p>
Let ABC be a triangle and let A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; be the cevian triangle of the
incenter, X(1). The circles O<sub>A</sub>, O<sub>B</sub>, O<sub>C</sub>
defined at X(5496) concur in X(5497). &nbsp;&nbsp; (Antreas
Hatzipolakis, May 29, 2013)</p>

<a href="http://amontes.webs.ull.es/otrashtm/HechosGeometricos.htm#HG290513">Hechos
Geom&eacute;tricos en el Tri&aacute;ngulo</a>.</p>
