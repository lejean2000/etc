<h3 id="X5776">X(5776)&nbsp;=&nbsp; INTERSECTION OF LINES X(3)X(9) AND
X(4)X(6)</h3>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) : f(c,a,b),
where f(a,b,c) = a*(a^7 - 2*a^6*b - a^5*b^2 + 4*a^4*b^3 - a^3*b^4 -
2*a^2*b^5 + a*b^6 - 2*a^6*c - 2*a^4*b^2*c + 2*a^2*b^4*c + 2*b^6*c -
a^5*c^2 - 2*a^4*b*c^2 + 2*a^3*b^2*c^2 - a*b^4*c^2 + 2*b^5*c^2 +
4*a^4*c^3 - 4*b^4*c^3 - a^3*c^4 + 2*a^2*b*c^4 - a*b^2*c^4 - 4*b^3*c^4 -
2*a^2*c^5 + 2*b^2*c^5 + a*c^6 + 2*b*c^6)
<p>
The B-excircle meets the sidelines of ABC in 3 points, and likewise
for the C-excircle. The 6 points lie on a conic, denoted by A*. Let A&prime;
be the center of A*, and define B&prime; and C&prime; cyclically. Then X(5776) is
the perspector of A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; and the 2nd extouch triangle (defined at
X(5927). (Randy Hutson, July 7, 2014)
</p>
<p>
A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is also the unary cofactor triangle of the intangents triangle, which is also the cevian triangle of X(1743) wrt excentral triangle. (Randy Hutson, August 29, 2018)
</p>
