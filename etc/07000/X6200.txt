<h3 id="X6200">X(6200)&nbsp;=&nbsp; PERSPECTOR OF CIRCUMSYMMEDIAL TRIANGLE AND LUCAS TANGENTS TRIANGLE</h3>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 3 cos A + sin A : 3 cos B + sin B : 3 cos C + sin C
<br>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; a(3S<sub>A</sub> + S) : b(3S<sub>B</sub> + S) : c(3S<sub>C</sub> + S)
<br>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; g(a,b,c) : g(b,c,a) : g(c,a,b), where g(a,b,c) = a^2 (3 a^2-3 b^2-3 c^2-2 S)
<p>
See C&eacute;sar Lozada,
<a href="https://docs.google.com/uc?export=download&id=0BxTBrium3DhVbGR0X0pDNEFGTzQ" class="bold">Perspective-Orthologic-Parallelogic</a>.

<p>
The circumsymmedial, Lucas tangents, and Lucas(4) central triangles are pairwise perspective, and the perspector of each pair is X(6200).  Also, X(6200) is the perspector of Lucas central triangle and circumsymmedial tangential triangle.  Let A&prime;, B&prime;, C&prime; be the free vertices of the Kenmotu squares, and let A&Prime;, B&Prime;, C&Prime; be the reflections of A&prime;, B&prime;, C&prime; in X(371).  The triangle A&Prime;B&Prime;C&Prime; is homothetic to ABC at X(6200).  (see also X(6396)).  (Randy Hutson, February 16, 2015)
</p>
<p>
Let A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; and A&Prime;B&Prime;C&Prime; be the outer and inner Vecten triangles, respectively.  Let A* be the trilinear pole, wrt A&prime;B&prime;C&prime;, of the line B&Prime;C&Prime;.  Define B* and C* cyclically.  The lines A&prime;A*, B&prime;B*, C&prime;C* concur in X(6200).    (Randy Hutson, February 16, 2015)
</p>
<p>
X(6200) is the trilinear pole, with respect to the inner Vecten triangle, of the line X(2501)X(3566), this being the perspectrix of the outer and inner Vecten triangles. (Randy Hutson, February 20, 2015)
