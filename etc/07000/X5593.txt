<h3 id="X5593">X(5593)&nbsp;=&nbsp; CENTER OF YIU CONIC OF THE TANGENTIAL TRIANGLE (IF ABC IS ACUTE)</h3>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp;
f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) : f(c,a,b), where f(a,b,c) =
a<sup>2</sup>S<sub>A</sub>(S<sub>A</sub> - S<sub>B</sub>)(S<sub>A</sub>
- S<sub>C</sub>)(g(a,b,c) - g(a,c,b)), where g(a,b,c) =
[b<sup>2</sup>S<sub>B</sub>S<sub>B</sub>(S<sup>2</sup> -
S<sub>C</sub>S<sub>C</sub>)(4S<sup>4</sup> - (S<sup>2</sup> +
S<sub>A</sub>S<sub>C</sub>)(3S<sup>2</sup> + S<sub>B</sub>S<sub>B</sub>
- 2S<sub>A</sub>S<sub>C</sub>))]

<p>The Yiu conic is presented at X(478); it passes through the 6 of the
nine touch-points of the sidelines of a triangle and the excircles of
the triangle. When the triangle is the tangential, the conic has center
X(5593).</p>

<p>Let u(a,b,c) = 4a<sup>2</sup>b<sup>4</sup>c<sup>4</sup> and v(a,b,c)
= a<sup>2</sup>(a<sup>8</sup> + b<sup>8</sup> + c<sup>8</sup> -
2a<sup>6</sup>b<sup>2</sup> - 2a<sup>6</sup>c<sup>2</sup> +
2a<sup>4</sup>b<sup>4</sup> + 2a<sup>4</sup>c<sup>4</sup> -
2a<sup>2</sup>b<sup>6</sup> - 2a<sup>2</sup>c<sup>6</sup> +
6a<sup>2</sup>b<sup>4</sup>c<sup>2</sup> +
6a<sup>2</sup>b<sup>2</sup>c<sup>4</sup> - 4b<sup>6</sup>c<sup>2</sup>
- 4b<sup>6</sup>c<sup>2</sup> + 6b<sup>4</sup>c<sup>4</sup>).</p>

<p>The Yiu conic of the tangential triangle of a triangle ABC is given
by</p>

<p>u(a,b,c)x + u(b,c,a)y + u(c,a,b) z + v(a,b,c)yz + v(b,c,a)zx +
v(c,a,b)xy = 0. (Peter Moses, Nov. 19, 2013)</p>
