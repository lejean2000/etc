<h3 id="X5404">X(5404)&nbsp;=&nbsp;CSC(A - &omega;/2) POINT</h3>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;f(A,B,C)
: f(B,C,A) : f(C,A,B), where f(A,B,C) = csc(A - &omega;/2)<br>

<p>Let B* be a point such that (angle CBB*) = &omega;/2, and let C* be
a point such that (angle CBC*) = &omega;/2. Let A&prime; = (line
BB*)&cap;(line CC*), and define B&prime; and C&prime; cyclically. The lines AA&prime;,
BB&prime;, CC&prime; concur in X(5404). (Randy Hutson, September 5, 2014)</p>

<p>X(5404) lies on the Kiepert hyperbola and these lines:

3238}, {83, 1343}, {98, 1342}, {485, 1689}, {486, 1690}, {1348,
1665}, {1349, 1664}, {2009, 3103}, {2010, 3102}
</p>
