<h3 id="X5409">X(5409)&nbsp;=&nbsp;4th LUCAS POLAR PERSPECTOR</h3>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; (cos A)(1 - cot A) : (cos B)(1 - cot B) : (cos C)(1 - cot C)
<br>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; cos A + sin A - csc A : :
<p>
Let A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; be the Lucas(-1) tangents triangle. Let L<sub>A</sub> be
the trilinear polar of A&prime;, and define L<sub>B</sub> and L<sub>C</sub>
cyclically. Let A&Prime; = L<sub>B</sub>&cap;L<sub>C</sub>, and define B&Prime;
and C&Prime; cyclically. The lines AA&Prime;, BB&Prime;, CC&Prime; concur in X(5409). The
same is true if A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is the Lucas(-2) central triangle. X(5409) =
{X(3), X(394)}-harmonic conjugate of X(5408). (Randy Hutson, February
9, 2013)</p>
