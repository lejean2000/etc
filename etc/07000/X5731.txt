<h3 id="X5731">X(5731)&nbsp;=&nbsp; INTERSECTION OF LINES X(1)X(7) AND
X(3)X(8)</h3>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) : f(c,a,b),
where f(a,b,c) = -5*a^4 + 2*a^3*b + 4*a^2*b^2 - 2*a*b^3 + b^4 + 2*a^3*c
- 4*a^2*b*c + 2*a*b^2*c + 4*a^2*c^2 + 2*a*b*c^2 - 2*b^2*c^2 - 2*a*c^3 +
c^4
<p>
X(5731) = {X(1),X(20)}-harmonic conjugate of X(962). Let A&prime; be the
antipode of the A-extouch point in the A-excircle, and define B&prime; and C&prime;
cyclically, and let A&Prime; be the antipode of the A-intouch point in the
incircle, and define B&Prime; and C&Prime; cyclically. Then X(5731) is the
centroid of {A&prime;,B&prime;,C&prime;,A&Prime;,B&Prime;,C&Prime;}. (Randy Hutson, July 7, 2014)</p>

<p>The triangle A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is here named the Hutson-extouch triangle, and
A&Prime;B&Prime;C&Prime;, the Hutson-intouch triangle - not to be confused with the
outer and inner Hutson triangles defined at X(363). Hutson established
(July 10, 2014) that A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; and A&Prime;B&Prime;C&Prime; are orthologic with orthology
center X(3555); also that A&Prime;B&Prime;C&Prime; and A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; are orthologic with
orthology center X(5920). X(5731) is the midpoint of the centroids of
A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; and A&Prime;B&Prime;C&Prime;; see X(5918) and X(5919).</p>

<p>Peter Moses (July 15, 2014) gives barycentrics for Hutson-extouch
triangle,<br>
-4a<sup>2</sup> : (a + b + c)(a + b - c) : (a + b + c)(a - b + c)<br>
(a + b + c)(b - c + a) : -4b<sup>2</sup> : (a + b + c)(b + c - a)<br>
(a + b + c)(c + a - b) : (a + b + c)(c - a + b) : -4c<sup>2</sup></p>

<p>and for the Hutson-intouch triangle,<br>
4a<sup>2</sup> : (-a + b + c)(a - b + c) : (-a + b + c)(a + b - c)<br>

(-c + a + b)(c - a + b) : (-c + a + b)(c + a - b) : 4c<sup>2</sup></p>
