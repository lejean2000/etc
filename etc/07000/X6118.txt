<h3 id="X6118">X(6118)&nbsp;=&nbsp; CENTER OF 1st DAO-VECTEN CIRCLE</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) : f(c,a,b), where f(a,b,c) = (a^2-b^2-c^2) (2 a^4-a^2 b^2+b^4-a^2 c^2-2 b^2 c^2+c^4) + 2 (6 a^4-7 a^2 b^2+7 b^4-7 a^2 c^2-10 b^2 c^2+7 c^4) S
<br>
<span class="bold">X(6118) = 3X(2) + X(485)</span>
<p>
In the plane of a triangle ABC, let A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; be the outer Vecten triangle, and let  M<sub>A</sub>M<sub>B</sub>M<sub>C</sub> be the medial triangle.  Let O<sub>A</sub> be the circumcenter of triangle B&prime;C&prime;M<sub>A</sub>, and define O<sub>B</sub> and O<sub>C</sub> cyclically.  Let O&prime;<sub>A</sub> be the point, other than A&prime;, in which the circles A&prime;B&prime;M<sub>C</sub> and C&prime;A&prime;M<sub>B</sub> meet, and define O&prime;<sub>B</sub> and O&prime;<sub>C</sub> cyclically.  The six points O<sub>A</sub>, O<sub>B</sub>, O<sub>C</sub>, O&prime;<sub>A</sub>, O&prime;<sub>B</sub>, O&prime;<sub>C</sub> lie on a circle, here named the 1st Dao-Vecten circle.  If the construction is carried out using the inner Vecten triangle for A&prime;B&prime;C&prime;, the resulting six points line on the 2nd Dao-Vecten circle.  (Thanh Oai Dao, November 4, 2014).
<p>
The line X(6118)X(6119) passes through X(575), X(3564), X(3589), X(3628), X(5449), and the radical axis of the 1st and 2nd Dao-Vecten circles passes through X(2501), X(3566), X(5203).  X(6118) = {X(3589),X(3628)}-harmonic conjugate of X(6119).  (Francisco Javier, ADGEOM, November 4-5, 2014).
<p>
The 1st Dao-Vecten circle is the nine-point circle of the outer Vecten triangle.  Also, let (O&Prime;<sub>A</sub>) be the circle centered at A&prime; and tangent to BC at M<sub>A</sub>, and define (O&Prime;<sub>B</sub>) and (O&Prime;<sub>C</sub>)  cyclically.  Then X(6118) is the radical center of (O&Prime;<sub>A</sub>), (O&Prime;<sub>B</sub>), (O&Prime;<sub>C</sub>).  (Randy Hutson, January 29, 2015)
