<h3 id="X5532">X(5532)&nbsp;=&nbsp; WOLK-FEUERBACH
POINT</h3>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) : f(c,a,b),
where f(a,b,c) = (b - c)<sup>4</sup>(b + c - a)<sup>3</sup>

<p>X(5532) is the point of intersection, other than X(11), of the three
collinear circles described at X(5531). &nbsp;&nbsp; (Barry Wolk,
Hyacinthos #21433, January 18, 2013)</p>

<p>X(5532) lies on these lines: {11,514}, {516,5183}, {1111,3323},
{1146,3022}, {2310,4041}, {3689,5199}, {4081,4163}</p>
