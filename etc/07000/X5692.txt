<p>The inner Garcia triangle A&Prime;B&Prime;C&Prime; is defined at X(5587). Another
construction of A&Prime; is as the reflection of X(1) in the perpendicular
bisector of side BC, so that |OA&Prime;| = |X(1)X(3)|, and A&Prime;, B&Prime;, C&Prime;,
and X(1) are on a circle with center X(3). (Paul Yiu, ADGEOM #1214,
April 3, 2014)</p>
