<p>Let A&prime; be the centroid of the A-altimedial triangle, and define B&prime;
and C&prime; cyclically; then X(5643) is the center of similitude of ABC and
A&prime;B&prime;C&prime;. (Randy Hutson, July 7, 2014)
</p>
<p>
X(5643) is the only finite fixed point of the affine transformation that maps a triangle ABC onto the pedal triangle of X(5).  (Angel Montesdeoca, August 19, 2016)
</p>
