<h3 id="X6178">X(6178)&nbsp;=&nbsp; CEVAPOINT OF THE IMAGINARY FOCI OF THE STEINER INELLIPSE</h3>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; f(A,B,C) : f(B,C,A) : f(C,A,B), where f(A,B,C) = 1/(e sin A - sin(A - &omega;), where e is defined at X(1340)
<br>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; g(a,b,c) : g(b,c,a) : g(c,a,b), where g(a,b,c) = a<sup>4</sup> + b<sup>4</sup> + c<sup>4</sup> - a<sup>2</sup>b<sup>2</sup> - a<sup>2</sup>c<sup>2</sup> - (b<sup>2</sup> + c<sup>2</sup>)(a<sup>4</sup> + b<sup>4</sup> + c<sup>4</sup> - a<sup>2</sup>b<sup>2</sup> - a<sup>2</sup>c<sup>2</sup> - b<sup>2</sup>c<sup>2</sup>) <sup>1/2</sup>
<p>
See also X(3558) and X(6177).  Contributed by Bernard Gibert, January 4, 2015.
</p>
