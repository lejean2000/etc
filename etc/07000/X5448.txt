A&Prime;B&Prime;C&Prime; be the circumcevian triangle of X(4) with respect to A&prime;B&prime;C&prime;. Let

(C&Prime;,|C&prime;B&Prime;|), and define R<sub>B</sub> and R<sub>C</sub> cyclically. The
lines R<sub>A</sub>, R<sub>B</sub>, R<sub>C</sub> concur in X(5448).
