<p>X(5939) = X(187)-of-circummedial triangle. Let A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; be the
triangle of which ABC is the 1st Brocard triangle; here called the 1st
anti-Brocard triangle, with circumcircle called the anti-Brocard
circle; then X(5939) = X(187)-of-A&prime;B&prime;C&prime;, and X(5939) =
inverse-in-anti-Brocard-circle of X(99). (Randy Hutson, July 18,
2014)</p>
