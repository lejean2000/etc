<h3 id="X5456">X(5456)&nbsp;=&nbsp; 3rd MORLEY-KIRIKAMI
POINT</h3>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; sin(2A/3) : sin(2B/3) : sin(2C/3)
&nbsp;&nbsp; (Peter Moses, May 14, 2013)

<p>Let DEF be the 1st Morley triangle of triangle ABC, and let
D<sub>1</sub>E<sub>1</sub>F<sub>1</sub> be the 1st Morley adjunct
triangle (defined at MathWorld). Let D<sub>2</sub> =
DD<sub>1</sub>&cap;BC, and define E<sub>2</sub> and F<sub>2</sub>
cyclically. The lines AD<sub>2</sub>, BE<sub>2</sub>, CF<sub>2</sub>
concur in X(5456). &nbsp;&nbsp; (Seiichi Kirikami, April 26, 2013)</p>

<p>Let DEF be the 1st Morley triangle. Let D' be the trilinear pole of
line EF, and define E', F' cyclically. Let D" be the trilinear pole of
line E'F', and define E", F" cyclically. The lines AD", BE", CF" concur
in X(5456). (Randy Hutson, September 29, 2014)</p>

<a href="X(5456).gsp" class="bold">X(5456)</a>.</p>
<p>X(5456) lies on these lines: {356, 3605}, {3274, 3602}</p>
