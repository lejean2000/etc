<h3 id="X5454">X(5454)&nbsp;=&nbsp; 1st MORLEY-KIRIKAMI POINT</h3>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; f(A,B,C) : f(B,C,A) : f(C,A,B), where
f(A,B,C) = ((-f(2A/3) + f(4A/3) + f(2B/3) - f(4B/3))(h(C/3 +
&pi;/6)(2f(C/3)g(A/3) + g(B/3)) + (2 + f(C/3)g(A/3)g(B/3))g(A/3 +
2&pi;/3) + (f(2A/3) - f(4A/3) - f(2C/3) + f(4C/3)(h(B/3 +
&pi;/6)(2f(B/3)g(A/3) + g(C/3)) + (2 + f(B/3)g(A/3)g(C/3))g(A/3 +
2&pi;/3)), where f = cos, g = sec, h = csc &nbsp;&nbsp; (Peter Moses,
April 26, 2013)
<br>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp; (4*a*g(A)+3*b*g(B)+3*c*g(C))*f(A)+(b*f(B)+c*f(C))*g(A) : :,
<br>
&emsp;where f(A)=cos(A/3)+2*cos(B/3)*cos(C/3) and g(A)= sec((A+2*&pi;)/3)  (C&eacute;sar Lozada, March 3, 2022)
<p>
Let DEF be the 1st Morley triangle of triangle ABC. The Newton lines
of the quadrilaterals AEDF, BFED, CDFE concur in X(5454).&nbsp;&nbsp;
(Seiichi Kirikami, April 26, 2013)</p>
