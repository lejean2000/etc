<h3 id="X5936">X(5936)&nbsp;=&nbsp; ISOTOMIC CONJUGATE OF X(3616)</h3>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; 1/(3a + b + c) : 1/(a + 3b + c) : 1/(a + b + 3c)
<p>
Let A<sub>21</sub>B<sub>21</sub>C<sub>21</sub> be the Gemini triangle 21.  Let L<sub>A</sub> be the line through A<sub>21</sub> parallel to BC, and define L<sub>B</sub> and L<sub>C</sub> cyclically.  Let A&prime;<sub>21</sub> = L<sub>B</sub>&cap;L<sub>C</sub>, and define B&prime;<sub>21</sub>, C&prime;<sub>21</sub> cyclically.  Triangle A&prime;<sub>21</sub>B&prime;<sub>21</sub>C&prime;<sub>21</sub> is homothetic to ABC at X(5936).  (Randy Hutson, November 30, 2018)
</p>
