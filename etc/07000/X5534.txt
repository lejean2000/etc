<h3 id="X5534">X(5534)&nbsp;=&nbsp; GIBERT-BUREK-MOSES CONCURRENT
CIRCLES IMAGE OF X(5533)</h3>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp; f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a)
: f(c,a,b), where f(a,b,c) = a<sup>6</sup> - 2a<sup>5</sup>(b +c) -
a<sup>4</sup>(b + c)<sup>2</sup> + 4a<sup>3</sup>(b + c)(b<sup>2</sup>
+ c<sup>2</sup>) - a<sup>2</sup>(b<sup>4</sup> + c<sup>4</sup> +
6b<sup>2</sup>c<sup>2</sup>) - 2a(b + c)(b<sup>2</sup> -
c<sup>2</sup>)<sup>2</sup> + (b + c)<sup>2</sup>(b<sup>2</sup> -
c<sup>2</sup>)<sup>2</sup> &nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp; (C&eacute;sar Lozada,
August 15, 2013)

<p>Gibert-Burek-Moses concurrent circles images are introduced at
X(5524). In the definition of X(5495), if A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is the excentral
triangle, then X(5534) is the center of the circumcircle of
O<sub>A</sub>, O<sub>B</sub>, O<sub>C</sub>. In this case, unlike that
of X(5495), the circle does not also pass through O. Also, X(5534) =
X(5)-of-3rd-antipedal-triangle-of-X(1). &nbsp;&nbsp; (Randy Hutson,
July 18, 2013)</p>
