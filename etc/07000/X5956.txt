<h3 id="X5956">X(5956)&nbsp;=&nbsp; CENTER OF OUTER HUNG
CIRCLE</h3>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) : f(c,a,b),
where f(a,b,c) = a (a^4 b^2+3 a^3 b^3+3 a^2 b^4+a b^5+3 a^3 b^2 c+3 a^2
b^3 c-a b^4 c-b^5 c+a^4 c^2+3 a^3 b c^2-2 a^2 b^2 c^2-8 a b^3 c^2-4 b^4
c^2+3 a^3 c^3+3 a^2 b c^3-8 a b^2 c^3-6 b^3 c^3+3 a^2 c^4-a b c^4-4 b^2
c^4+a c^5-b c^5) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (Peter Moses, August 9, 2014)

<p>In the configuration for X(5213), the circle externally tangent to
the circles (K<sub>A</sub>), (K<sub>B</sub>), (K<sub>C</sub>) is here
named the outer Hung circle; see X(5955) and X(5213).</p>
