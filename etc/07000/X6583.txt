<h3 id="X6583">X(6583)&nbsp;=&nbsp;HATZIPOLAKIS-LOZADA NINE-POINT IMAGE OF X(1)</h3>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 2*cos((B-C)/2)*sin(3*A/2)+2*(1-cos(A))*cos(B-C)-1 : :
<br>
<br>
X(6583) = (5R + 2r)*X(1) - (R + 2r)*X(3)

<p>
In the plane of ABC, let P be a point, and let A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; be the pedal triangle of P.  Let A<sub>B</sub> be the reflection of A in B&prime;, and define B<sub>C</sub> and C<sub>A</sub> cyclically.  Let A<sub>C</sub> be the reflection of A in C&prime;, and define B<sub>A</sub> and C<sub>B</sub> cyclically.  Let N<sub>A</sub> be the nine-point center of AA<sub>B</sub>A<sub>C</sub>, and define N<sub>B</sub> and N<sub>C</sub> cyclically.  The nine-point center of N<sub>A</sub>N<sub>B</sub>N<sub>C</sub> is here named the Hatzipolakis-Lozada Nine-Point Image of P.  See
<a href="http://www.hyacinthos.epizy.com/message.php?msg=23083" class="bold">Hyacinthos 23083</a>.  (Antreas Hatzipolakis and C&eacute;sar Lozada, February 2, 2015)
</p>
