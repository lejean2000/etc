<h3 id="X5407">X(5407)&nbsp;=&nbsp;2nd LUCAS POLAR PERSPECTOR</h3>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
(cos A)(2 - cot A) : (cos B)(2 - cot B) : (cos C)(2 - cot C)

<p>Let A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; be the Lucas(-1) central triangle. Let L<sub>A</sub> be
the trilinear polar of A&prime;, and define L<sub>B</sub> and L<sub>C</sub>
cyclically. Let A&Prime; = L<sub>B</sub>&cap;L<sub>C</sub>, and define B&Prime;
and C&Prime; cyclically. The lines AA&Prime;, BB&Prime;, CC&Prime; concur in X(5407), which
is the {X(3), X(394)}-harmonic conjugate of X(5406). (Randy Hutson,
February 9, 2013)</p>
