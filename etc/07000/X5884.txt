<h3 id="X5884">X(5884)&nbsp;=&nbsp; X(52) OF FUHRMANN TRIANGLE</h3>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) : f(c,a,b), where f(a,b,c) = a[a^5(b + c) - a^4(b^2 + c^2) - a^3(b + c)(2b^2 - 3bc + 2c^2) + a^2(b - c)^2(2b^2 + 3bc + 2c^2) + a(b - c)^2(b^3 + c^3) - (b^2 - c^2)^2(b^2 - bc + c^2)]   (Randy Hutson, January 29, 2015)

<p>Let P be a point on the circumcircle. Let A&prime; be the orthogonal
projection of P on the A-altitude, and define B&prime; and C&prime; cyclically. As
P traces the circumcircle, the locus of the incenter of A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is an
ellipse with center X(5884). (Antreas Hatzipolkis, Hyancinthos #20792,
February 6, 2012, and subsequent postings)</p>

<p>Let T be the orthic triangle of the Fuhrmann triangle (whose
vertices are the incenters of the three altimedial triangles); then
X(5884) = orthocenter of T. Let T' be the triangle whose vertices are
the orthocenters of the altimedial triangles; then X(5884) = incenter
of T'. (Randy Hutson, July 18, 2014)</p>
