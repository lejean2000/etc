<h3 id="X5410">X(5410)&nbsp;=&nbsp;5th LUCAS POLAR PERSPECTOR</h3>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
(sin A)(2 - tan A) : (sin B)(2 - tan B) : (sin C)(2 - tan C)

<p>Let A&prime; be the perspector of the A-Lucas circle, and define B&prime; and C&prime;
cyclically. (The lines AA&prime;, BB&prime;, CC&prime; concur in X(1151).) Let
L<sub>A</sub> be the polar of A&prime; with respect to the A-Lucas circle,
and define L<sub>B</sub> and L<sub>C</sub> cyclically. Let A* =
L<sub>B</sub>&cap;L<sub>C</sub>, B* = L<sub>C</sub>&cap;L<sub>A</sub>,
C* = L<sub>A</sub>&cap;L<sub>B</sub>. The lines AA*, BB*, CC* concur in
X(5410). X(5410) = {X(6), X(25)}-harmonic conjugate of X(5411). (Randy
Hutson, February 10, 2013)</p>
