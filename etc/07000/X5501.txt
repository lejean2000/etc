be the nine-point center of NBC, and define N<sub>B</sub> and
N<sub>C</sub> cyclically. The circumcenter of
N<sub>A</sub>N<sub>B</sub>N<sub>C</sub> is X(5501), which lies on the
Euler line of ABC. &nbsp;&nbsp; (Antreas Hatzipolakis, June 2,
2013)</p>
