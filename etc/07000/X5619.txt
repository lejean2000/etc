<h3 id="X5619">X(5619)&nbsp;=&nbsp; 2nd MONTESDEOCA EQUILATERAL
TRIANGLES POINT</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) :
f(c,a,b), where f(a,b,c) = 1/[(b<sup>2</sup> -
c<sup>2</sup>)(27<sup>1/2</sup>b<sup>2</sup>c<sup>2</sup>S<sub>A</sub>
- S(S<sup>2</sup> + 9S<sub>A</sub>S<sub>A</sub>))]

<p>The negative Montesdeoca equilateral triangles for a point P are
constructed as follows: in the construction of the positive Montesdeoca
equilateral triangles atX(5618), replace the rotation angles (30, -60,
-60) by (-30, 60, 60). Barycentrics for X(5619) are obtained from those
of X(5618) by replacing S by - S. &nbsp;&nbsp; (Peter Moses, November
8, 2013)</p>
