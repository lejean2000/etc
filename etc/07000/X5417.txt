<h3 id="X5417">X(5417)&nbsp;=&nbsp;PERSPECTOR OF 1st KENMOTU CIRCLE</h3>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
f(A,B,C) : f(B,C,A) : f(C,A,B), where f(A,B,C) = 1/(cos A + sin A + 2
sin B sin C)

<p>The 1st Kenmotu circle is defined at
<a href="http://mathworld.wolfram.com/KenmotuCircle.html" style=
"font-weight: bold">MathWorld</a>. Let A&prime; be the pole of line BC with
respect to the 1st Kenmotu circle, and define B&prime; and C&prime; cyclically. The
lines AA&prime;, BB&prime;, CC&prime; concur in X(5417). (Randy Hutson, February 9, 2013)</p>

<p>X(5417) lies on the conic {A, B, C, X(2), X(1173)} and these lines:
{371, 5446}, {372, 1599}, {491, 639}, {5412, 6419}, {5419, 5421}
</p>
<p>
X(5417) = isogonal conjugate of X(5418)</p>
