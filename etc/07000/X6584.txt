<h3 id="X6584">X(6584)&nbsp;=&nbsp;HATZIPOLAKIS-LOZADA-KOSNITA POINT</h3>
Trilinears &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  a/((b-c)*(b^4+a*b^3+4*b^2*c*a-2*b^2*c^2-3*a^2*b^2+4*b*c^2*a-4*b*c*a^2-a^3*b+c^3*a-c*a^3+2*a^4-3*c^2*a^2+c^4)) : :
<p>
Let I = X(1), and let N<sub>A</sub> be the nine-point center of IBC.  Define N<sub>B</sub> and N<sub>C</sub> cyclically.  Let K<sub>A</sub> = X(54)-of-IBC; i.e,, the Kosnita point of IBC, and define K<sub>B</sub> and K<sub>C</sub> cyclically.  The circumcircles of the four triangles ABC, AK<sub>B</sub>K<sub>C</sub>, BK<sub>C</sub>K<sub>A</sub>, AC<sub>A</sub>K<sub>B</sub> concur in X(6584); their radical center is X(484).  Also, K<sub>A</sub> = IBC-isogonal conjugate of N<sub>A</sub>, and the points K<sub>A</sub>, K<sub>B</sub>, K<sub>C</sub> lie on the line X(1)X(3).  See
