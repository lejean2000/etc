<h3 id="X5589">X(5589)&nbsp;=&nbsp; PERSPECTOR OF AQUILA AND INNER GREBE TRIANGLES</h3>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp; f(a,b,c) : f(b,c,a) :
f(c,a,b), where f(a,b,c) = a(a<sup>2</sup> - b<sup>2</sup> -
c<sup>2</sup> + 2ab + 2ac + S)
