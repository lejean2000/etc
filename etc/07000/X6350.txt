<h3 id="X6350">X(6350)&nbsp;=&nbsp; 2nd SODDY HOMOTHETIC CENTER</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; - 1 + sec B + sec C : - 1 + sec C + sec A : - 1 + sec A + sec B
<br>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; (a^2 - b^2 - c^2) (a^4 - 2 a^3 (b + c) + 2 a (b - c)^2 (b + c) - (b^2 - c^2)^2) : :

<p>
Let H<sub>A</sub> be the hyperbola that passes through A and has foci B and C.  H<sub>A</sub> is the A-Soddy hyperbola, as in (Paul Yiu, <I>Introduction to the Geometry of the Triangle</I>, 2002; 12.4 The Soddy hyperbolas, p. 143).  Let M<sub>A</sub> be the polar of X(3) with respect to H<sub>A</sub>.  Define M<sub>B</sub> and M<sub>C</sub> cyclically.  Let A&Prime; = M<sub>B</sub>&cap;M<sub>C</sub>, B&Prime; = M<sub>C</sub>&cap;M<sub>A</sub>, C&Prime; = M<sub>A</sub>&cap;M<sub>B</sub>.  Triangle A&Prime;B&Prime;C&Prime; is homothetic to the medial triangle, with homothetic center X(6350).  Also, triangle A&Prime;B&Prime;C&Prime; is homothetic to ABC, with homothetic center X(278).  The orthocenter of A&prime;B&prime;C&prime; is X(944).    (Randy Hutson, January 30, 2015)
</p>
