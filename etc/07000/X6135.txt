<h3 id="X6135">X(6135)&nbsp;=&nbsp; 1st HUNG-SODDY TOUCHPOINT</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; a*(a-b)*(a-c)*(a*b+S)*(a*c+S) : :
<p>
The incircle of the antipedal triangle of the inner Soddy point, X(175), of a triangle ABC is tangent to the circumcircle of ABC, and X(6135) is the touchpoint.  (Based on a problem by Tran Quang Hung, with contributions by T. Cohl ane P. Moses,
<a href="http://www.artofproblemsolving.com/Forum/viewtopic.php?t=610195&p3655162" class="bold">Art of Problem Solving: Tangent to circumcircle, October 16, 2014</a>),
</p>
<p>
X(6135) lies on these lines:  {105,1123}, {662,1307}, {4557,6136}
</p>
<p>
X(6135) = isogonal conjugate of X(6364)
</p>
