<h3 id="X5457">X(5457)&nbsp;=&nbsp; 4th MORLEY-KIRIKAMI POINT</h3>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp; f(A,B,C) : f(B,C,A) : f(C,A,B), where
f(A,B,C) = 1/(cos A + cos(A/3)) &nbsp;&nbsp; (Peter Moses, April 26,
2013)<br>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp; g(A,B,C) : g(B,C,A) : g(C,A,B), where g(A,B,C) =
(sin A/3)/sin(4A/3) &nbsp;&nbsp; (Randy Hutson, August 9, 2014)

<p>Let DEF be the 1st Morley triangle of triangle ABC, and let
D<sub>3</sub> be the reflection of D in line BC, and define
E<sub>3</sub> and F<sub>3</sub> cyclically. The lines AD<sub>3</sub>,
BE<sub>3</sub>, CF<sub>3</sub> concur in X(5457). &nbsp;&nbsp; (Seiichi
Kirikami, April 26, 2013)</p>

<p>X(5457) is the Hofstadter -1/3 point; see X(359). (Randy Hutson,
August 9, 2014)</p>

X(5458)</a>.</p>
