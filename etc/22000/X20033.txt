<h3 id="X20033">X(20033) = TOUCHPOINT OF THE NINE-POINT CIRCLE AND ALTINTAS CIRCLE</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; b^2 (p - q) + c^2 (p - r) + Sqrt[T] : : where
T = t(a,b,c) + t(b,c,a) + t(c,a,b), t = 2 b^2 c^2 (p - q) (p - r) + a^4 (q - r)^2, and p : q : r are barycentrics for X(20032)
<br>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; SB*SC*(((b^2+3*c^2)*(a^2-b^2)*a^2+(b^4-c^4)*c^2)*S*b*sqrt(tan(B))-((3*b^2+c^2)*(a^2-c^2)*a^2-(b^4-c^4)*b^2)*S*c*sqrt(tan(C))+(b^2-c^2)*(-a^2+b^2+c^2)*(a^2*b*c*sqrt(tan(B)*tan(C))-(b^2+c^2)*S)*a*sqrt(tan(A))) : :
<br>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; SB*SC*(SA*(SB-SC)*(b*sqrt(SB)-c*sqrt(SC))-a*sqrt(SA)*(S^2+SB*SC-2*b*c*sqrt(SB*SC))) : :  &nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;    (César  Lozada, Dec 1, 2022.)
<p>
The circle externally tangent to O<sub>A</sub>, O<sub>B</sub>, O<sub>C</sub> constructed at X(20032), and here named the <b>Altintas circle,</b>, is tangent to the nine-point circle, and X(20033) is the touchpoint. See X(20032)
</p>
