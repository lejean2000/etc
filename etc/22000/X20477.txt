<h3 id="X20477">X(20477) = X(2)X(53)&cap;X(3)X(95)</h3>
Barycentrics &nbsp;&nbsp; a^8-3*(b^2+c^2)*a^6+3*(b^4+c^4)*a^4-(b^4-c^4)*(b^2-c^2)*a^2+2*b^2*c^2*(b^2-c^2)^2 :  :
<b><br>
X(20477) = 3 X(2) - 2 X(53)
</b>
<p>
Let A'B'C' be the tangential triangle.  Let La be the reflection of line B'C' in the perpedicular bisector of BC, and define Lb and Lc cyclically.  Let A" = Lb&cap;Lc, and define B" and C" cyclically.  Triangle A"B"C" is also the tangential triangle of the dual-of-orthic triangle, and X(20477) = X(7)-of-A"B"C". (Randy Hutson, August 29, 2018)
</p>
