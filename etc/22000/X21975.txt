<p>
Let Q be the pedal curve (a limaçon of Pascal) of the circle with center X(7728) and radius |OH|, with respect to X(265). Let A'B'C' be the triangle formed by the tangents at A,B,C to Q.  Then the triangles ABC and A'B'C' are perspective, and their perspector is X(2963). Moreover, X(21975) is the only finite fixed point of the affine transformation that maps a triangle ABC onto A'B'C'.  See <a href="X(21975).png" style="font-weight: bold">X(21975)</a>. (Angel Montesdeoca, September 10, 2019)
</p>
